 Moisture can encourage growth of bacteria or mildew, so ensure each container is dry first. Let the containers air dry in the sun., Group by size and stack in neat piles in a clean, dry place such as a greenhouse. Containers made from breakable should, ideally, be stored individually and not stacked. All clean containers should be stored separately from dirty ones to prevent the transfer of bacteria to the clean pot., Your plant containers are now ready to be filled with soil and plants.
