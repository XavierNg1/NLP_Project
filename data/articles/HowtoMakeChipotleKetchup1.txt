 Place 14 ounces (30 ml) of ketchup, 2-3 chipotle peppers, and 1 tablespoon of adobo sauce in a blender. You can add more peppers if you would like to make a spicier sauce., Turn on the blender at medium speed and mix the ingredients until they create a smooth sauce. Alternatively, you could whisk together the ingredients in a small bowl, using a handheld whisk., Once you have finished blending the ingredients, transfer the sauce into a sealed bowl or jar and refrigerate. You can also serve the sauce immediately. The sauce will last for up to one month in the refrigerator.
