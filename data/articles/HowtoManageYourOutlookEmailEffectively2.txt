 By creating a new email folder, the users can group related messages together in the folders. For example, you can group the messages by subject, project, communication or other categories that are appropriate to your works or your hobbies. The users can also create a folder for all the messages sent from your boss or someone important including the principle information that need saving. To create a new folder in Outlook 2010, on the Folder tab, in the New, click New Folder. To create a new folder in Outlook 2007 or Outlook 2003, on the Menu bar, click on File, choose New then Folder.
