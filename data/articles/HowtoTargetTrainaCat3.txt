 Once your cat has perfected target training at a close range, you can begin moving the target further away. For example, place the target close the cat and then once the cat moves towards the target, pull it further away. The cat should continue to follow the target., Once your cat follows and then touches the target, press the clicker and give your cat a treat., Try placing the target behind the cat, or move it further away from the cat. Continue to train this activity until your cat has mastered the skill of following the target.After a few training sessions, your cat should be able to happily follow the target.
