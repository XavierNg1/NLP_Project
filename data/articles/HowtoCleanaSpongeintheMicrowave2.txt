 If you'd like to eradicate any funky smells from your sponge (along with germs) you may prefer to use the lemon-water disinfectant method. Begin by squeezing the juice from half a lemon into a clean, microwave-safe bowl., Next, fill your clean, microwave safe bowl with fresh water. You want the total amount of liquid in the bowl to be just enough to cover the sponge., Rinse your sponge in warm water and ensure that any food scraps are removed. Then place your sponge in the bowl of lemon-water. Wait for a at least 10 minutes, allowing the sponge to absorb the liquid, prior to microwaving.
