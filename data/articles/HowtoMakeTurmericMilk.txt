 Get the coconut milk, turmeric, black pepper, cinnamon, agave, and coconut oil in your work area. Using all natural organic ingredients is best however, non organic products will work just fine.;
, Add coconut milk, turmeric, black pepper, cinnamon, agave and coconut oil into a good quality blender. Measure the ingredients properly first before adding them into the blender.
 After everything is added into the blender properly blend on high for 30 seconds or until everything is well combine.
 In a large saucepan transfer the mixture from the blender into a clean saucepan.
 Turn on the stove to medium heat then boil the mixture for 10 minutes or until it's nice and warm. Avoid boiling for too long as it will burn it's also important to watch it too.
 Remove the saucepan from the stove then pour it into a large mug there may be some leftovers.
 Sprinkle even more cinnamon on top for an extra kick.
