, Mix with coriander leaves, gram flour, water, turmeric, salt, asafoetida, sesame seeds and carom seeds., Cook on low, stirring it continuously until the mixture dries up., Cover the pan with a lid and cook on low for the next 4-5 min., Spread the mixture across the dish by pressing it with your hands or a spoon gently from all sides., Then cut it into small square or diamond shapes., Keep going until they are brown and crisp.

If you are shallow frying, then heat a little oil in pan and fry the pieces along with sesame seeds, until crisp and brown.
, Offer some tomato ketchup on the side.

You can also serve it for snack with date and tamarind chutney or green chutney.
