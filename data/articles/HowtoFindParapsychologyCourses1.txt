 Parapsychology is still a course of study in its infancy. Very few universities offer degree programs in parapsychology. Currently, there are no parapsychology accredited degree programs in the United States, although many universities offer programs in consciousness studies; most of these programs include a comprehensive study of paranormal psychology.;
