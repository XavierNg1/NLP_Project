 When you run into a friend, you may say hisashiburi. This phrase means “nice to see you again. It can also mean “long time no see.”It is pronounced ‘’hee-sah-shee-boo-ree’’., When you greet an acquaintance, you may say mata o ai shimashitane. Translated, this means “I see you again.” It also means, “we meet again.” It is pronounced ma-ta oh aye she-MOSH-teh ne.
 When you are introduced to someone new for the first time, you may say hajimemashite. This means “nice to meet you.” It is pronounced hah-jee-meh-MOSH-teh.
