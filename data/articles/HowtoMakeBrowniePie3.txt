 Line the pie dish (9 inch/23cm) if needed. Place the pie case into the pie dish in readiness., Add butter and cocoa to the saucepan., Stir occasionally to help mix them together., Allow to cool to room temperature., This warms the eggs gradually and stops them turning into scrambled eggs. Add the remaining chocolate mixture and stir through, Stir to mix well.

Nuts are optional but a nice addition for flavor and texture.
 Place in the preheated oven and bake for 40 to 50 minutes.

To check for readiness, lightly tap the center to see if it has hardened.
, Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.

Best eaten on the day of baking.
