 In a large bowl pour the softened butter, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and sugar. Use a spatula or mixer to combine them thoroughly.
 Then add the flour, only adding one tablespoon at a time, while mixing properly. Combine the ingredients until there are no flour streaks and the ingredients start to form a dough., Use the rubber spatula to mix well., Eat the cookie dough right out of the bowl or add it on your favorite desserts, such as ice cream, cake, and/or brownies.
