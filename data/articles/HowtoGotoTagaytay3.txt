 From Manila, the simplest way to get to Tagaytay by car is the South Luzon Expressway (formerly known as the South Superhighway). The SLE usually has the least traffic getting out of Manila., Get off the SLE at the Santa Rosa exit. You'll need to stop to pay a toll.In an ordinary car or jeep, the toll should be less than 60 pesos. Larger vehicles will pay a higher toll.
 After paying the toll, turn right. Then continue along the road until you reach the Tagaytay City marketplace.The marketplace is the entrance to Tagaytay. It should take about 20 minutes to get there from the Santa Rosa exit.
