 Never underestimate word-of-mouth recommendations. Do you have a friend, or know somebody or rides locally? Ask them where they go. Other places to get recommendations are:


Horse-savvy relatives or friends
Your local equine veterinarian
Employees at the tack/riding supply shop or at your local feed store
Local tack shops.
