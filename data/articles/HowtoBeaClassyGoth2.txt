 Grow a love of dark, brooding classical music. Because gothic culture draws so much from Romanticism, sample the Romantic composers: try Beethoven, Chopin, Tchaikovsky, Mendelssohn, Wagner, and Dvorak.Don't forget to study female Romantic composers, like Emily Mayer, Clara Schumann, and Florence Price.If you don't like Romantic classical music, classy goths can also listen to Gregorian chant, opera, or Baroque music.
