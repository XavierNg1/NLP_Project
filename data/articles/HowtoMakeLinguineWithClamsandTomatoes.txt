 Add the linguine to a pan of salted boiling water and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or according to your taste.;
, Add the garlic and chilli and season with salt and pepper. Cook gently for 1 minute, then pour in the clams giving the pan a shake to mix everything together.
 Using the pasta tongs, stir the ingredients. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for 3 minutes or until the clams have opened.
 When the pasta is cooked, drain through the colander.
 Add the drained linguine to the clam sauce and mix together. Add the chopped parsley, mix well, then remove from the heat.
 Split the meal between four bowls.
