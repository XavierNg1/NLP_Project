 Insert the outlet hose into the water tower at the back. Loosely install the eight screws to secure the cover to the pump. Tighten every second screw to prevent the cover warping and follow the same procedure with the next set of screws.



Tip: Take care not to drop any of the screws at this point as they will go down the drain and you’ll have to uninstall everything to get to these screws.
