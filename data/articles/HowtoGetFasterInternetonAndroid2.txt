 Open Google Play and search for the app there. Tap “Install” inside its app information page to download it onto your device., Locate the app in your app drawer or home screen, and tap on it., Once the app loads, you will see a green button with the word “Start” in the middle. Tap on it, and the app will begin tweaking your Internet settings. You will see a progress circle below the “Start” button, and once the tweaking is done, the button will change to “Stop” and the word “Activated” will appear at the bottom of the screen.

The boosting of your Internet speed is automatic after tapping “Start.” There is no need to make any configuration changes.
To stop boosting your Internet speed, just tap on “Stop.”
