 An example of standard sized round tier cakes in 14 inches (35.6 cm), 10 inches (25.4 cm), and 6 inches (15.2 cm). Frost and cover with fondant (if desired). Now you are ready to stack.;
, You can purchase dowels from any hardware store, or a cake supply shop. Dowels are not necessarily required, but it gives the cake extra support.
 The dowels will be inserted into the cake. Cut about 4 dowels per cake. You may use 6 for the larger tiers.
 Use another dowel to push them into the cake. The dowels should touch the cake board and be flush with the top of the cake.
 Place the next size tier in the center of the bottom tier. Aligning it as needed. Repeat until all of the cakes are stacked.
