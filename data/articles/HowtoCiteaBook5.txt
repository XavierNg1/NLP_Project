 Generally speaking, the citation should include the same elements as the citation for a print book: author, date and title. However, ebooks often don’t have page numbers, so the citation doesn’t require listing them. In addition to the basic reference information, ebook citations should include the source (URL or DOI):


Anderson, R. (2010). The Love of Money . Retrieved from http://www.xxxx. Refer to the latest edition of the style guide you are using to cite your references for electronic sources. This type of citation is still undergoing multiple revisions by all style guides.
