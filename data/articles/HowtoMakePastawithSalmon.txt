 Cut the salmon into small strips.;
, Put the fresh pasta in, then cook for five minutes.
 Fry the chopped onion without browning. Then pour the cream in; when the mixture is hot, add the smoked salmon and the chopped herbs. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
 Add the paste first. Spread the hot, creamy fish mixture over the pasta. You can also add some aromatic herbs for decoration.
 Enjoy your meal!
