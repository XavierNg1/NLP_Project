 Cut a hole in the bag the frozen corn is packaged in and begin to pour the corn out into a microwave safe container or bowl., Add any seasoning you would like to your corn before placing it in the microwave. Try using at least salt and pepper., Microwaves vary in strength and consistency, some heat items quickly while others take much longer. Instead of trying to microwave the frozen corn all at once use a handful of shorter times and stir the corn in between.The overall microwave time of corn should be around 2-3 minutes on a high setting.
 Continue heating and stirring the frozen corn until it has been heated to your desired temperature. Once heated properly, remove from the microwave and enjoy!
