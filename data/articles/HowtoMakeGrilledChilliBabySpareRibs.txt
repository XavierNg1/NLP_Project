 Be sure to start a charcoal barbecue about 30 minutes or a gas barbecue about 20 minutes before planning to cook the ribs.;
, Combine the water, soy sauce, honey, sugar, sambal olek, lemon grass, coriander, ketchup, and shallots in a saucepan.
, Leave it to simmer with the lid partially on the top.
 Use a strainer with the marinade and separate the solids from the liquids. Reduce the liquid for a few minutes.
 Use the reduced marinate and glaze the ribs. Grill for about 2 to 3 minutes (per side).
 Place the ribs on a plate and garnish with a sprig of coriander.
