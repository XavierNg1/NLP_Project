 Open a new web browser tab or window, and go to the Cubby website.;
, Click on the “Log In” button located on the top right corner of the homepage, and you will be brought to the login page. Enter your email address and password for Cubby, then click on the “Log in” button below it.
 Once logged in, you will be brought to your main folder with all your cubbies.
 Hover over a cubby you want to share, and a menu button will appear on the far right side for the row item. Click on it, and a context menu will appear.
 Click on “Share” from the context menu, and a small window will open. Enter the email addresses of the Cubby users you want to invite to collaborate with this cubby. Click the “Invite” button to send out the invites.


You can only share cubbies for collaboration with other Cubby users. All the folders and files under the shared cubbies can be accessed and edited by all those invited. They can also share the cubby with others and create a link to it.
