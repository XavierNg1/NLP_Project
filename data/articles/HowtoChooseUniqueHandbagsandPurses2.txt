 Monogrammed bags and purses never really go out of style, and this season we’ll see a huge range of colors, shapes sizes and styles. A strong upcoming trend is leather sling hipster handbags with elegantly embossed initials or a name. Soft leather is a beautiful fabric for handbags because it’s durable, always very stylish, and it goes with everything. Sling handbags are very comfortable to carry, and perfect either for business or as a casual accessory.
