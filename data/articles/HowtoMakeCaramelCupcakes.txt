 Line muffin pans with 16 to 18 baking cups. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl.;
, Add sugar, 1/2 cup at a time, and beat until mixture is fluffy and has lightened in color, about 2 minutes.
 Add flour mixture to butter mixture in thirds, alternating with 2 additions of buttermilk (1/4 cup each).
 Fill cups to 3/4 of an inch from top. Bake until a toothpick comes out clean, 20 to 24 minutes. Cool before icing.
