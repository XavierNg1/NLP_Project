 Grease bottom and sides of the square pan with shortening.
, In a medium bowl, mix 1 cup flour, the butter and 3 tablespoons powdered sugar with spoon until flour is moistened. Press in pan. Bake 10 minutes., In a medium bowl, beat eggs with wire whisk until blended. Stir in pineapple and all remaining bar ingredients. Spread over partially baked crust., Cool completely, about 1 hour., In small bowl, mix all pineapple glaze ingredients until smooth and thin enough to drizzle. Drizzle glaze over bars.
