 Place your mortar and pestle under warm running water. Rinse them until a majority of the food debris is removed., Use a new (unused) scrub brush. Scrub your mortar and pestle to remove food debris that has set into them. Scrub until all food debris is removed.Keep this scrub brush separate from the scrub brushes you use to clean dishes. Only use it to clean your mortar and pestle.
 Once the food debris is removed, rinse your mortar and pestle again under warm, running water. Make sure to rinse it thoroughly until all debris is removed. Set your mortar and pestle on a drying rack to air dry.
