 Place 1/2 pint of hulled and quartered strawberries and 2 tbsp. of sugar into a food processor. Pulse the mixture until it's smooth and the strawberries are pureed with the sugar., Place the pureed strawberries, 4 oz. of pineapple juice, 3/4 cup of mango juice, 3 oz. of white rum, and 2 oz. of triple sec in a large pitcher. Stir the ingredients together to incorporate them., Cool the mixture for at least an hour., Place this tasty strawberry cocktail in a chilled martini glass and garnish it with a sprig of mint.
