 Hold your arms out in a straight horizontal line. Rotate them in small circles, or for a harder exercise, make the circles larger., Have each arm form a ninety degree angle, facing upwards. Then, flip them upside down and straighten them out. Repeat, making the motions as quick as possible., Hold your arms out in a straight horizontal line and jerk them up very slightly, then back down. Repeat., Hold your arms out in a straight horizontal line. Pull them in so they form a upside down triangle, making sure your biceps are slightly behind your back. Repeat., Place your arms in a position where when put together, they make a long triangle. Push them out so they are straight in front of you and parallel. Repeat., Put your hands and arms in a straight, vertical position so they are pressed against one another and shoulder level. Raise them up, then lower to shoulder level. Repeat.
