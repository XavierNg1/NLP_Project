 Grease two 23cm/9in round cake tins (pans) and line with baking parchment. Grease the paper. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.;
, Beat in the orange rind and juice, then the oil.
 Divide the cake mixture between the tins.
 Leave to stand for 15 minutes, then run a knife around the inside of the cakes and transfer them to racks to cool completely.
