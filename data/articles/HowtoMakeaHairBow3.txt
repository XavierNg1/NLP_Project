 Fold the smaller fabric piece in half lengthwise so that the wrong side of the fabric is facing out. Use sewing pins to hold the raw edges together. Use a sewing machine or work by hand to sew a zigzag or straight stitch along the raw edges. Remove the pins and turn the fabric right-side out. You now have a small fabric tube.
 Use a sewing machine or work by hand to sew the short ends together with a zigzag or straight stitch. Reverse the fabric so that the new seam is facing inward. You now have a small fabric loop.
