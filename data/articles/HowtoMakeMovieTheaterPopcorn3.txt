 You just need a basic brown paper lunch bag that you can get from most grocery stores., Pour in 1/4 a cup of popcorn. Close the bag by folding down the top a few times., This process can take 2 to 4 minutes, depending on your microwave. Listen for the popcorn to slow down to a couple of seconds between each pop. When it does, stop the microwave., Open the bag, being careful of the steam. Pour the popcorn in a bowl., Pour the 2 to 3 tablespoons of clarified butter on the popcorn and stir. Add the salt, and stir again. The butter will help the salt stick to the popcorn.
