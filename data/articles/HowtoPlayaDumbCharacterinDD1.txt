 The three basic party roles are expert, spellcaster, and warrior. Experts (such as bards and rogues) are good at sneaking, solving problems, and knowing useful knowledge. Spellcasters (such as clerics and wizards) cast spells (obviously), but they might also be party healers, long-range attackers, etc. Warriors are good at one thing: fighting. Knowing this, it should be pretty easy to classify your character.
