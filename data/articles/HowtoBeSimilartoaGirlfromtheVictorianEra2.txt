 The Victorian period lasted from June 1837 to January 1901 (but the style didn't end until 1912), a time in the late 1830s and early 1900s, taking over about two thirds of the nineteenth century. Over the 63 years of the era, the fashion (as well as many other things) had changed drastically. The Victorian Era's name originated from Queen Victoria's reign.;
