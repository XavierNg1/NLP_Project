 Greenscaping is a controversial process. Some growers recommend it to speed the drying process and decrease the chance of dark mold staining. Other growers say greenscaping, or doing anything to the surface of the gourd before it is fully dry, increases the risk of damage and infection., Only dry them for a few weeks (they will only become slightly dried.)

, Doing this will reveal lighter layer below.
 Place the gourds in a warm, well-ventilated, and light area. Turn them every 2 to 3 days if you are drying them on a flat surface.


Know that if the gourds dry too quickly they will most likely wrinkle.
