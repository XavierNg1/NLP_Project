 Choose a pair of pants that fit you perfectly; they aren't too long or too short, and are just tight enough to be comfortable. You will be measuring the inseam on the pants, so make sure they truly fit you. Fold them in half vertically, so that you can only see a single leg.;
, Lay them out on an ironing board or the floor. Smooth them out so there aren't any wrinkles or bumps, and the fold is evenly lined up.
