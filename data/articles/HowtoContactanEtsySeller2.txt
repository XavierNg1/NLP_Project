 Right under the item title and next to the price should be a button labeled “Ask a Question.” Click on it, and a message draft should automatically pop up., A basic title and the listing URL will already be included in the message, but you can add your own questions and comments. Press “Send.”, The seller will be notified the next time they log into Etsy. If they have set email notifications on, they will also immediately be emailed about your new message.
