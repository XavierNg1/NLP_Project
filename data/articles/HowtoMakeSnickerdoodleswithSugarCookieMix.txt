For the best results and easier stirring, sift a small amount at a time in between stirring.
 Mix the 1/4 cup (50 grams) sugar and 1 teaspoon cinnamon together in a small bowl.
 Use a cookie scoop to form 1-inch (2.54 cm) balls.
 Place them on a parchment paper lined baking sheet, about two inches apart from each other.
, Leave them on the cookie sheet for a few minutes. This is to allow the cookies to actually "set" while still piping hot.
 Sprinkle the mixture before the cookies completely cool down.
 Set aside for about 15 to 20 minutes.
