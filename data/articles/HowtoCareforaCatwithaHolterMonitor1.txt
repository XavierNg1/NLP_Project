 After you equip your cat with the Holter monitor, allow it to go about its regular activities. Do not make it run, exercise, or eat more than it usually would, or engage in any other unusual activity., Do not take your cat on a long car trip or to a friend’s house. In order to gather the most accurate data, your cat should remain at home for the duration of the Holter monitor’s operation., When your cat starts wearing the Holter monitor, begin an activity log. Include what the cat is doing and the time. This will help the vet match the Holter monitor data to a particular activity as recorded in your activity log., After 24-48 hours, the Holter monitor will have collected enough data for the veterinarian to analyze. Bring your cat and your activity log to your vet. Your vet will remove the Holter monitor and analyze the data.
