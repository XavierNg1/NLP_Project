 Nodular melanomas, which make up about 20% of cases, don’t follow the ABCDE rule. Luckily, they do have common features that help you identify them, such as being elevated. Check for moles that feel like a bump. Compared to other moles, they will feel raised., Benign moles usually feel like the rest of your skin, so a mole that is hard is questionable. A nodular melanoma will be firm to the touch.Use your finger to check the firmness of your moles. Check with your doctor if a mole feels hard.
 Any mole growth is suspicious, even if it’s your only symptom. If you have rapid mole growth, then you need to make an appointment with the doctor. Nodular melanomas grow quickly, so they need to be addressed as soon as possible.
