 Insert your non-stick lining bag or spray the insides with a light and thin layer of non-stick cooking spray.;
, Combine the potatoes, onion, celery, chicken broth, and celery seeds into the slow cooker.
 Place the slow cooker on high for 3 to 4 hours.
 Mix the cornstarch and milk together in a small bowl.
 Drizzle the mixture slowly around, in the last 20 minutes of cooking. Fold in the paste into the mixture and re-cover the slow cooker.
 Sprinkle salt and pepper, to taste, and serve in individual bowls.
