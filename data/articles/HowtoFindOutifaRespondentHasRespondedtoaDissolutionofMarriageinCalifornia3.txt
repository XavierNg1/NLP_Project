 In some dissolution cases, the petition is served to your attorney on your behalf. If this happens, your attorney will most likely let you know at his/her earliest convenience. But you should also feel free to contact your attorney’s office and ask., In some dissolution cases, the petitioner’s and respondent’s attorneys communicate frequently about the case.
