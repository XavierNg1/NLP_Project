 Use a one and a half to two quart slow cooker. Add all your ingredients and mix them together until you have an even mixture. The order you add the ingredients is not important., Turn your slow cooker to the low setting. Allow your mixture to cook on low for seven to eight hours before serving., Mix in a small amount of salt and pepper. Add enough that the flavors in your mixture are distinct. Add small amounts of salt and pepper at a time, tasting as you go, until you reach your desired taste.
