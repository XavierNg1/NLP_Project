 All symbols can be regarded as English abbreviations, except for ten that have Latin names and abbreviations and one (Wolfram) whose name can be considered of German origin. Excluding Antimony and Tungsten, these are all important and frequently used elements.







Knowing the Latin names as well enables you to decipher most Latin names of inorganic chemicals. In most Romance languages (French, Italian, Spanish, etc.), the present day word is derived from the Latin word.
