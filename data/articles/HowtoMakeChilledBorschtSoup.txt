 In a large saucepan, combine beets, water, stock, onion, garlic and bay leaf. Simmer, covered until beets are very tender, about 45 minutes to an hour. Remove beets to cool, then peel and coarsely shred into a bowl.
 Return to the saucepan, bring it back to a simmer, then add the lemon juice, brown sugar and salt., Immediately refrigerate until very cold.,
