 Combine 1 cup of packed fresh basil leaves, 1/2 cup of fresh parsley, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, 1/4 cup of pine nuts, 1 large quartered garlic clove, and 1/4 tsp. of salt in a food processor., Stop frequently to scrape the sides so all of the ingredients are processed.
, Continue to blend the ingredients until they're thick.
 Serve this pesto pasta sauce with fresh fettuccine, chicken, shrimp, or broccoli.
