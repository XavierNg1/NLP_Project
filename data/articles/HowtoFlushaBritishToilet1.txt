 Though many countries have “squat style” toilets, British toilets are almost always designed for sitting. In this way, they are usually similar to other Western style toilets. , Use a trash can if it’s available. This is usually a sign that the toilet cannot accommodate much paper. If there is no trash can, throw the paper in the toilet. Be careful not to put in too much at a time. , Be sure that you push the lever as far down as it will go, but don’t hold it there for more than a second. Release it quickly after you feel it stop.
 If a single flush didn’t work, wait until the toilet refills itself, and try again. This time, try holding the lever at the bottom for a couple of seconds. Some older toilets require a bit longer for the water to fill up.
