 Place the pan over low heat and stir through constantly until the sugars dissolve into the syrup completely.;
, Mix through while the mixture continues to cook over low heat.
 Preheat the oven to 170ºC. Line the baking tray with baking paper.
 Pour over the hot mixture and mix together well. Leave for a few minutes to cool a bit.
 Gradually add in the sifted flour while stirring.
 Arrange on the baking tray. Flatten each ball a little with the ball of your hand.
 Bake for 10–12 minutes. Remove when ready and leave to cool on a wire cooling rack.
 Make the icing by adding 1 tablespoon of warm water to the icing sugar. Add small amounts to each cookie and leave to set.
