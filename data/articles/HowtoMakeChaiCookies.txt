 If fan force, preheat to 140ºC only. Prepare the baking trays (sheets) by lining with baking paper.;
, Use an electric mixer if wished.
 Mix together to form a soft dough.


Remove the cookie dough onto a clean work surface and knead it for 5 minutes.
Once smooth, stop kneading.
 Place the dough on it and another sheet over the top. Roll the dough out over the top sheet of paper. Aim for about a 1 centimeter (0.4 in) thickness of dough.
 Use the end of a glass or a circle cookie cutter.
 Brush with the milk to form a glaze when cooking. Sprinkle each cookie with a little white sugar to complete.
 Bake for 15 minutes or until a golden colour.
 Remove from the trays onto a wire cooling rack to cool completely.
 Once cooled, the cookies are ready to serve with a cup of chai. If storing, place in an airtight container and eat within a few days of baking.
