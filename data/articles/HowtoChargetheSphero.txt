 The charger cradle for your Sphero is round, bright blue, and is shaped like a cup to fit your Sphero ball., The Sphero’s heavy side is visible through the ball, and can be found by placing the Sphero on a hard surface and allowing the heavy part to settle at the bottom., The Sphero is now charging.
 The charger’s light will stop blinking and become solid blue when your Sphero is fully charged.
 Your Sphero will now be able to function at full speed for about 60 minutes before its needs to be recharged.
