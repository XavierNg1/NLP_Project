 Blend and cream well on a medium setting, mixing until the butter is fully whipped, taking about 3-5 minutes., Blend once more on high speed for about 5 minutes until fully combined and creamy.,, Mix well using a spoon until the frosting color shows and is vibrant.

Use a clean spoon every time you mix a new frosting color to avoid a grayish color into the frosting from mixing multiple colors.
