 This wrap is made for babies 3-5 months that can fully stretch their legs. Take the shoulder piece that is closest to your body, and slide one of the baby’s legs through the hole. Hold the baby up to your opposite shoulder as you do this., Slide the fabric up over the baby’s shoulder and bottom. Spread it out until it reaches the hollow of your baby’s knee. Then, pull your baby’s second leg through the section with the logo., Spread the fabric until it covers the baby’s shoulders and back. Make sure that the legs are still free when you do this. You can choose to leave the head free, or tuck the head into one of the shoulder sections.
