Bring both hampers to the washing machine when it’s time to wash a load and mix socks into the load one pair at a time., Get into the habit of putting in a smaller load for nothing besides your socks.By isolating all of your socks while they’re being washed, you’ll limit the chances they have to get jumbled up with other clothing and disappear.
