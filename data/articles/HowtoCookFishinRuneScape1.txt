 Fires tend to burn food more frequently, but gives you more experience than a range. If you have completed the Cook's Assistant quest, you are allowed to use the range in Lumbridge castle, which also gives you a huge advantage of being able to cook fish lower than your regular level, as well as the bank distance.
 High level fish, such as lobsters and swordfish, are the most popular food sources to heal life points with in one click.
