 Take note that in some languages you will say different versions of I love you based on the gender of the person to whom you are speaking. In other cases, there is a distinction between saying I love you to a lover, and I love you to a friend. There is also different intensities of love; I love you's that are stronger and weaker. For example, in Spanish you say “te quiero” to a friend and you reserve “te amo” for a lover. For many, when you say te amo you are taking a serious step in your relationship.
