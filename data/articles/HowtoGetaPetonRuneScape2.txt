 Every boss in Runescape now has a 1/5000 chance to drop an item that lets you summon a smaller version of it. However, the more of that boss you kill, the higher that odd becomes, maxing out at a 5% chance to get any given boss.
 To summon a boss pet, you must have 99 summoning. Once you have that, left click the "Summon" option to have your new pet following you
