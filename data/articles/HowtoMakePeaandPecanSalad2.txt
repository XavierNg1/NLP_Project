 Cook in the oven at 400ºF/200ºC until crisp.


Once cooked, crumble into smaller pieces.
 Pour the mayonnaise, vinegar and Worcestershire sauce into the salad bowl. Add the sugar and pepper to taste. Mix to combine well.
 Add the crumbled bacon. Toss through to cover.
 Cover to prevent fridge odors transferring.
 Add salad servers and you're good to go.
