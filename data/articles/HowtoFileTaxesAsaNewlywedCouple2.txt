 The name on your tax return should match what is on record in your Social Security Administration file. If necessary, you should file Form SS-5 with the Social Security Administration (SSA). You can get this form by visiting the SSA website or calling 800-772-1213.You can also get a copy of the form from your local SSA office.
 Notify the IRS of your change of address using Form 8822, Change of Address. You should also tell the Post Office of your new address so that mail will be forwarded., Your employer also needs to know your new name and any new address so that you can receive your W-2, Wage and Tax statement at the end of the year.Contact them as soon as possible with your updated information so that it can be put on file.
