 Schedule an appointment to get your Aadhar card at http://appointments.uidai.gov.in/. This site will help you locate the nearest Aadhar card center., Choose your state, district, and locality from each of the drop-down lists. Fill in the required details, such as name, street address, and phone number. Don’t leave any of the fields blank to avoid any delays in securing an appointment., Select your nearest enrollment center from the list. Then, choose the date and time most convenient for you. After that, click the button labeled “Fix Appointment.”, You’ll see your receipt after setting your appointment. It will include the location of your enrollment center, the date and time of your appointment, and the name of a staff member to report to.
