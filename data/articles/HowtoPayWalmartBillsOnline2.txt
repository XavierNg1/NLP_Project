 If you want to use MyCheckFree to pay a Wal-Mart bill online, you'll need to create an account. The process is straightforward, but may take a bit longer than simply creating a Wal-Mart account. More information is required for security purposes.


You will need to provide your name, address, phone number, and e-mail address.You will also need your account number from your Walmart account., Once you've created your account, you can pay your Wal-Mart bill online fairly easily. You can pay through checking account, money-market account, or any major credit cards accepted at Walmart., Much like paying through Wal-Mart's website, it will take 2 days for your bill to be processed. The money may not be removed from your account until then, so be conscious of how much you're spending to avoid bouncing a check or payment.
