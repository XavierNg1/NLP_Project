 The flowers should be in good condition.

If picking your own, cut the stalks from the base of the plant.
 Pull apart the florets., Mix together., Alternatively, add to kitchen plastic wrap and roll into a sausage shape. Twist the ends of the wrap closed., Allow to set for a few hours.

The flavor develops best over a few hours.
 If you've used the sausage wrap approach, cut the butter into small serving discs by slicing. They look especially lovely like this, as the butter is shot through with purple lines and petal shapes., The butter can be used on sandwiches or for crusty rolls in place of butter. Or, use it to make dishes taste better, such as grilled fish or meat, tucked into baked or hasselback potatoes, melted over steamed vegetables, added to sauces, etc.
