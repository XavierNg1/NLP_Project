 When the flowers open, harvest the heartsease. Gently remove the petals. Measure out two cups of them., In a blender, mix the heartsease petals and one cup of raw honey. Cut a lemon in half and squeeze the juice from both parts into the blender. Blend the ingredients together until the honey is smooth., Transfer the honey into a jar using a spatula. Seal the jar tightly. To keep the honey for as long as possible, store it in the freezer and thaw it out for future use.
