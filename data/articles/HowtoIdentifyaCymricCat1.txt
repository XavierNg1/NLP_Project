 A Cymric's head is round in shape, resembling a circle. This is in contrast to other breeds. Other breeds are more likely to have a round head that narrows near the chin and mouth, similar to an upside down triangle., Its widely spaced ears stand erect on top of its head. In terms of shape, its ears are wide at the base and gradually taper to a rounded tip.Its ears may have wisps or tufts of hair., Its eyes are also positioned at a slight downward position toward its nose. In other words, the inner corners of its eyes are slightly lower than the outer corners.A Cymric may have hazel, green, amber, copper, gold, yellow or orange eyes.
