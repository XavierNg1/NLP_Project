 When the water comes to a full rolling boil, add the pasta and 2 tablespoons of salt. Stir.;
, Place strips of bacon in a pre-heated pan and allow them to gently cook. Grate the garlic and sprinkle it over the bacon. Allow it to slowly flavour the meat.
 Dice the tomatoes, onions, sweet peppers and hot pepper. Cut the carrots into thin circles, and then halve them. Sprinkle these with a little salt and black pepper.
 Add 2 1/2 tablespoons of spaghetti sauce to a bowl. Stir in 2 tablespoons each of ketchup and soy sauce. Sprinkle in 1/2 tablespoon of salt, black pepper and dried parsley.
 When the bacon pieces have turned brown, remove them and place on tissue paper to dry off excess fat. Do not discard the extra fat in the pan!

, Cook for 3 minutes, then add the sauce mixture and 2 cups of water. Stir the mixture and bring it to a simmer.
 Allow to cook for 3 more minutes.
