 Prepare a baking tray by lining with baking paper or a silicone sheet., Rub together until well combined., Mix through. Then add the mixed fruit and mix in as well., Mix well. If the mixture seems too doughy or stiff at this stage, you can add a little milk or water to loosen it a bit., These will spread, so leave room between each one added. To finish off, you can sprinkle a little sugar on top of each one, but this is optional., Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until the appear golden in colour., Transfer to a wire cooling rack and allow to cool. These are always best eaten on the same day they are baked. If not eaten, keep in an airtight container but be warned, they lose texture and flavour quickly after baking day.
