, Heat a saucepan filled about 1/3 full with water. Bring the water to a simmer and place a bowl in the saucepan. Add the chocolate and butter in the bowl and stir & melt both ingredients. Remove the bowl from the heat.
 Measure and pour the vanilla essence to the bowl. Stir again.
 Fold and mix everything together. Cover the bowl with cling film and place it in the refrigerator for about an hour and a half.
, Place the finished dough balls onto the baking tray.
 When they come out of the oven, sprinkle some more icing sugar on them. Let them cool on a cooling rack.
