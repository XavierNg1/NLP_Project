Allow to rise for 1 hour.
 Push your thumb through the center to form a hole.


Stretch and shape dough to form an even ring. Place on floured surface. Cover and let rest for 10 - 15 minutes.



Drop 2 bagels at a time and leave for 45 seconds, turn over and cook for another 45 seconds.






When finished, take them out the bowl and drain with a paper towel.



Put them in separated, on baking sheets, coated with cooking spray. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 - 20 minutes or until golden brown.
