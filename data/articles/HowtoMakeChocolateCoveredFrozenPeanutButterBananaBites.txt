 Cover with a slice or wax or parchment paper to stop the banana slices from sticking.;
, Arrange the banana slices on the pan or baking sheet. You'll need an even amount to work with.
 Use a butter knife to add the peanut butter.
 Form little sandwiches from the pieces. These now form the basis of the bites.
 Place the whole pan or sheet in the freezer. Allow to freeze for an hour.
 Melt the chocolate in a double boiler or in a microwave-proof bowl. Remove from the heat when melted.
 Move around to ensure that each banana sandwich is entirely coated in chocolate.
 Allow to freeze for at least 3 hours, or overnight.
 Enjoy as a tasty and healthy snack.
