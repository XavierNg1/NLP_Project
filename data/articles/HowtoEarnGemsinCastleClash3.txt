 Each day, you will receive a hero, cards, exp books, slime essences, etc. You will also receive some honer badges and some gems. Usually you will get 20 gems, but sometimes, it can be 50, 100, or even 200 gems as a reward. A legendary hero usually comes with this reward.
 There will also be another reward. Claim the 5 gems that you will get with your daily reward every day.
