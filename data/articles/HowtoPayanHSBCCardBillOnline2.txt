 If you want to pay an HSBC card bill online, first log in to your account. Use the login ID and password you created when you registered for an online banking account., There will be a small menu to the left of the screen. Look for the tab that says "Pay Bills" and select that tab., If you're new to HSBC's online banking system, you'll have to make a list of payees. These are companies and people you usually pay. If you're paying your HSBC credit card, that may automatically be in your online banking system. If not, simply add a payee for your card and enter your credit card information., Select the proper payee. Enter the amount you want to pay. Then hit "submit." It may take a few days for your payment to show up on your statement.
