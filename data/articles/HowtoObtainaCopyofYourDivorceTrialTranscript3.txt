 If your lawyer requested a copy of the transcript, then you can call and request a copy from him or her.This will be the easiest way to get a copy.


You might have hired a new attorney for an appeal. He or she can get the transcript from your old lawyer.
 Your lawyer can charge you for photocopying based upon your fee agreement.It is quite common for lawyers to charge for photocopies. Take out your fee agreement and check how much it will cost.
