 Peel the white radish and cut it into small pieces. Slice the tomatoes, onion, garlic, dried chilies, and green chilies.;
, Cook over medium heat. If the oil seems hot, add the turmeric powder first. Then add the sliced garlic, onion, and chili. Cook until the onion turns a golden color.
 Cover it and cook until the tomato is cooked well.
 At this stage, add the salt and chicken powder as well. Cover it again and cook for another 15 minutes.
 Cover the curry soup and cook for one minute.
 Serve immediately; enjoy this Myanmar radish and tomato soup.
