 To capture the entire screen, press Command + Shift + 3. This will instantly take a shot of your screen -- you will even hear the sound of a camera taking a picture. This will also automatically save the image to a file on your desktop., To capture any rectangular portion of the screen, press Command + Shift + 4. This will turn your mouse into a tool that can select any portion of the screen. Simply drag the mouse around the desired portion and then let go of the keys to take a screenshot.
 Press Control along with the other functions to save the image to the clipboard temporarily instead of to a permanent file. This will allow you to copy and paste the screen image into another application, such as software that allows you to edit the image.
