 If there is a particular post or photo that you want to add to your collections, you can manually add that post. Find the post in your timeline and select it by tapping on it., Tapping and holding the icon will bring up the various collections you've already created. It will also give you the ability to create a new collection from the photo that you selected., You can add your photo to an existing collection that you've saved. Scroll up and down to go through all your various collections. If no collection fits for this particular photo, you can create a brand new collection by pressing the plus sign on the right of the screen., After you've added the post to the collection you want to save it in, hitting the "Done" button will save it. Now you can go back to your bookmarks page and find the post under the collection that you saved it under.
