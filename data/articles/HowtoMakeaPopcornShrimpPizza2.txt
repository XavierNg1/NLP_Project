 Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit or 232 degrees Celsius., Roll the pizza crust using a rolling pin and flatten it out. If desired spread melted butter or oil over it for a buttery crust., Using a spoon, spread the one half cup tomato sauce all over the pizza until covered. Use circular motions to ensure the sauce covers the whole pizza., Lightly sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese all over your pizza. Sprinkle it all around so each slice can have cheese on it., Place a popcorn shrimp in a different area onto the pizza. Do not dunk the whole box of popcorn shrimp on the pizza, each slice should have an equal number of shrimp pieces. Place your pizza on a pizza stone or baking tray to prepare to bake the pizza., Spread the minced garlic over the pizza and squirt a pinch of lemon if desired. Place your pizza on a pizza stone or baking tray to prepare for baking., Place the pizza in the oven and have it bake till the cheese melts and the popcorn shrimp lightly browns, usually taking about ten minutes., Remove the pizza from the oven and place it on a rack to cool for about ten minutes., Cut a slice of pizza using a pizza cutter and lay on a serving plate. Top it off with a few basil leaves. Enjoy!
