 Add 16 oz. Of elbow macaroni, then reduce heat to medium high. Cook 7 to 10 min., stirring occasionally until tender. Pour into colander to drain, then place in serving bowl. Set aside.;
, Add ¼ cup all purpose flour and ½ teaspoon salt. Cook for 3 min., stirring constantly. Sauce should be as thick as paste.
 Add 2 cups milk. Cook for 5 min., stirring constantly until sauce thickens.
 Stir until melted. Pour sauce over elbow noodles, then stir until well mixed. Serves 6 to 8.
