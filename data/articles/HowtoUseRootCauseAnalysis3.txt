When done - write the problem in a box.
 This includes assessing the following:


Identify factors that may be a part of the problem.
Draw out factors as much as possible.
Brainstorm.
Draw a main line ("spine") for the diagram and add the factors to it.
 To do this, try the following.


For each factor considered, add a cause that can be related to the problem.
Add the possible causes to the main line (add "bones").
For each factor identify brainstorm causes and add to the diagram.
 The diagram should be finished. Investigate what causes the problem.
