 Cut out several pieces of 5 centimeter (2.0 in) by 16mm black ribbon rectangle and 5 centimeter (2.0 in) by 8mm white ribbon rectangle. Trim one end of black rectangle; a puff rectangle is done. Flame the edges of the petal go and forth to make them curl up by lighter. Fold the two sides of the petal as the pic shows and secure it with glue.;
, Lay the circle felt as flower base, glue the black folded petal to the base until the flower looks like full; then do the same to the white petal, add black beads as flower heart by sewing.
