 If your hair is dry and unruly, you might benefit from using an oil treatment on your hair before shampooing. Look for pre-shampoo treatments that utilize coconut, Abyssinian, or avocado oil., Applying the butter to your hair section-by-section can help reduce tangles and leave your hair soft and moisturized., Pre-shampoo treatments that incorporate wheat germ can help restore the natural lipid film on color-treated hair. Opt for a treatment specifically formulated for color-treated hair. These products are usually created to not alter dye pigments.
