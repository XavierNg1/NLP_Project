 To make a chocolate sauce for the marshmallow kebabs you must first obtain a source of chocolate such as a chocolate bar, chocolate chips or chocolate melts. Place the chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl, set the microwave setting to lowest heat and microwave for 30 seconds. The chocolate chips must be microwaved for 10 seconds otherwise they will burn.

After the chocolate has cooked, mix it and leave it to cool.;
, Do this when the chocolate sauce has cooled so that it isn't hot. Place the kebabs on a plate., When the chocolate sauce is cooled you may drizzle it onto the marshmallow kebabs or dip the kebabs in the sauce and serve.
