 Prepare the cupcake cases by arranging them across a baking tray or sheet.

If preferred, you can place the cupcake cases inside the holes of a muffin pan.
 Heat and stir until it turns smooth, then remove. Set aside to cool., Add the melted chocolate, butter, milk and vanilla extract., Stir until the ingredients form a smooth batter., Stir until completely combined., Distribute the batter equally across all the cases., Bake for 20-25 minutes. The cupcakes are done when a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean., Allow to cool on the tray or pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire cooling rack to cool completely., Use the frosting of your choice., They can be added randomly, in a pattern or piled up in a heap., Serve when the frosting has firmed.
