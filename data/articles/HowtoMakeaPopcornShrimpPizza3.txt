 Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit or 232 degrees Celsius., Roll the pizza crust using a rolling pin and flatten it out. If desired spread melted butter or oil over it for a buttery crust., Using a spoon, spread the one half cup tomato sauce all over the pizza until covered. Use circular motions to ensure the sauce covers the whole pizza., Add each vegetable on the pizza; the carrots, olives, tomatoes, broccoli, and green beans. If available you can add more of your favorite vegetables on top of your pizza., Place your pizza in the oven and have it bake till the vegetables are properly baked and the crust is golden, usually taking about ten minutes., Remove the pizza from the oven and place it on a rack to cool for about ten minutes., Cut a slice of pizza using a pizza cuter and lay on a serving plate. Sprinkle a little bit of black pepper or Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
