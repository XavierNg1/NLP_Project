 Lay out 3 tortillas. Top the tortillas with the shredded cheese, sliced Roma tomatoes, and pepperonis. Put 3 more tortillas on top of the ingredients, forming a quesadilla.You can alter the ingredients to your liking. For example, you can choose to use beef instead of pepperoni.;
, Get out a 12-inch (30.5 cm) non-stick skillet. Put the skillet over medium heat. Melt 1 tablespoon (14.2 g) of butter in the skillet. Rotate the skillet as the butter melts so that the butter coats the bottom of it., Place one quesadilla in the skillet on top of the butter. Cook it for 6 to 8 minutes. Turn it over once during the 6 to 8 minutes. The quesadilla will be lightly browned when it is complete., Repeat the preparation and cooking with the remaining ingredients. Place the finished quesadillas on a plate while the next one is cooking. You should have 16 servings once all of the ingredients have been used., Cut the quesadillas into square pieces. Or, you can choose to cut the quesadillas into triangles if you prefer. Serve warm with warmed pizza sauce.
