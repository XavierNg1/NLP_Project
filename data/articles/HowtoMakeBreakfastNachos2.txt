 Place your frozen hash browns on a baking sheet. Bake them at 425 degrees Fahrenheit (about 218 degrees Celsius) for 20 to 25 minutes. When done, the hash browns should be golden brown., Remove your hash browns from the oven. After they cool to the touch, cut each patty in half diagonally., Sprinkle both types of cheese evenly over the hash browns. Then, top your hash browns with eggs, bacon, salt, and pepper., Toss your hash browns back into the oven. Bake for an additional 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
