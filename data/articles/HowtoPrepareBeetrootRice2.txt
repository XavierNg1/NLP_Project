 Pour oil and wait till it gets heated. Add the mustard seeds, black gram, Bengal gram and red chillies.;
, Roast till it turns a brownish color.
 Roast and roast till the raw smell of the beetroot goes away and it turns slightly to a brown color.
 Pour 2 cups of water in. Add salt and curry leaves.
 Cook. Turn off the cooker after 1 to 2 whistles.
 You can add washed and cleaned coriander leaves for garnishing.
