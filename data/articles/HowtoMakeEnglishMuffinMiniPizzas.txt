 Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit or 177 degrees Celsius. Preheating the oven ahead of time helps make the baking a lot easier and faster. Cover a baking tray with foil.;
, Cut the English muffins in halves gently with a butter knife. Lay the muffin slices on the tray.
 After placing the muffins on a baking tray, toast them to your preference. Usually they will be lightly crisped and be a light brown color. That takes about five to seven minutes but all ovens vary. Have cool.
 Place the toasted English muffins on the baking tray about 1 1/2 inch (4 centimeters) apart from each other.
 Spread the tomato sauce on each muffin half, lathering it all around with a metal spoon.
 Slowly sprinkle the cheese on each slice all around.
 Set one or two tomato slices on each muffin half.
 Sprinkle basil over each mini pizza if wanted. Feel free to add other toppings such as meat or vegetables.
 Place the tray in the oven for about five to eight minutes or until the cheese melts.

Keep an eye on the pizzas -- they don't take long. They will be finished when the cheese has completely melted.
 Have the pizzas cool for about five minutes. Serve on a serving plate.
