 Add in the flour, butter, yeast, sugar, salt, baking powder, xanthan gum, egg, and water.;
, Mix the ingredients with your hand or with an electric mixture.
 After the dough is kneaded smoothly place the dough into a greased bowl.
, Line the two baking pans with parchment paper or grease them lightly with oil or butter.
 After boiling the water place the dough into a floured surface and divide into twelve even pieces.
 Cross both of the ends of the ropes then fold it over to create the pretzel shape.
 Lay the pretzels with a gap between them so they don't get stuck together in the baking process.
 Gently place the uncooked pretzels dough one at a time into the boiling water for 5 seconds.
 Sprinkle some salt on top of the pretzels for extra flavour. Pop the pretzels into the oven for 18-20 minutes (soft pretzels) 25 minutes (chewy pretzels) or 35 minute for hard, crunchy pretzels.
 Let the cool down for 10 minutes before eating.
