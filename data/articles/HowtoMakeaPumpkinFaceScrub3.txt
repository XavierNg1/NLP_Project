 Add the pumpkin puree and raw honey to a small mixing bowl. Use a fork to thoroughly combine the ingredients. The honey should be evenly distributed throughout the pumpkin., Once you have completely combined the honey and the pumpkin, add the brown sugar and ground cinnamon to the mixture. Combine the ingredients thoroughly with a fork., Use your fingers to gently apply the mixture to your face. Massage the scrub into your skin using small, circular motions. Continue until you have reached your desired level of exfoliation, or around 2 minutes. Store any unused scrub in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
