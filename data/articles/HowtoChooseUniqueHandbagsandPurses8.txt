 Hobo purses can be cool and casual for shopping, or groovy and glamorous to go with evening wear.Casual hobo purses come in suede or leather, and soft colors like pastel pinks, beige, cream and white are very much in vogue. A new look for hobo purses which is on the way in is a small purse in a gold or silver mesh fabric, or even ultra-glamorous sequins and rhinestones. These purses are a new take on the traditional evening purse and make an eye-catching alternative.
