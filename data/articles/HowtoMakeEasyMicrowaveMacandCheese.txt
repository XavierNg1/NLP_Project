 Put the macaroni in a microwave safe mug.;
, Pour some milk or water over the macaroni noodles. Using milk will make it more cheesy and delicious.
 Sprinkle your cheese of choice on top of the macaroni. Add more cheese to make the macaroni even more cheesy.
 Place the mug in the microwave oven and microwave on high for 5 minutes or until the noodles are fully cooked and let it cook.
 Take the mug out of the microwave and allow to cool before eating.
