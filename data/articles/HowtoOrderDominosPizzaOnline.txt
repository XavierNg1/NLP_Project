 Go to dominos.com; on the toolbar at the top of the website, there should be a button that says Order Online. Choose between Delivery or Carryout.
 From pizza, desserts and soft drinks, there is always something great to choose at Domino's! Just click on the thing that you want to order., If you are ordering pizza, you can customize it online. Just click on the toppings you want. When you are finished, click "Add to My Order"., Once you've finished making your order, click "Done with order"., You can either pay online with a card, or you can pay with cash at the door. Remember: a tip is always appreciated., Once you choose your payment option, review your order, and click "Place My Order"., After the order is placed, the Domino's Tracker will show you when the pizza is done. Once it's done, it will either be delivered or you can go pick it up to enjoy it at home!
