 You could choose which mood-feedback you get. If you want to get the happy mood-feedback, put a tick on the "Happy" one. If you want to get the sad mood-feedback, put a tick on the "Sad" one. If you want to get the confused mood-feedback, put a tick on the "Confused" one. If you want to get the happy, sad, and confused feedback, put a tick on them.
 If you want to find a user's feedback, write their name in the "username" one.
 If you want to keep track of the users you replied to their feedback, put a tick in the "My responses only" part, and if you want to see the unanswered feedback, put a tick in the "Show unanswered".
