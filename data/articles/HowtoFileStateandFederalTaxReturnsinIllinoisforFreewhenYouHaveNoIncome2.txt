 Go to http://www.revenue.state.il.us/index.htm and click the button that says "Click here to file your IL-1040" on the bottom right of the page.
 When you start the process and enter your social security number, it will ask for your "IL-PIN". If you know it, enter it, if not, click "Pin Inquiry", and supply the necessary information.
, Just follow the steps, leave everything blank, and at the very bottom will be a button that says Submit or File state return or something like that. It will re-confirm that your income is 0 and just keep clicking "accept."
