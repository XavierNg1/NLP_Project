 Add 3 tablespoons (45 ml) of fresh lemon juice and 3 tablespoons (38 g) of granulated sugar to a small mixing bowl. Use a whisk to blend the two together until the sugar dissolves.You can substitute lime juice for the lemon if you prefer.
 Once the sugar has dissolved, add ¼ cup (59 ml) of olive oil, ¼ cup (59 ml) of vegetable oil, 3 tablespoons (63 g) of honey, and 2 teaspoon (36 g) of poppy seeds to the bowl. Whisk the mixture until all of the ingredients are well blended. Set the bowl aside for the moment.You can substitute canola oil for the vegetable oil if you prefer.
 Add 8 peeled and segmented mandarin oranges, 4 cored and diced apples, 4 peeled and diced kiwis, 4 peeled and diced bananas, and 1 ½ cups (261 g) of pomegranate seeds to a large bowl. Use a wooden spoon or salad tongs to combine the fruit so it’s all well mixed.You can use any type of apples that you like and are available at your local grocery store or farmer's market. However, 2 gala and 2 golden delicious is an ideal combination.
 Once the salad is mixed, add the dressing to the bowl. Toss the salad thoroughly to ensure that all of the fruit is coated., After you’ve tossed the salad with the dressing, spoon it into individual bowls. For the best taste, serve the salad right away.Store any leftover salad in the refrigerator. It should keep for 1 to 2 days.
