 Cut dough into twelve 4x3/4 inch strips. Re-roll scraps as necessary, until all dough has been cut into strips. Repeat process with vanilla dough.
 Place one strip of chocolate dough on sheet of plastic wrap. Brush edge with egg. Repeat with one more chocolate strip and one more vanilla strip to make bottom layer. Brush top with egg.
