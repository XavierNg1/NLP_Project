 Double-click a web browser's icon on your desktop to launch it.
 Once the browser is open, type in www.expensify.com on the address bar at the top of the screen and hit Enter. You will be taken to the website's home screen.
 Type in your email address and password on the fields provided then click “Sign in” to access your account.
, Select the policy you’d like to enable the Mileage and Time Tracking feature.
 This will redirect you to the Mileage and Time Tracking feature.
 Enter the rate per mile and kilometers in the text field provided. These rates will be used to compute your Mileage.


You can also choose whether you’d like to use Miles or Kilometers as your default unit of measurement.
 Put a check mark on the box beside the “Enable Time Tracking” option, and enter the default hourly rate on the text field provided.


You don’t need to click on any buttons to save the information you’ll enter. The details you type in will automatically be used in tracking your mileage and time.
