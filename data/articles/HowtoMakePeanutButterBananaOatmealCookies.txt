 In order to properly cut a 1/4 cup of butter, use the measurements on the wrapper of a block of butter. If there are no measurements on the wrapper, scoop 4 tablespoons (59.1 ml) of butter. If using sticks of butter, use 1/2 a stick of butter. Put the butter in a glass measuring cup.
 Set the microwave for 15 seconds. When the microwave notifies you that the butter is finished microwaving, carefully take the cup of butter out of the microwave with oven mitts., Mix all of the ingredients with a fork.
 Mix all of the ingredients with a fork. If using a hand mixer, now would be the time to use it. Turn on the hand mixer and put it in the batter. Turn the mixer on a low speed and gradually increase the speed while mixing. Mix until there are little to no clumps. Before taking the mixer out of the batter, be sure to turn the speed to 0.
 Take 2 tablespoons (29.6 ml) of batter and mould it into a ball. Next, keep the ball of batter sitting in one hand and use the other hand to perform circular motions on the ball. This will smoothen the ball of batter., Roll the ball of batter into the bowl of oats until the batter is covered with oats.
 Next, use a fork and flatten the ball once vertically. Finally, horizontally flatten the ball again until the ball is flat but still circular.
, Take the cookie sheet, which has all 38 flattened, circular batter on it, and put it into the oven. Let the cookies bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
 Wait until they cool before eating the cookies.
