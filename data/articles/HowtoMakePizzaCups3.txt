 Turn the oven on to 350°F (176°C). While the oven is preheating, lightly mist your muffin pan with cooking spray. You can also wipe a small amount of cooking oil onto the pan if you don’t have a spray., This recipe calls for 2.5 (70.8 g) ounces of diced mushrooms and 20 slices of sliced turkey pepperoni, but you can use any ingredients that you’d like. If you choose to use mushrooms and turkey pepperoni, first cook the mushrooms in a small skillet for a few minutes until wilted/browned. Then, put the pepperoni and mushroom slices in a bowl with one can of pizza sauce. Stir the ingredients together., Lay the French bread dough onto a clean, dry surface. Use a sharp knife to cut the dough into eight rounds. Then, press the rounds into three to four inch rounds with your hands or a rolling pin. Press the rounds into the muffin pan, forming dough cups., Pour the mixed ingredients into each dough cup using a spoon. Make sure that the ingredients are distributed evenly. Then, sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese on top of the ingredients. Finish with a pinch of Italian seasoning onto each cup., Put the muffin pan in the oven and bake for 16-18 minutes. The oven should have been already preheated to 350°F (176°C). Bake until the edges are brown and crusty. Then, remove the pan and allow to cool for three to five minutes before serving.
