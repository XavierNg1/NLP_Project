 Ask each player to write a question on the first slip. Collect the papers and put them into a bag or basket.;
, Put into a different bag.
, Ask them to read the two out to the company. The result is sometimes very funny - for instance:


Questions

Do you like roses?
Where are you going to this summer?
Do you like beef?
Do you like spiders?


Answers

Yes, with mustard.
I am very much afraid of them.
Yes, without thorns.
To Switzerland.
