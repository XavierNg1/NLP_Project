 Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.;
, Cut butter into a few chunks and rub it into the flour mixture with your fingertips.
 Pour about 4 tablespoons into the flour mixture and stir until the mixture starts to come together. Add the banana chunks and continue to stir, adding more milk if necessary, until dough comes together.
 Cut into four sections with a knife and place each on baking sheet.
