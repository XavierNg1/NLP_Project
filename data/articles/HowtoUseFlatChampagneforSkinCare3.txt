 In a large bowl, add 1 cup (8 oz.) of granulated sugar and 1/2 cup (4 oz.) of flat champagne. Add six drops of sweet orange essential oil (available at health stores) to the bowl. Stir until it reaches a thick but even texture., Add a 1/4 cup (2 oz.) of coconut oil to a small microwaveable bowl. Heat it in the microwave on a medium setting for 30 seconds, or until melted. Add it to the champagne mixture and stir., Apply the mimosa body scrub to any dry parts of your skin. Use small, circular motions to slough away dead skin. Rinse thoroughly with warm water and pat skin dry.The mixture can also be added to your bath to create a Mimosa soak.
 Note that, if properly sealed, the scrub can last up to a week. Use the mixture up to 3 times a week for better skin. Always moisturize your skin carefully after using the scrub.
