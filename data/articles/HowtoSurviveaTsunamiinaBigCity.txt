 Pack up enough food, water, and emergency supplies well in advance that can last for at least 2-3 days. Almost always the result of earthquakes, tsunamis are very difficult to predict and can strike within minutes.;
, Unlike tornadoes or hurricanes, where underground shelters are sufficient, tsunamis can submerge towns and cities under more than 20–30 feet (6.1–9.1 m) of seawater (sometimes even deeper).
 Most big cities with emergency broadcast systems will air when it is safe to come out of shelters.
