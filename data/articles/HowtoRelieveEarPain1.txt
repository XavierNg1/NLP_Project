 Using a warm compress is the easiest way to provide relief for ear pain caused by an ear infection. To use a warm compress, get a clean cotton washcloth and hold it under warm running water until it is wet. Then, wring out the excess water and place the warm, moist cloth over the affected ear. Hold it there until the washcloth loses its warmth.
