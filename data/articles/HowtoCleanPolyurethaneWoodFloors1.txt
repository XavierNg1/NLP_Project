 You should sweep daily, and a dust mop is ideal. Simply run the dust mop over the floors to pick up any dirt and debris. Shake it out over the trash if needed., If you prefer a bristle broom to a dust mop, you can certainly use one to clean your floors. However, look for one that has synthetic fiber ends (exploded tips), which will be the most efficient., You can vacuum hardwood floors as often as you'd like, though once a week is recommended. Simply set your vacuum to the setting for hardwood floors. If your vacuum doesn't have that setting, try turning off the beater bars and rotating brushes.Vacuuming will pick up most of the dust and debris on your floor.
