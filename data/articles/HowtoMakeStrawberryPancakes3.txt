 Spread butter onto the griddle or frying pan., Set aside., In a large bowl, whisk the flour, both starches, sugar, salt, xanthan gum, baking powder, and baking soda., Pour in the vanilla, melted butter, egg, strawberry puree, and milk. Whisk until well combined.,, Using a ladle, scoop pancake batter from the bowl and pour it on the pan/griddle. Cook each side for about 2-4 minutes until the pancake is lightly golden brown.,, Place a few pancakes on a serving plate and garnish with maple syrup and fresh strawberries. Enjoy!
