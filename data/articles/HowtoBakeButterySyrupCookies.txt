 Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper, if needed, or use a silicone cookie sheet.
 Add either vanilla extract or lemon juice. Stir with a wooden spoon until they are combined., Stir to combine and form into a cookie dough. Add a little milk in if the dough seems too dry., Press it down with your right palm or the back of a spoon. Fill the whole tray like this., Bake for 7 to 10 minutes or until they are golden in color., Allow to cool on the sheet for five minutes, then transfer to a wire cooling rack to cool down completely., Store uneaten cookies in an airtight storage container and finish within a few days of baking.
