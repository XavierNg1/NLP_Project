 Put it in a container with plain water and let it stand overnight, changing the water frequently.
 When it's cooked, remove from heat and remove the skin and bones. Use your fingers to peel it into small pieces., Add chopped parsley, chopped onion, eggs and cod, salt (but not too much because the cod is already salty) and pepper. Knead the dough until the mixture is well blended. If the mixture is too compact, add a little bit of boiling milk. Make sure the batter is neither too thick nor too thin., Scoop the mixture with a spoon, then press it with another tablespoon several times to give the dumpling its particular shape. Fry. When the fritters are golden brown, remove them and drain them on paper towels., Enjoy your meal!
