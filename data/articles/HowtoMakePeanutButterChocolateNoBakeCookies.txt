 In a heavy saucepan, add the sugar, the cocoa, the butter, and the milk. Boil for 1 minute. While it is boiling, stir it continuously with the wooden spoon.

Don't boil this mixture for too long, one minute is sufficient. Any longer may cause the mixture to be over cooked.;
, Once the 1 minute of boiling has finished, remove the saucepan from the stove, and turn off the stove. Mix in the peanut butter, vanilla and oatmeal. Try to be quick with this step., Do this step quickly. Then use the back of the spoon to flatten out the cookies just a bit., The cookies should be set up, and then you're ready to eat!
