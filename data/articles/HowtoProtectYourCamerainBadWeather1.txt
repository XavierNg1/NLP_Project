,, One problem with cold weather is excessive moisture in the air. This is exacerbated when moving from cold conditions directly to warm conditions e.g. from the cold outside to a warm room. Resulting condensation will fog your lens and can damage your camera. By Placing your camera in a plastic bag before entering a warm room, you ensure that moisture condenses on the bag rather than on your camera.
 Leave it in an unheated room to warm up, or alternatively, use a hair dryer on a medium setting.
