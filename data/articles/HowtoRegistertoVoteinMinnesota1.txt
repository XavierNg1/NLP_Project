 To register online, you must have a valid email address and a Minnesota driver’s license, Minnesota state I.D., or a Social Security number. If you do not have one of these, then you will need to fill out a paper application to register., Do this if you are eligible to register online. Minnesota’s voter registration website is: https://mnvotes.sos.state.mn.us/VoterRegistration/VoterRegistrationMain.aspx. Copy and paste the URL address into your Internet browser and click enter. You will then be directed to the voter registration website where you can fill out the application., You will be prompted to answer questions about your citizenship, age, name, and birthdate, as well as your contact information. You must also provide an identification number. In order to register to vote online, you must answer all of the required questions. Once you are done, click submit.A voter registration card will be sent to the address you registered with within seven to ten business days.
The deadline for registering online is 21 days before Election Day at 11:59 p.m.
