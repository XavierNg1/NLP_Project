 Use the affirmative imperative to issue a demand about how someone or something will be.Note that there is no imperative conjugation for the singular first person, "yo" or "I."
Example: "Be happy," Sé feliz.

tú: sé

él/ella/usted: sea

nosotros/-as: seamos

vosotros/-as: sed

ellos/ellas/ustedes: sean

Note that there is no imperative conjugation for the singular first person, "yo" or "I."
Example: "Do not be a coward," No seas un cobarde.

tú: no seas

vosotros/-as: no seáis
