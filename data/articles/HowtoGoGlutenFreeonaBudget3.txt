Do not buy any products with wheat, barley, bulgur, Durham, farina, Graham flour, Kamut, matzo meal, rye, semolina, spelt, or triticale on the label.
Avoid cookies, crackers, pastries, bagels, breads, cakes, candies, beer, oats, dressings, gravies, processed or imitation meats or seafood, pastas, sauces, and soups unless they are specifically marked “gluten-free” due to common contamination with gluten.
