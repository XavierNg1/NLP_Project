 If you have information about a crime such as human trafficking our immigrant exploitation that ICE investigates, you can submit a tip online.You must include your name, email address, and phone number if you submit a tip online. If you would prefer to remain anonymous you must use the toll-free tip line., You can report suspicious activity or information about possible child exploitation or human trafficking anonymously using ICE's toll free number.


In North America, you can call (866) 347-2423. Elsewhere in the world you can call (802) 872-6199. These tip lines are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week., If you are the family member of someone in detention, or another concerned individual such as a member of a nonprofit organization or an advocacy group, you can call the Detention Reporting and Information Line, operated by ICE's Office of Enforcement and Removal Operations at 1-888-351-4024.Call center representatives are available from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. Monday through Friday to help with requests for basic case information or respond to reports of incidents in detention or individuals in detention who are in need of assistance., If you want to locate someone who is currently in ICE detention, or who was released within the last 60 days, you can use ICE's Online Detainee Locator System.


The system can only search for individuals over the age of 18. You can search either by A-number or using the person's first and last name, and country of birth.
