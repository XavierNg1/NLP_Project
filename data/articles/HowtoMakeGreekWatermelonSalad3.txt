 Wash the watermelon and cut it in half. Cut away the rind of one half of the watermelon so you are left with the red fruit. Then cut the fruit into 1 inch (2.54 cm) cubes., Take the block of feta cheese and dice it into pieces that are about ½ inch thick (1.27 cm). You can also use feta cheese in large crumbles if you don’t have access to blocked feta cheese., Mix the orange juice and balsamic vinaigrette in a small salad dressing pitcher along with a pinch of salt and pepper. Then add the olive oil and mix., Place the watermelon and feta cheese in a large bowl and mix. Then pour on the vinaigrette over the salad and garnish with shredded mint leaves. Serve the salad while cold.
