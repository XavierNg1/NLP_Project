 Boil 7 cups of the water, and pour in the tapioca balls, stirring as you do. Let them cook for 25 minutes on medium heat. Be sure to stir every few minutes.Rinse off the balls. Do so for each serving you are making., Add a tablespoon of sweetened condensed milk to a shaker along with some ice. Once the tea has steeped, add it to the shaker, and shake it until it's cooled., Add a couple of tablespoons of bubbles to a glass, along with some ice. Pour the tea over the ice.
