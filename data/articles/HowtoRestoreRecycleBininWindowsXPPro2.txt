 Select 'Run' from the All Programs option. Then, type regedit into the text box provided., Navigate to the following directory: HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\Desktop\NameSpace., Then hit 'New' and then 'Key'., This will create a new registry key., Then, type 'Recycle Bin' into the field. You have successfully restored the Recycle Bin.
