 All available Microsoft positions in the world will be accessible to you on this website.


Go to the "Microsoft Careers" website provided to you in the Sources section of this article to begin your job search.



Select a country or region from the drop-down menu provided, or search for jobs by entering specific keywords or by choosing a job category. For example, if you want to work in software engineering, select this category from the "Job Categories" drop-down menu.
Click on the "Search" button after you have entered your job criteria. You will then be presented with a list of job titles that match your search criteria.
 The Microsoft Careers website will allow you to apply for any number of positions at once.


Click on the link for any job title, then click on the "Apply to job" or "Add to job cart" buttons located below the job description.
Create your resume using the Microsoft resume interface provided. You will also have the option to paste your resume into a text field.
Follow the on-screen prompts to submit your resume to Microsoft. Your resume will then be sent to Microsoft recruiters, who will review your information to determine if you qualify for the positions you applied for.
