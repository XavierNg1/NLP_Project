 Pandora is a free app (with a paid option) that allows you to listen to streaming music on your Android., Tap Sign Up, then follow the on-screen instructions to create your account., You can listen to any of suggested stations, or tap Browse Genre Stations for more options., Enter one artist to create a station that plays similar music, or multiple artists to add variety.

See Create a Pandora Station to learn how to further customize your station.
