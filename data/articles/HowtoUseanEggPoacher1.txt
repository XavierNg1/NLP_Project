 Fill the bottom of the pan with enough water to touch the bottom of the cups when they are placed in the pan.;
, Crack each egg and pour it into a cup. If you are not using all the cups, fill the extra cups with water to avoid any burning.
, Cover the poacher with the lid and cook the eggs for 2 to 3 minutes. Eggs are cooked when the outsides are pale white and the yolk is soft.
