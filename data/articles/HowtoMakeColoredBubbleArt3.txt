 You will need tempera paint, an old tray, washing-up liquid, a sponge, construction paper, and a container., Combine a cup of washing-up liquid and a few tablespoons of paint until properly mixed through using the drinking straw.,, Press the soup onto the paper using the sponge, until you have as many pretty bubble patterns and designs as desired. Use multiple colors for an extra vibrant piece of art., Hang it up and enjoy!
