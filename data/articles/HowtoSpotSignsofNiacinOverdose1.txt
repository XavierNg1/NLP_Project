 One of the most visible symptoms of a niacin overdose is flush skin. This can include redness, warm, itchy or irritated skin, as well as a tingly feeling under the skin. These symptoms can be enhanced if alcoholic or hot beverages are taken shortly after the niacin., Niacin is processed by the liver, and as a result, an overdose can cause the development of jaundice like symptoms. This includes yellowing of the skin and eyes., A niacin overdose can cause symptoms associated with nausea and an upset stomach. For example, you may experience vomiting, stomach pains, and diarrhea.
