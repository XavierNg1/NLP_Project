 If you grow your own lavender, pick the lavender buds from the garden, early morning. Wash them and dry on a paper towel before proceeding. If you don't have your own lavender, purchase lavender buds from a specialty grocery store.;
, Prepare the cake tin with baking paper.
, Stir through well.
 Beat together until well blended.
 Smooth over the top.
 The cake will be ready when a skewer comes out clean.
 Leave to cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then place on a wire cooling rack to cool completely.
 Make the icing using either rosewater or lemon essence for flavouring (either is superb with lavender cake). For increased effect, add a drop of purple food colouring for a light lavender shade. Leave to set.
