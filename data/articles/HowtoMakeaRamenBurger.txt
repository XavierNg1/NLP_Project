  Make 4 hamburger patties. Grill or fry as desired.;
, Drain noodles.
  Separate the noodle in half.  Place one half in one ramekin dish, the other half in a second ramekin dish. Place saran wrap over each dish and press the noodles down with a can of food or another bowl.  Refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  Stir.
  Fry until golden brown on both sides.  You have just made your buns.
  Time to assemble.
  Place a layer of arugula, then the hamburger patty.  Next, put cheese slice, the fried egg, and onions (sauté, chopped green onions, or onion rings).  Stand back and admire your masterpiece.
