 Since 2007 roof measurements have been available from multiple online services. These services utilize satellite and/or aerial imagery to measure roofs wherever they have image coverage. These services are able to achieve their measurements remotely from computers and deliver their roof measurements via email for a small fee.
 Waste is dependent on the complexity of your roof and how you decide to roof your valleys (there are 3 methods of roofing a valley). Check out How to Estimate Roofing Materials for more help on these calculations.
