 There are tons of options out there for video looping but some of the more recommended and trustworthy options are GIF Chat, Loopideo, Video Roll, and Haffy. While there are some options that cost a few dollars, you should be able to find at least a few great selections that are free. On your iPhone, click go to the App Store and find the app that works best for you., You can do this by simply selecting"Download" and "Install" in the App Store and then waiting for the app to load.
 Usually, you will be able to select a video from within the looping app.
 Different apps will have different options for you to add music, transitions, and other frills. Explore your different options and get creative! Once you have finished choosing your setting, select "Loop."
