 The SSA offers automated messages that provide status information 24 hours per day.You can call the SSA at 1-800-772-1213 or via TTY, a telecommunication device for the deaf or hard of hearing, at 1-800-325-0778.
Listen to and follow any prompts, such as one asking you to enter your Social Security number. Then listen to the information about your claim status.
 SSA agents are available Monday through Friday between the hours of 7 a.m. and 7 p.m. at the SSA toll-free number, 1-800-772-1213, or via TTY, a telecommunication device for the deaf or hard of hearing, at 1-800-325-0778.


Note that the SSA offers free interpreter services for many different languages., If you don't want to call the national toll-free number, you may be able to reach a representative at your local office. Usually the state SSA agency is responsible for processing your claim. Ask the representative about your Social Security claim status.


You can find the office information (including phone number) using a phone book, or you can find it online using the SSA's office locator (link below).
