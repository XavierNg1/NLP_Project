 Smiling is a quick and easy way to show everyone around you that you want to connect with other people. You’re much more likely to be approached by someone if you’re smiling!, Loving, kind people are much more likely to find love. If you ignore other people or only say negative things to them, it will be much harder for a prospective partner to approach you. Try to say something nice to someone every day--it will be a habit very soon., If you can do or say something to help another person, do it!
