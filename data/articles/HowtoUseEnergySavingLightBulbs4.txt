 They can be used indoors or out of doors when the light is pointing downward. When the light is pointing upward, they need to be in enclosed fixtures if out of doors. Some brands can be used in cold weather down to −10 °F (−23 °C). Below that the manufacturers can’t guarantee that the light bulb will head up enough to actually come on. You can see Energy Savings of up to $50 over the life of the light bulb.


Dimmable Reflector Floodlight bulbs are available and seem to be very popular amongst folks with homes that have ceilings resembling Swiss cheese. Reflector Floods are used in recessed fixtures and open hanging fixtures.
