3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
3 cups chicken broth
8 oz. cream cheese
4 oz. grated Monterey Jack cheese
4 oz. grated cheddar
2 seeded diced jalapeño peppers
4 oz. chopped canned green chiles
1 1/2 tsp. cumin
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Fresh corn from 2 cobs
1/4 cup salsa
1/3 cup panko
4 green bell peppers
