 Turn the oven on to 350 degrees (176°C). While the oven is preheating, prepare your ingredients. Set out your wonton skins, pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese, and choice of toppings., You can use 24 wonton wrappers, or use more if you have more ingredients. Press each wonton wrapper into the spaces of the muffin tin. Make sure that the edges of the wrappers do not curl in., Put the pan with the wonton wrappers in the oven. Bake the wrappers for seven minutes. Remove after seven minutes when the wrappers are firm., Fill each wonton wrapper with one teaspoon of marinara sauce (or any other kind of pizza sauce). Then, filled the wrappers with the ingredients of your choice. You can choose ingredients like sausage, pepperoni, mushrooms, and onions. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded cheese on top of the ingredients.Make sure ingredients, like sausage, has been cooked before putting them in the wonton cups.
 Once all of the ingredients are in the wonton wrappers, put the muffin pan back in the oven. Bake the pizza cups for seven to ten minutes on 350°F (176°C) until the cheese has melted. Remove from oven and serve warm.
