 PRWeb, Marketwire and other services have proven channels that they use to disseminate a press release. You will usually find it appears on more services and is ranked higher, if you pay for a service like this.


If you do not have a PR professional distributing your press release, make sure that 1 of these services will submit it to news RSS Feeds, where it can be picked up by journalists.
