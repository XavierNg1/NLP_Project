Enjoy all that the library has to offer from fish tanks to toys., Here is a great schedule for story time:

Open with a song
Recite rhymes
Catch the children's attention with a puppet, costume, object, food, or video
Read a book
Talk about the story and ask children questions about the story
Color a coloring page or do an activity that goes with the story
