 Washing your gloves in a washing machine may damage them and will shorten the amount of time you can use them. Hand washing is a much more gentle process., Moisturizers and similar products could ruin the palm grips of your gloves. They could also result in cracking and other issues. As a result, do not put any products on your gloves that are not intended for them., Washing your gloves with soap, especially dishwashing detergent or clothes detergent, could ruin the grip on your gloves. In addition, it might dry the gloves out, decreasing their longevity.
