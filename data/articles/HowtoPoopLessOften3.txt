 Generally speaking several bowel movements per day is considered more than normal, especially if this changes suddenly. Increased bowel movements or changes in the consistency, volume, or appearance of poop can indicate an underlying medical condition.
 Be prepared to inform your healthcare provider about your bowel habits, and the typical consistency, frequency, and appearance of your poop.
