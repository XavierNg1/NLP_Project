 Milk will remove all potion and status effects that you may be currently experiencing. Hold the right mouse button with the milk bucket equipped to drink it.You cannot currently drink milk in Minecraft PE. The ability to drink milk is scheduled to come with Minecraft PE 0.11.0., Milk's other user besides drinking is as an ingredient for Cakes. You'll need 3 Milk Buckets, along with 3 bushels of Wheat, 2 Sugars, and an Egg.


Place the 3 Milk Buckets along the top row of the Crafting window. After crafting, your empty buckets will be left behind.
Place the Sugar and Egg in the center row, with the Egg in the middle.
Place the 3 Wheat in the bottom row.
The cake will need to be placed on a surface before it can be eaten.
