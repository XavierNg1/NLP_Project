 Fill a pot with water and place it on the stove. Turn the stove on to high and wait for the water to begin boiling. This method is generally safe for wine glasses made out of crystal or glass. It may not be safe for painted or glittered wine glasses, however.
 Slip the stem between your fingers loosely so that the base is resting on your hand.
 If the steam is not covering the glass, try bringing it closer to the water. Do not let the glass touch the water, however, or it may crack.
 The hot steam will have sterilized the glass.
 Choose a soft cloth, such as microfiber, and wipe down the inside and outside of the glass.
