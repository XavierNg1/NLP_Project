 You can chill a large glass in the fridge for a few minutes. Then, add the whiskey, kahlua, and lactart to the glass. Stir them together so they're completely combined., Slowly add your scoops of ice cream one at a time. Gingerly release them into the glass to avoid causing your coffee mixture to splatter., Once the ice cream is added, pour your soda over the top of the mixture. The type of soda you add is up to you.
