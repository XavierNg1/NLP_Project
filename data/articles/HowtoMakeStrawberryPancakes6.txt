 Spray non-stick spray onto the griddle or frying pan., In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt., Add the oil, water, eggs, and vanilla into the dry ingredient mixture. Stir until well combined.,, Using a ladle, scoop pancake batter from the bowl onto the pan/griddle. Cook each side for about 2-4 minutes until the pancake is lightly golden brown.,, Place a few pancakes on a serving plate and garnish with maple syrup and fresh strawberries. Enjoy!
