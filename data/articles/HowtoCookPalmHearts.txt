 If you get palm hearts very young and fresh, the centers can be eaten raw by slicing and adding to a salad. Select only the most tender parts of the heart.


Drained canned palm hearts can also be added to a salad.;

 Both of these cooking methods are the traditional way to cook palm hearts. Other methods include light boiling, battering, deep-frying, and adding to curries.
