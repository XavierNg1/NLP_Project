 If you wake up with swollen eyes in the morning, an eye gel can help. Apply the eye gel around your eyes before bed. Gels tend to work better than oils on eyes, as oils can seep into your eyes, making them puffier.If your puffy eyes are due to an allergy or cold, you should also take a decongestant before bed.
 Ceramides are the lipids found on skin. If your lips tend to dry out overnight, look for a lip gel that contains ceramides. Apply a heavy layer before bed.
