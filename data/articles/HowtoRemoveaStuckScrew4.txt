 Even if you are not a skilled welder, it is possible to adhere a nut to the stripped screw head. Purchase a super strong welding adhesive. Find a nut that is the same diameter as the screw head., Place the nut on the screw head, making sure it is centered. Carefully fill the nut with the super strong welding adhesive. Allow the product to dry the recommended amount of time., Make sure the nut has completely adhered to the screw. Grab a socket wrench and place it on the nut. Rotate the socket wrench and remove the stripped screw from the surface.
