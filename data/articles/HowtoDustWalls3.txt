 Ideally, the mop head or cloth will be made of microfiber. The exact process by which you change the dusting cloth or mop head depends on the manufacturer. Generally, however, the process is as simple as laying the new cloth over the flat head of the mop, then moving the clips that bind it to the top of the mop head.Other flat head mops have replaceable heads that simply slide on.
Microfiber cloth excels at collecting dust and is minimally abrasive.
 Moving along the wall in a “W” pattern will ensure you are dusting the entire wall. Start at the top left corner of the wall, then move down and over, then up and over in a continuous motion., Wipe the wall down using the damp microfiber cloth, especially on areas where dust seems to have been left behind.
