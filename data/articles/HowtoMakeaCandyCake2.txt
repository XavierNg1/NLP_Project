 In a saucepan, melt one package of miniature marshmallows, ¾ cup of canola oil, and ½ cup of butter. Stir them together until it is smooth., In a large bowl, combine 20 cups (5 quarts) of popped popcorn, 1 package of gumdrops, and 1 cup of salted peanuts., Pour the melted marshmallow mixture into the popcorn and candy mixture. Toss the ingredients in the bowl to coat the popcorn and candy in the marshmallow sauce., Press the mixture into a greased 10-inch tube pan. Don’t use a two-piece tube pan for this cake. Cover the pan and refrigerate it for at least five hours., Dip the pan in hot water for 5-10 seconds. This will help unmold it. Remove the cake from the pan. Slice it with an electric or serrated knife and serve it.
