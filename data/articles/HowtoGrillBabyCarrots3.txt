 Wash the carrots if you need to. You can chop the carrots in half or leave them whole. They cook faster if chopped in half. Lightly pat them dry with a paper towel., Melt the butter in a bowl. Add the oil, sugar, and zest to the bowl, as well as the salt, pepper, and the seasoning of your choice. Stir until the sugar is fully incorporated into the glaze., On medium heat, let the carrots cook for about 5 minutes or so. Start brushing the butter mixture on the vegetables. About every 2 minutes, brush more glaze on., The carrots should cook for about 20 minutes. When they are fork-tender, place in a serving bowl. Pour any remaining glaze over the carrots, and toss them to coat them in the mixture.
