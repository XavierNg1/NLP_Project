 To check your Internet speed from your computer's settings, you must connect your computer to your router via Ethernet cable., This icon is in the bottom-left corner of the screen., It's in the bottom-left corner of the Start window., It's in the top row of settings on the Settings page., This tab is on the left side of the window. Doing so will open your Ethernet settings., It's in the upper-right side of the window., You'll see this monitor-shaped icon at the top of the Network Connections window., It's near the bottom of the "Connections" section of information on the Ethernet Status window. The number here (e.g., "90.0 Mbps") represents the speed of the connection between your computer and the router.
