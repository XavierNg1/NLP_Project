 Add 1/2 cup of cocoa powder and place in the microwave for 60 seconds. Mix with an electric mixer to remove all clumps of cocoa powder from the mixture.;

 For chocolate chips or chocolate curls, you may wish to finely chop the chocolate from the sheet, then place in a small glass jar.
