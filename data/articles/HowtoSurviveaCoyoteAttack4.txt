Coyotes are creatures of habit.Be sure to change your routine if you see a coyote at the same time and place while walking your pet., Coyotes involved in biting attacks will be targeted and removed from the population. They are then often tested for rabies and are killed if found infected. However, it is important to note that an attack by an individual coyote does not warrant the general killing of coyotes. Recall that coyote attacks on humans are exceptionally rare.
