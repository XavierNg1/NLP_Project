 Adding seasoning is quite simplistic, and requires little time. Mix the following in a small pan, and warm on the stove:


1/2 cup corn oil
6 Tablespoons of chili powder
3 Tablespoons of garlic powder
3 Tablespoons of ground cumin
1 Tablespoon black pepper (or less).
2 Tablespoons of salt. Keep these within reach, as some will be needed later.
