 Before Garrus joins your squad, he mentions that a bounty hunter named Wrex is being questioned at the C-Sec office., You can find the C-Sec office by heading west from the clinic and taking the first set of stairs you find downward. The office is at the end of a short hallway to the north., Wrex is near the Wards entrance to the C-Sec office. Talk to him after he is questioned by the C-Sec officer.,
