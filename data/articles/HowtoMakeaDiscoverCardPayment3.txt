 Your account statement will list all of your monthly transactions, your total balance, and your monthly minimum payment. It also includes a detachable payment form. Pull this off and send it along with your payment.
 Do not send cash because this can easily be taken by a third party. Write your account number on the memo line of the check to help identify the payment.Send all payments to the address listed on your account statement.
 There is a reason why they call it “snail mail.” If you want to ensure that your payment arrives on time, you need to send it 7-10 days before it is due.
