 You will be using the side of the grater with the largest holes.
 Measure the amount of butter which you need for your recipe, using the markings on the butter packaging or a kitchen scales.
 Grate the butter directly into the mixing bowl. It's best to keep the butter wrapped in its packaging with this method, as it will prevent you from getting butter all over your hands. This will help to soften the butter and is great for use in crumbles and shortbread.
