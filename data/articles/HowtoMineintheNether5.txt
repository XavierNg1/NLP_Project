 Sometimes, there is Nether Wart growing on Soul Sand around the staircases. Mine the Nether Wart and the Soul Sand as well. This way, if you place Soul Sand in the Overworld, you will be able to grow Nether Wart.


Nether Wart is crucial in brewing. They are used to create the Awkward Potion, a basic ingredient for other complex potions. You can grow them in the Overworld by placing them on Soul Sand.
