 Applying a finish to the leather will help seal in the paint and will give the leather a nicer looking finish. High gloss will be reflective and will look shiny, while a matte finish will give the paint a softer, less shiny, look. Once you decide on which finish you want, purchase the leather finisher online.Matte and satin finishes will look shiny when they are first applied but then dry to their respective finishes.
 Saturate a sponge brush with the leather finisher and apply it in thin coats over the painted leather. Continue going over the surface of the leather, making sure to fill in any seams., Put the leather in a dust-free area until it dries. The finisher will dry after a full 24-hours. Your leather should now look professionally painted.
