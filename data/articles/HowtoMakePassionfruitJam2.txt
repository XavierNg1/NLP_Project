 Peel and cut them into chunks., Sprinkle sugar over the top. Add another layer and sprinkle with sugar again. Continue until all the chunks are used up., Leave in the refrigerator, covered., Cook until soft., Briefly warm the lemon juice and add it too. Stir through., Test for setting point, when a gel forms; this stage will be reached after about 15 to 25 minutes of cooking.

If gelling isn't occurring, add more lemon juice.
Sterilize the jam jars during the boiling of the jam.
 Seal, label and date. Store in a cool, dry and dark place for 1 to 2 years.
