 Mobile numbers do not have an associated area code or city code. To call a Ukrainian mobile number, dial in the following format: exit code – 380 – XX– xxx – xxxx. Network codes are 2-digit numbers, and are different depending on the mobile service provider.Some of the common mobile network codes in Ukraine are 39, 50, 63, 66, 67 and 68.
