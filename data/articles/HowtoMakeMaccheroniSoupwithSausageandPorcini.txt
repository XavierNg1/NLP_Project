 When the butter begins to melt, add the onion, carrot and celery. Sauté until the vegetables begin to brown, about 5 minutes.
 Peel the tomatoes and coarsely chop them.
 Break up the larger pieces of sausage with a wooden spoon and cook, stirring often, until it loses its raw color.
 Do not discard the water. Rinse the porcini under running water, chop them coarsely, and add them to the pot along with the tomatoes. Season lightly with salt and cook, stirring for about 2 minutes.
