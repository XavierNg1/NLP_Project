 Set the temperature to 350 °F (177 °C)., Place a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the oil, and let it heat up. Throw in the onion, and cook until the onion is translucent. Take it off the heat., In a bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and salt., That is, use your hand to make a depression in the flour mound., Add the buttermilk, butter, and egg whites to another bowl. Mix them together., Stir it together to incorporate the flour and liquid. Don't overmix., Stir in the onions, olives, and oregano. Fold them in gently., Spray an 8 inch (20.3 cm) by 4 inch loaf pan with cooking spray. Spoon the batter in the pan evenly. Wipe off any spilled batter., Test for doneness by inserting a toothpick. It should be clean when it comes out., Let it sit for 10 minutes. Once it's cooled, take the bread out and place it on the wire rack to cool further.
