 Prepare a round cake pan by either lining with parchment/baking paper or greasing with butter. You can also use both.
 Bring to a boil on medium to low heat. Allow to simmer until the liquid fills the fruit.
 Stir for 2 to 3 minutes, waiting for it to come to a boil and thicken.
 Cover with a lid or plastic wrap and leave to cool to lukewarm.
 Add the eggs one at a time. Beat well after each egg is added.
 Then fold in the sultana mixture.
 Smooth out the top to make it even.
 Bake for 2 hours. It is ready when it is firm in the middle, when gently pushed.
 Allow to cool in the pan for 15 minutes. Then remove it from the pan and allow to cool fully on the wire cooling rack.
