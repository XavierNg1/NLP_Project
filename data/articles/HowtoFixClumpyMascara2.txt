 Remove the mascara's wand from the bottle. Using any brand of eyedrops, which you can find in a drug store, add 10 small drops to the mascara., Place the wand back in the container. Give the container a good shake for a few seconds. This should disperse the eyedrops throughout the container., Unscrew the wand slightly and twirl it around in the mascara. Then, check the mascara. If successful, the mascara should be restored to its original state.
