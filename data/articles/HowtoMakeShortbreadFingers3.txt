 You'll need:

250g butter, softened
1/3 cup or 75g caster/superfine sugar
1/4 cup or 35g rice flour
2 1/4 cups/335g plain or all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons granulated white sugar.
 Grease the baking sheet or pan. Alternatively, line with parchment paper., Beat with an electric mixer until light and fluffy., Stir through., Knead together., Press down evenly across the sheet or pan., Using a fork, prick holes across the dough surface. Sprinkle with the granulated sugar., Bake for around 40 minutes., Let stand to cool in the baking sheet or pan for 5 minutes, then cut along the finger marks., Once cooled, they are ready for serving or storage.
