 Green tea contains polyphenols, plant chemicals that help to neutralize the sulphur compounds released in onions and garlic. Antioxidants in green tea are also effective in combating bad breath., Milk has been shown to be effective in the fight against garlic breath. Whole milk, in particular, reduces the concentration of foul-smelling compounds in your mouth., Lemon, lime, grapefruit and cranberry juices, as well as many soft drinks, help to fight the odor-producing alliinase enzyme found in onions and garlic.
