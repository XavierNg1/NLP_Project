 Sudden, sharp stabbing pains on one side of the head, a watery eye and runny nose on the same side of the face can indicate a cluster headache., Short, consistent bouts of stabbing pain on one side of the face usually indicate cluster headache, which affects more men than women.
 A severe headache consistently appearing during morning or late night might be a cluster headache.
 A cure doesn't exist for cluster headache, which affects less than 1 percent of the human population.
