 This is the form you need to fill out to get a copy of your Form I-797 Notice of Action. The form is available for download from the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) website. You should download it in PDF format and also get a copy of the instructions.You can also request a form by calling 1-800-375-5283.
You cannot use this form if your approval notice has expired or been revoked.;
, If available, send a copy of the original I-140 petition. If you don’t have a copy, then ask your employer to provide you with one. However, if you don’t have it, then you don’t need to include it., If you have your Form I-797 (but want an additional copy), then send a copy along with your application.You need to submit both the front and the back.


If you don’t have the Form I-797, then you don’t need to send it.
 You can complete the form in PDF format. Type in the requested information. Answer all questions and type “N/A” or “not applicable” to anything that applies. The form will ask for the following information:your reason for the request: check “A duplicate approval notice”
name
date of birth
country of birth
identifying information, such as Alien Registration Number, Social Security Number, IRS Tax Number, etc.
mailing address
physical address

, You have to pay a $405 filing fee. Make your check or money order payable to “U.S. Department of Homeland Security.” Don’t use abbreviations like “DHS” or “USDHS.”Fees are subject to change. You should call 1-800-375-5283 for updated fee information.
 Make a copy of your complete application for your records. Then put your application in a secure envelope and mail it to the appropriate address. You can get the appropriate address by calling the National Customer Service Center at 1-800-375-5283.USCIS might contact you for more information. Please provide requested information promptly.
