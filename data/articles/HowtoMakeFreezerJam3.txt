Set aside for 10 minutes., Bring to the boil, stirring constantly, boiling and stirring for 1 minute., Stir for 3 minutes., Leave 1/2 inch/ 1/5cm space from the top of the jam to the lid, for expansion., Let stand in the refrigerator until it sets; this should not be any longer than 24 hours. It can be eaten immediately or stored. Label and date each jar., Refrigerate for up to 3 weeks, or freeze for up to 1 year. Upon thawing (in the fridge), consumer within 3 weeks.
