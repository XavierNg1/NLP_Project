 Preheat the oven to 350 °F (177 °C). Line a loaf pan with parchment paper.

Ensure you make the paper as flat as you possibly can and smooth out the wrinkles and bumps.This will make it easier to pour the items in the pan.;
, Pour the flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon, and cloves and stir with a whisk., Pour in the eggs, oil, milk, vanilla, and pumpkin puree in another large bowl., Mix with a hand or electric blender on low speed until well-combined and there are no more flour streaks., Scrape off any excess batter with a rubber spatula., The results of the heated Nutella should be slightly gooey and soft afterwards., Drizzle it all around the batter.,,, Cut the bread into slices. Lay a piece on a serving plate. Garnish with whipped cream or extra Nutella. Enjoy!
