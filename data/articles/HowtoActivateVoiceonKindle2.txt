 The reading toolbar appears at the top of the screen., The settings button is in the bottom left of the reading toolbar., Note that Text to Speech is not available for every Kindle book. Furthermore, Text to Speech is currently only available in the U.S. and in English.Amazon will list if Text to Speech is supported in the “Product Details” section of a book’s store page., The “Play” button is located next to the reading progress bar and will start reading the text aloud from the start of the current page.

Sound will play through the Kindle's external speakers or you can use earphones plugged into the headphone jack.
