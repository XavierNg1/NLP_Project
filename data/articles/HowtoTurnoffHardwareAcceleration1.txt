 Most newer computers using Nvidia or AMD/ATI graphics cards will not have the ability to change the amount of acceleration through. These options are typically only available on older computers or computers using onboard video.In order to change the acceleration settings for these cards, you'll need to open the control panel for your card. You can access it by right-clicking on your desktop and selecting your video card control panel.
The acceleration settings will vary depending on the manufacturer and the card model. You'll generally it in the "System settings" or "Image settings" sections.
