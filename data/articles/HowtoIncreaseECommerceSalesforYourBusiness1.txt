 A business needs to be where their customers are. This is why social networks, like Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, and Twitter, are a great way to start. Create your pages on these networks.

Optimize your pages by adding a nice display picture, cover image, and page description. Design a page that helps identify what your page is selling. Do not forget to include About Me and Contact Details.;
, Once you have created the page, you now need to increase the number of fans. You can do this by sharing relevant content that interests your target audience. Offer special discounts and coupons to your fans. Conduct contests and encourage your users to participate in them by offering them rewards, like discount or freebies.
