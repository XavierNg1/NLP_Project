 You can cheat and use a good quality purchased fudge sauce. Be sure the sauce is gently warmed so the sauce will cause the ice cream to slightly melt and ooze down into the brownie. This is what makes this sundae so yummy. Warm fudge sauce, melting ice cream and of course the brownie.
