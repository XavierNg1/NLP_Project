 Follow the instructions on the packaging for timing. Add a dash of olive oil to the cooking water.
 Rinse under cold water to stop the cooking. Drain again. Set aside to cool.
 Add the cooked pasta and gently toss through.
 Pour all of the ingredients into a clean jar with a lid. Add the lid and shake well.
 Toss through well.
 Place in the refrigerator and chill for at least half an hour.
 Toss once more before serving.
