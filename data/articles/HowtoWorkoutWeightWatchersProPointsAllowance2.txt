 You are a 29-year-old female who is nursing. You weigh 175 pounds and are 5'6". You do a moderate amount of daily physical exercise. What is your allowance?


To add up:
Female, nursing: 10 points
29 years old: 3 points
Moderately active: 4 points
5'6" height: 1 point
175 pounds: 17 points


10 + 3 + 4 + 1 + 17 = 35 ProPoints
