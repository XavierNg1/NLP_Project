 Call 1-866-809-0290 (toll-free) between 6:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. (Central Standard Time), Monday-Friday (except holidays)., A representative will guide you through questions to verify your identity and to complete the application requesting a birth certificate. Be prepared to answer these questions by having the relevant information available, including:


Name as it appears on the record
Date of birth
Place of birth
Parents' names
Reason for requesting a copy of the certificate
Your relationship to the person named on the certificate (if you are requesting your own certificate, then this is "self")
The address you want your copy sent to

, You will have to pay by credit card (American Express, Discovery, Mastercard, or Visa) the $20 fee for a certified copy of your birth certificate as well as a $13 fee to screen your call and verify your identity.


The $20 fee includes the cost of retrieving the certificate, and making one certified copy. If you want multiple certified copies of the birth certificate, you must pay $20 more for each additional copy.
It will take approximately two weeks to process your request.
Certificates will be mailed 7-10 business days after processing.
