 Leave the cream cheese on the counter for several hours before starting this recipe., A heart mold works well for this recipe., Add the 4 ounces of cream cheese, 1/3 cup of yogurt, 4 teaspoons of sugar, 1 teaspoon lemon zest, 1/4 teaspoon lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon vanilla, and a dash of salt to a bowl. Whisk the ingredients together until thoroughly combined., Run the mixture through a clean strainer to remove any lemon pulp or big pieces of zest., Fill the mold with the mixture, and then cover it up with the cheesecloth. Set in the refrigerator for about 4 hours., Release the mold onto a plate, setting the coeur in the middle of the plate. Let it stand for about 20 minutes., Use the squeeze bottle to add a pool of coulis around the coeur. You can also drizzle it over the top. Garnish with a raspberry if desired.
