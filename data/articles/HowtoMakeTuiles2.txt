 After it has rested, add the melted butter and stir through.
 Pour the teaspoonful onto the prepared tray. Spread thinly with the back of a fork that has been dipped in cold water.
 Leave plenty of space between each one to allow for spreading during baking.
 Bake for 5-8 minutes (this longer time compared to the above recipe is due to the fact that the temperature is quite a bit lower).
 Since these biscuits are dark, it'll be hard to judge readiness just by looking. You'll need touch to test these ones.
 Roll over the rolling pin or clean bottle to curve each biscuit. Repeat for each biscuit.
 Let it cool and harden.


If using almonds as garnish, press into the biscuits before they harden.
