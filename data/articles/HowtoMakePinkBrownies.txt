 Place the sugar, flour, and vanilla into a large baking bowl. Mix ingredients thoroughly with a whisk or wooden spoon.;
, Add four drops of pink coloring and two drops of green. Pour the cocoa powder in the bowl as well. Stir.
 Pour brownie mixture in a small, glass tray. Bake on HIGH for six whole minutes. Watch as brownies rise.

If brownies are watery and have not risen, place them in microwave again for an addition thirty seconds or a minute.
 Have them cool for about 3-4 minutes.
 Use a butter knife to lather frosting and jam over brownies. Use can also use a frosting pipe to make letters or shapes.
