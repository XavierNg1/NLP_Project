 As the rubbing alcohol transfers from the paper towel to the sticker, it should start dissolving the glue. You should only have to wait a few minutes for this to happen., After letting the alcohol work, most of the glue should have dissolved. Most, if not all of the sticker should come off easily. Scrub with the paper towel, and you should be able to lift most of the sticker., Make sure to follow the grain of the stainless steel as you do so. Otherwise your surface could lose its luster, and even get scratched. Thanks to the alcohol, you should be able to simply scratch off whatever is left of the sticker.
