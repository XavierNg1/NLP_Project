, Line the cake pan with baking/parchment paper.
 Grate coarsely. Place in a separate bowl and cover in the lemon juice. The juice will stop the grated apple from going brown.
 Remove when cooked, drain and rinse under cold water.
 Beat lightly.
 Fold in the cooled pasta and grated apples.
 Cover with foil before placing in the oven to bake. Bake for 40 minutes.
 Sprinkle with the demerara sugar. Let the cake bake another 10 minutes uncovered until the sugar turns lightly brown.
 Leave in the pan for 15 minutes to cool and set.
 Add a blob of whipped cream or a dash of plain yogurt. The cake should be served at room temperature and eaten on the day of baking.
