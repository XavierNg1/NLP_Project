 Measure all of the tahini dressing ingredients into small blender or jar with a lid. Shake or blend the ingredients until the dressing is smooth and combined. You'll need:3 tablespoons tahini
1½ tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
2 tablespoons water
Juice of half a lemon
2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
1 tablespoon curry powder
1 teaspoon turmeric
½ teaspoon salt and pepper to taste

, Wash your fresh produce and chop the green onions, half of a small bunch of cilantro, and 1 bell pepper. You'll also need to chop 1/3 cup (50 g) of cashews.Chop enough of the green onions to get 1/2 cup (75 g).
 Open 2 15-ounce (425 g) cans of chickpeas and drain them. Rinse the beans and place them in your serving bowl. Add the chopped green onions, cilantro, bell pepper cashews, and ½ cup (75 g) of raisins. Stir the dressing over the salad and serve it.Refrigerate any leftover curried chickpea salad. Keep in mind that any leftovers will become stronger as they sit.
