 Line or grease the cake pan.;
, Add the sugar and stir to melt.
 Add the sugar and salt and mix through.
 Add the honey or syrup.
 Mix well.
 Use the spatula to remove any mixture left stuck to the bowl.
 Bake for one hour or until a skewer inserted into the cake comes out clean.
 Leave to cool on a wire baking rack for 5 minutes, then remove from the pan and leave to cool on the rack out of the pan.
