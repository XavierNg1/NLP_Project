 Once you get to your purchase history, scroll through the items you've bought and locate the order that you want to contact eBay about., Click the "More Options" link with the downward arrow on the right side of your item. If you don't see this option, it's because you need to try to contact the seller first.
 eBay will take up to two full business days to respond to your request. After reviewing your case and the item that you purchased, eBay will either give you a refund for the item, or give you more details about your purchase and additional steps to follow.
