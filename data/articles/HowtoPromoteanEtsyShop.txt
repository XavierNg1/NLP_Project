, Wanelo helps you bookmark and share products. You can post your wares on Wanelo and other users will save them to their own collections, which will help send you traffic. Some of that traffic will end up buying something.
 Twitter is a great place to connect with people and promote your Etsy shop. It's important to regularly update, have interesting content, and to be social. @-reply other users, respond to your replies, and reach out to people. If you have a specialty, like knitting, you could give advice and tips on your Twitter and then occasionally link to an item in your store.
