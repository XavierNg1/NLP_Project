Prioritize your enjoyment of the experience with your pet, and your dog's comfort, over getting your dog clean in a fast and efficient manner.A slow pace will also allow you to use a softer water pressure to wash and rinse your dog, which will be more soothing to them., Use positive reinforcement to make your small dog more comfortable with their bath time.
