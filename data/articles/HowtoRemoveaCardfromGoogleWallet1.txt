 Open a new web browser tab or window, and visit the Google Wallet page.;
, Under the Sign In box, type in your Gmail email address and password. This is your one Google ID for all of Google’s services, including Google Wallet. Click the “Sign in” button to proceed.
 Click the link for “Payment methods” from the left panel menu. A list of all credit and debit cards linked to your Google Wallet account will be displayed here.
 Select the card you’d like to remove, and click the “Remove” link beside it. Click the “Remove” or “OK” button when prompted to confirm the deletion.
