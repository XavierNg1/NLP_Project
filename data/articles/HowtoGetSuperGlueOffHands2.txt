 Both petroleum jelly and lotion can help to break the bond of the glue to your skin. Use a liberal amount so that the affected area is completely saturated., Gently massage the jelly or lotion into the affected area. Once you feel the bond of the glue start to break, pull the attached skin apart. Gently peel the remaining glue from your skin.








Do not force the glue from your skin. This is dangerous and can cause damage.
 Thoroughly wash your hands with soap and warm water to wash away any residual glue. Pat your hands dry.
