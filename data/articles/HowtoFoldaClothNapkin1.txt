 With the napkin laid out face-down in front of you, fold the top corners down to the bottom corners (fold the napkin in half, towards you).;
, Fold it in half again to form quarters.
 With the napkin oriented so that the corner with all of the open corners is at the top-left, fold down the first layer until it is at the opposite corner.
 Turn the napkin over carefully so that the diagonal is going from the top left to the bottom right.
 Fold the right third of the napkin over and then the left third over to cover it.
 Tuck the left third into the triangle at the bottom of the right third fold, such that it is held firmly in place.
 Torn the packet back over and you will find that you have a nice pouch which you can place silverware in!
