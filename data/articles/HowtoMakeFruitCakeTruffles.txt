For serving: Line a plate or tray with foil and arrange as wished. Keep refrigerated until serving time, to firm them completely.
For gift: Line a box or container with wax paper, baking paper or foil. Arrange the truffles in one layer. If adding another layer, place another layer of paper/foil to prevent sticking.
