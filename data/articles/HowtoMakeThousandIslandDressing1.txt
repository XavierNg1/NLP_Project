;
, Blend all of the ingredients together to form a smooth, pinkish sauce. Add up to 2 tablespoons of water, to thin to the desired consistency. Add salt to taste.
 Place the dressing in a sealed container and refrigerate for at least an hour, stirring occasionally. This time allows for the sugar to dissolve and the flavors to blend.
 Thousand island dressing makes a wonderful accompaniment to crisp lettuce, freshly sliced tomatoes or a seafood salad. The dressing will keep, covered in the refrigerator, for up to one week.
