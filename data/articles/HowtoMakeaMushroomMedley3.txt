 Turn the stove to medium-high heat. Heat the oriental sesame oil in a skillet. Put the bell pepper, ginger, and garlic into the skillet. Sauté them for one minute., Add in all of the mushrooms (shitake, button, and oyster). Pour in 2 to 3 tablespoons (29.6 mL to 44.4 mL) of water if you are beginning with a dry mixture. Sauté them until they turn brown and become tender. This will take about 10 minutes., Add in the spinach and onions once the mushrooms have turned brown and tender. Cook until the spinach begins to wilt. This should take about 2 minutes., Add in 1 tablespoon (14.8 mL) of soy sauce. Then, sprinkle some salt and pepper over the medley. Use a stirring utensil to mix all of the ingredients together thoroughly., Transfer the finished medley to a bowl or serving dish. You can either squeeze a lemon wedge over the entirety of the medley, or allow each person to have the option of adding lemon juice. Serve while warm.
