, Put both bags of Oreos on a hard surface, such as a kitchen counter, and run the roller over both bags continuously. Keep this up until the Oreos are completely crushed, making them look like "sand."

, container of cream cheese and pour it all into a small microwave safe bowl. Microwave the bowl of cream cheese for 2-3 minutes. If the cream cheese is still not softened, microwave it for an addition 1-2 minutes.
 Take the entire 8 oz. Cool Whip container, the 6 ounces of softened cream cheese, the package of cheesecake pudding mix, ½ of a cup of milk and a teaspoon of vanilla extract and add them all into the mixer bowl. Once all the ingredients are in the mixer, turn the mixer up to high power and mix for 3-5 minutes.
 Take the pale (optional) or a large bowl and layer the crushed Oreos onto the bottom of the pale. Use about ¼ of the crushed Oreos for the first layer.
 Take ¼ of the pudding mixture and layer that on top of the first layer of Oreo crumbs.
 Do this until both the Oreos and pudding are completely gone. Make sure to end with the crushed Oreos on top of the pale/bowl to give the appearance of sand on the top of the cake.
 Take plastic food wrap and cover the top of the pale/bowl. Place if a refrigerator for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes have passed, the cake is ready to be served!

, Take candy sea creatures and place them on top of the final layer of crushed Oreos to give the cake more of a beach feel.
