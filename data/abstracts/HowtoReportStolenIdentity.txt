
Keep a log.As soon as you suspect that your identity has been stolen, start taking notes to keep track of necessary information.
Place a fraud alert on your credit file.There are three nationwide consumer reporting companies you can contact: TransUnion, Equifax, and Experian.
Review your credit report.
Close or suspend suspect accounts.
Contact other affected agencies.
File a local report with the police.
Fill out an Identity Theft Victim's Complaint and Affidavit with the Federal Trade Commission.Filing a federal complaint will allow law enforcement throughout the country to look for the criminal responsible for the theft of your identity.
