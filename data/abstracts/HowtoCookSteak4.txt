
Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a cast-iron skillet over high heat until heavily smoking.
Add the steak to the pan, paying attention to placement if pan has raised ridges.

,
Flip frequently, about every minute or so, for about 6 to 12 minutes, until desired internal temperature has been reached.
Before doneness has been reached, add 2 tablespoons of butter and any additional aromatics.Aromatics you may use while pan-searing may include:


Rosemary
Thyme
Marjoram
Garlic
Sage

,
After steak is cooked (steak will continue to cook off the heat), allow it to rest for at least five minutes.
Enjoy.
