
Get your ingredients.
Sprinkle yeast over warm water in a large bowl.
Stir in the salt and cold water.
Knead dough.
Let the dough rise.
Preheat your oven.
Dust your dough with flour.
Top your pizza.
Place your pizza on the stone.
Bake your pizza.
Cut and enjoy.
Let the stone cool.
Use a soft brush, soap, and water.
Dry your stone.
Finished.
