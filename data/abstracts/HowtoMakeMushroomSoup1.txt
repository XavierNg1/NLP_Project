
Prepare the onion, mushrooms and thyme.
Cook the onions over medium heat.
Add the mushrooms and cook for five minutes.
Sprinkle in the thyme and cook for ten minutes.
Add the bay leaf, salt and soy sauce.
Combine the tapioca starch and vegetable broth.
Add the broth mixture and the almond milk to the soup.
Cook for fifteen minutes.
Garnish and serve.
