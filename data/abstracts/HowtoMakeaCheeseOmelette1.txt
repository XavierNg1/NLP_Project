
Break two eggs into a bowl.
Beat the eggs with a fork or whisk.
Butter an 8-inch (20.32-centimeter) skillet.
Pour the egg mix onto the skillet.
Continue spreading the egg around with a spatula.
Add the cheese just before the omelette finishes cooking.
Fold the omelet in half.
Finish cooking the omelette, then transfer it to a plate.
Garnish and serve the omelette.
