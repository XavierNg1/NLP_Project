
Go to https://www.bitmoji.com/slack/ in a web browser.
Click Add to Slack.
Click Authorize.
Type /bitmoji into a Slack channel and press ↵ Enter or ⏎ Return.
Click Create Your Avatar.
Sign up for Bitmoji.
Create your Bitmoji.
Add Bitmoji to the chat.Type /bitmoji to add a Bitmoji (of just you) to the chat.
