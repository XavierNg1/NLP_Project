
Fill out your application for a Free Application for Federal Student Aid, if you are a woman looking to go to school or return to school.
Apply for the Federal Pell Grant.
Call the Financial Aid office at the school where you plan to attend.
Go to the US Department of Education website at ed.gov/fund/grants-apply.html.
Contact your state's Department of Higher Education.
