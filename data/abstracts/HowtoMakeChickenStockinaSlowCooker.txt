
Chop the onions into quarters.
Slice the garlic bulb lengthwise.
Slice the celery and carrots into pieces.
Place the chicken in the slow cooker.
Toss the vegetables and herbs around the chicken.
Fill the slow cooker with water.
Cook the stock for at least 8 hours.
Place the large pieces from the stock in the strainer.
Pour the rest of the stock through the strainer.
Divide the stock into smaller containers and allow to cool.
Store the stock in the refrigerator.
Finished.
