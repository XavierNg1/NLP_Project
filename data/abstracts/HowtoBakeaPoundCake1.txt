
Preheat your oven to 300ºF (150ºC).
Measure out the dry ingredients.
Break each egg and put them in a separate bowl.
Cream the butter in a large bowl.
Add the egg yolks (beaten until thick and lemon-colored), flour, mace, and brandy.
Beat vigorously for five minutes.
Pour batter into your pan(s) and place in the oven.
Top as desired.
