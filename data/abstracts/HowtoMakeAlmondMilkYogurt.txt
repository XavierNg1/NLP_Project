
Make the almond milk.
Add culture starter to the milk.
Ferment the almond yogurt mixture.
Place the fermented almond yogurt into the refrigerator to slow down fermentation.
Place a piece of cheesecloth over a mason jar opening and put on the outer lid to keep the cheesecloth in place.

,
Pour the fermented almond yogurt over the cheesecloth to allow the yogurt to strain into the jar.
