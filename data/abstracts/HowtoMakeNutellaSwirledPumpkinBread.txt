
Prepare to make the bread.
Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Beat the wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
Pour the dry ingredients in with the wet.
Slowly pour the batter on the loaf pan.
Heat up the Nutella in a microwave for about 15 seconds to soften.
Swirl the Nutella around on the batter using a small knife.Bake the bread for about an hour, until the bread rises and the edges are golden brown.Have the loaf cool on a wire rack for about ten minutes.
Serve.
