
Add the flour, yeast, sugar, salt, eggs, milk, and butter into the bowl of your mixer.
Turn off the mixer.
Knead the dough.
Make the filling.
Preheat the oven to 190°C/ 375ºF.
,
Roll the dough.
Spread the filling on the dough.
Place the sliced rolls on the greased baking tray or sheet, leaving enough space for the rolls to rise.
Add the egg wash. Take the egg wash and using the pastry brush, paint each roll individually.

,
Put the tray into the oven.
Let the rolls rest for 10 - 15 minutes to cool.
