
Tell an interesting story.Your stories could be humorous or serious.
Express an emotional opinion.
Mention something enjoyable.If you’re at a wedding, for instance, you might mention to the other wedding guests, “This cake is terrific,” or “The ceremony was so lovely.” Saying something positive will inspire others to reply with their own opinion on the subject.
Steer the conversation toward the other person.
Compliment your conversation partner.Talking about the weather is cliche and not very interesting, but there are many equally mundane things you could talk about that make for interesting conversation.For instance, you could ask your conversation partner about his or her wardrobe.
Don’t try to brag or show how great you are.
