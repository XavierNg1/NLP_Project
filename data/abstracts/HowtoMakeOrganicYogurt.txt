
Gradually heat the milk.
Remove the warm milk from the stove.
Run the milk through a strainer.
Remove 1/2 cup (125 ml) of the still-warm milk.
Add the powdered organic fiber to the separated portion of milk.
Add the treated milk back into the original batch.
Remove another 1/2 cup (125 ml) of milk.
Inoculate the separated milk.
Add the separated milk back to the rest.
Pour the milk into clean glass jars.
Store the jars in a warm location.
Wrap the jars in towels.
Let the jars incubate.
Strain the yogurt through cheesecloth.
Mix in the honey, berries and granola, if desired.
Store the yogurt in the refrigerator.
