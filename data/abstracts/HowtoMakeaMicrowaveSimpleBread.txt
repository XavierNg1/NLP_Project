
Combine the ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
Mix in a teaspoon each of baking powder and vanilla.
Add one egg.
Add 2-3 tablespoons of milk.
Add 2 tablespoons of water.
Add 2-3 tablespoons of oil.
Knead the dough.
Shape the dough.
Place the dough onto a microwave-safe dish.
Microwave for 5 minutes on a high heat.
Finished.
