
Turn on the oven and prepare a pan.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Whisk the egg whites and add the sugar.
Mix in the egg yolks.
Stir in half of the dry ingredients.
Stir in the milk and vanilla extract.
Add the rest of the dry ingredients.
Bake the chocolate cake.
Combine the milk mixture.
Poke the cooled cake.
Pour the milk mixture over the cake.
Chill the cake.
Finely chop the milk chocolate.
Heat the cream and water.
Pour the liquids over the chocolate.
Chill the chocolate cream.
Whip the milk chocolate whipped cream.
Frost the cake.
