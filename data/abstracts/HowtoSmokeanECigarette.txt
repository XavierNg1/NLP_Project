
Buy an e-cigarette kit.
Charge the battery for the e-cigarette.
Screw in the liquid cartridge.
Be aware of the difference between smoking a cigarette and smoking an e-cigarette.
Do a “primer puff”.
Draw slowly and steadily on the e-cigarette.
