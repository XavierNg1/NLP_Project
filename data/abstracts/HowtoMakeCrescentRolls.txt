
Whisk together the dry ingredients.
Cut the butter.
Mix the sliced butter into the dry ingredients.
Add the ricotta cheese.
Knead the dough.
Split the dough in half.
Roll the dough into a rectangle.
Fold each rectangle into thirds.
Re-roll and re-fold each rectangle.
Wrap and refrigerate.
Remove the dough from the refrigerator.
Cut both dough rectangles in half.
Form the square of dough into a rough circle.
Use a pizza cutter to slice the dough.
Form crescents.
Repeat the shaping process with the remaining dough.
Place the rolls on a baking sheet.
Let the dough rise.
Bake the rolls in a preheated oven.
Finished.
