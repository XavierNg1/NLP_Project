
Place ½ cup (115 grams) of finely-ground coffee into a medium-sized mixing bowl.
Add ½ cup (115 grams) of white sugar.
Add the ¼ cup (60 milliliters) of coconut oil or olive oil.
Add some fragrance with vanilla extract and/or ground cinnamon, if desired.
Mix the ingredients together using a fork, then make any necessary adjustments.
Transfer the scrub to an airtight jar and store it in a cool, dry place.
Use the scrub once to twice a week on your arms and legs.Hop into the bath or shower and get your skin wet.
