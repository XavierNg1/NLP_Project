
Be aware of the charges against you.
Attend all hearings and meetings.
Talk with your attorney.
Get the Federal Sentencing Guidelines Manual.
Look up your "Crime Group" in Chapter 2.
Find your "Base Point Number".
Check for any "Point Adjustments" as listed in Chapter Three.
Calculate your “Criminal History and Criminal Livelihood” points.
Use the Federal Sentencing Table to determine your length of time in prison.
Read the pre-sentencing investigation report.
Anticipate victim impact statements.
Exercise your rights to allocution.
Ask people to testify about your good character and attempts at rehabilitation at your sentencing hearing.
Remember to credit time served.
