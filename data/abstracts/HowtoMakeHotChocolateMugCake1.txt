
Get a large, ceramic mug.
Add the dry ingredients into the mug.
Add the wet ingredients.
Stir everything together.
Microwave for 40 to 50 seconds.If you have a weaker microwave, cook the cake for 60 seconds instead.
Allow the cake to cool for 5 minutes.Use a pot holder to take the mug out of the microwave.
Garnish the cake with mini marshmallows or a dollop of whipped cream.
