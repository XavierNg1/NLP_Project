
Mix together in a large bowl, mix 1 pound of ricotta cheese with 1 slightly beaten egg and 1 teaspoon salt.
Turn the dough onto a floured board.
Break off pieces of the dough.
Cut each rope into 1⁄2 inch (1.3 cm) pieces.
Continue until all the gnocchi dough is used.

,
Lay each gnocchi on waxed paper on a cookie sheet or flat pan that will fit in your freezer.
Wait for 15 minutes.
Serve immediately.
