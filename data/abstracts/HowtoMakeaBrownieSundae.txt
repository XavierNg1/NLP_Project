
Melt the chocolate with butter over low heat in a medium saucepan.
Add boiling water to melted chocolate and blend well before adding corn syrup and sugar.
Simmer until mixture thickens slightly which should take about nine minutes.
Make your favorite brownie recipe and let the brownies cool.
Top the ice cream and brownie with the warm Fudge Rum Sauce or any favorite warm fudge sauce.
Top the whole dessert with whipped cream and a maraschino cherry.

Serve.
