
Mix the ground flaxseed and water.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Mix the onion, coconut milk and flaxseed.
Combine the wet and dry ingredients and refrigerate the batter.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (204.4 C).
Mix the coating ingredients.
Form the batter into balls and coat with the coating mixture.
Bake the hush puppies for 15 minutes.
Serve the hush puppies while warm!
