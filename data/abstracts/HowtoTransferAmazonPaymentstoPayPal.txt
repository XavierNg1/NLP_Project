
Sign into your Amazon seller account.
Access the “Seller Account Information” section.
Select the country location of the bank using the drop-down list next to “Bank Location Country.” Type the name of bank in the required field.
Enter your bank “Routing number” and “Account number” in the required fields.
Submit the information.
Log into your PayPal account to connect your US bank account as a payee.
Add your bank account details.
Submit your information.
Go to your Amazon payment account page at https://payments.amazon.com.
Click on “Withdraw funds” under the “My account” tab.
Choose the “Bank account” option.
Enter the amount to withdraw in provided field.
Click on “Confirm” to transfer your money to your bank account.
Transfer the money from your US bank account to your PayPal account.
