
Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C/Gas Mark 4).
Mix together the dry ingredients, except for the sugar.
Cream the butter and the sugars together.
Mix in the eggs gradually, then add the milk and vanilla extract.
Slowly add the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
Pour the batter into the prepared muffin tin.
Bake the cupcakes for 18 to 20 minutes, then let them cool.
Cut a well into the middle of each cupcake.
Fill the wells with mini M&M's.
Prepare the icing.
Pipe the frosting onto the cupcakes.
