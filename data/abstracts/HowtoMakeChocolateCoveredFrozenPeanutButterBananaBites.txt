
Prepare the baking pan or sheet.
Slice the bananas into evenly sized slices.
Spread peanut butter on half of the banana slices.
Place the non-buttered pieces onto the buttered banana pieces.
Arrange the sandwiched banana and peanut butter bites back on the pan or sheet.
Remove from the freezer.
Dip the frozen banana and peanut butter bites in the melted chocolate.
Return the covered banana bites to the freezer.
Remove the bites when set.
