Periodised training


Despite some training programs which focus on overall conditioning with no periodization, it is better to focus on training only some competences at different stages, so you can prioritize your work and improve that area in less time.
Train intense


Think about training in less time so you can rest more and allowing your body to recover and therefore improving faster so you can achieve your results fast.High intensity interval training has been proven to be very effective for training MMA.
