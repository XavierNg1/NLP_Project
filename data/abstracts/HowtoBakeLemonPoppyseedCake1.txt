
Preheat the oven.
Prepare the pan.
Combine the all purpose flour, cake flour, baking powder, and salt.
Mix together the butter and granulated sugar in a separate bowl.
Add the eggs to the butter and sugar.
Add the dry ingredients and milk.
Fold in the lemon zest, poppy seeds, and lemon extract.
Place the batter in the prepared cake pan.
Bake the cake.
Cool the cake.
Make the icing.
Glaze and serve the cake.
