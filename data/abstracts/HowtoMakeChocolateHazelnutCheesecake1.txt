
Make the crust and preheat the oven.
Press the crust into the pan and bake it.
Make a ganache and adjust the oven temperature.
Beat the cream cheese with the sugar.
Add the cornstarch, hazelnut liqueur, and eggs.
Combine the ganache and the cheesecake filling.
Spread the filling in the crust and bake the cheesecake.
Serve the chocolate hazelnut cheesecake.
