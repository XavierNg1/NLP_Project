
Preheat the oven and prepare your baking pan.
Melt the butter and chocolate.
Beat a wet brownie batter.
Whisk the dry ingredients and add them to the wet brownie batter.
Bake the brownies.
Soften the frosting ingredients.
Beat the cream cheese and butter.
Add the powdered sugar and vanilla.
Cut the brownies into triangles.
Insert a candy cane into the base of each tree brownie.
Consider tinting the frosting green.
Frost the brownie trees with cream cheese frosting.
Decorate the brownie Christmas trees.
