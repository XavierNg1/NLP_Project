
Soak the kokum in water.
Heat the oil in a pan.
Add the mustard seed and fenugreek, and cook for several minutes.
Mix the shallots, chillies, curry leaves, and garlic and ginger paste into the pan.
Stir in the chili powder, coriander, and turmeric, and saute until golden brown.
Add the kokum, some water, and salt to the pan, and bring to a boil.
Mix in the fish and more water if necessary.
Simmer the mixture until the fish is cooked.
Stir well to ensure all of the ingredients are mixed.
Remove the curry from the heat and add coconut oil.
Garnish with curry leaves and serve.
