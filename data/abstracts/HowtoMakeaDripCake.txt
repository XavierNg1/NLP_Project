
Bake a layer cake.
Ice the cake.
Refrigerate the iced cake.
Decide on a color.
Place the chocolate and heavy cream in a microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave the chocolate and cream for 60 seconds.
Microwave for additional 30-second segments.
Add food coloring.
Take the chilled cake out of the refrigerator.
Use a spoon to drip the glaze on the sides of the cake.
Fill in the top of the cake with the remaining glaze.
Decorate the cake.
