Peel onion and slice in half.
Wash chicken, butterfly (meaning if the chicken is too thick, cut the thickness in half) then cut into 1 inch width sized strips.Cut the garlic and add it to the bowl with the chicken.

Break all the eggs into a bowl and add a small amount of milk and the pinch of salt.
Starting on a high flame, add canola oil to a pan and let it heat.
Remove bacon from packaging, keep together and slice according to the picture, afterward place in bowl.
Add the ketchup, the white vinegar, the soy sauce, the Worcester sauce, the garlic and the pineapple juice in a bowl.
