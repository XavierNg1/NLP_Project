
Cream the butter and brown sugar.
Pour in the vanilla extract and salt.
Stir in the flour.
Combine in the milk.
Fold in the mini chocolate chips.

,
Scoop the cookie dough onto a flat surface.
Roll the cookie dough into small balls.
Carefully open the Oreos.
Take a ball of cookie dough and use your hand to gently press on it and flatten it slightly.

,
Place the flattened ball of cookie dough into the center of the Oreo.

,
Press the other half of the Oreo to the cookie dough.
Repeat this process for each cookie you want to make.

,
Serve the cookies.
