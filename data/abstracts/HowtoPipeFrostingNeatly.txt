
Learn the right consistency and recipe for your frosting.
Choose gel paste food coloring instead of liquid food coloring.
Select pastry bags with couplings.
Let the cake or cookie cool off before icing.
Create a smooth base.
Chill the icing between coats until harden (fifteen minutes).
Insert the tip to the bottom of the bag.
Fill the pastry bag with the icing.
Press the top of the pastry closed with one hand by gathering the top edges.
Twist the top of the bag to close and to maintain pressure.
Practice the strokes and learn the correct amount of pressure to use.
Use a toothpick to map out a specific design on your base coat of icing.
Imprint a reference design using a cookie cutter.
Chill the frosting in between applications of different designs or different colors.
