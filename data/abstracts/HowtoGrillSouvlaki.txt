
Combine the marinade.
Trim and cut the pork loin.
Marinate the pork for at least two hours.
Combine and chill the tzatziki.
Soak the wooden skewers in water.
Prepare the grill.
Thread the pork onto the skewers.
Grill the souvlaki for 8 to 12 minutes.
Rest the souvlaki.
Grill the pita for 1 to 2 minutes.
Assemble the souvlaki.
