
Activate the yeast.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Beat the egg.
Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ones.
Add water.
Begin to knead the dough.
Soften the butter and add it to your dough.
Continue kneading your dough to evenly distribute the butter.
Let the dough rest.
Divide the dough.
Let the dough balls rest.
Make your egg wash.
Bake the dough.
Remove and let cool.
