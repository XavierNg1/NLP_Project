
Create a Twitter account for your business.
Use your Twitter link on all marketing materials, including business cards, letterhead, website, Facebook account, invoices and all other online or printed promotions.
Master the use of the hashtag.
Find people to follow by using a Twitter directory like twellow or Justtweetit.
Avoid hard sales tactics.
Encourage employees to communicate with the company Twitter account using their personal accounts or specially-made company Twitter accounts.
Announce all new events or products on Twitter, using product shots and details.
Use Twitter-based contests to promote your product.
Follow the "Trending Topics" on Twitter and use these common hashtags when they apply to you.
Employ a social media service, if you are unable to do all of this promotion within your company.
Buy a Twitter Promoted Tweet to try to target a new market.
