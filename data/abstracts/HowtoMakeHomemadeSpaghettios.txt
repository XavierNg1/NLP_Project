
Melt some of the butter in a saucepan.
Add the garlic and onion and saute.
Mix in the tomato paste and cook for several minutes.
Stir in the rest of the butter, tomato puree, spices, and water.
Cook the sauce until it thickens.
Combine the salt, pepper, and ground beef.
Roll the meat into balls.
Place the meatballs in the sauce and heat until they’re cooked through.
Bring a pot of water to a boil.
Add the pasta and cook until it’s al dente.
Drain the pasta.
Add the pasta and the grated cheese to the saucepan and mix.
Spoon the pasta into bowls and serve.
