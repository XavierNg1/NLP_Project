
Rinse 2-3 cups of poha in water and let it soak for 3-4 minutes.
Cook one cup of diced potatoes for two minutes in the microwave.
Drain the water from the rice.
Heat 1 tsp of oil on medium in a large wok or saucepan.
Add 1 tsp mustard seeds to the oil until the crackle.
Add the diced onions, green chilies, and partially cooked potato.
Add the four curry leaves, spices, 1/2 cup peanuts, and 1/2 tsp sugar.
Add the rice and stir well.
Garnish with cilantro and lemon juice and serve hot.
