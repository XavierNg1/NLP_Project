
Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C) and grease a doughnut pan.
Mix the dry ingredients together, except for the sugar.
Combine the sugar with the wet ingredients.
Gradually add the flour mixture into the sugar mixture.
Transfer half of the batter into a piping bag.Leave the rest of the batter in the bowl.
Stir in the melted chocolate, cocoa powder, and remaining buttermilk.
Transfer the chocolate batter into a separate piping bag.
Fill the left side of each cavity with the chocolate batter.
Fill the right side of each cavity with the plain batter.
Swirl the batters together with a toothpick.
Bake the doughnuts for 10 to 11 minutes.Let them cool for 5 minutes in the pan before turning them out onto a wire cooling rack.
Prepare the glaze.
Dip the doughnuts into the icing.
Let the icing set 1 hour before serving.
