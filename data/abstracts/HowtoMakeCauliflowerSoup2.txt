
Prepare the vegetables.
Put the vegetables into a large saucepan, then add the water and bouillon.
Bring the mixture to a boil, then simmer it for 12 to 15 minutes.
Melt the butter in a separate saucepan.
Stir in the flour, salt, and pepper.
Slowly pour in the milk while stirring with a whisk.
Boil over medium heat for 2 minutes.
Reduce the heat, then stir in the Cheddar cheese.
Slowly stir the milk mixture into the vegetable mixture.
Serve the soup while it is still hot.
