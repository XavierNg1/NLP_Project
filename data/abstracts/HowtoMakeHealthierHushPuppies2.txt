
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (176.6 C).
Cut the squash into cubes.
Boil the squash for 10 minutes.
Mix the cornmeal, sugar, salt and pepper.
Combine the milk, eggs, butter, diced onion and jalapeno.
Drain the squash and add it to the egg mixture.
Puree the squash and other ingredients.
Form the batter into balls and bake them for 30 minutes.
Serve while hot.
