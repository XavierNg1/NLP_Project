
Decide if you will file for divorce alone or if you and your spouse will file jointly.
Obtain the proper forms.
Fill out and sign the forms.
Determine the appropriate Court in which to file your forms.
File the divorce forms with the Court.
Serve your spouse.
Read all notices and correspondence from the Court.
Provide your spouse with required financial documents.
Complete the remaining divorce forms.
Attend the Status Conference, if required.
Attend mediation, if required.
Schedule a hearing.
Prepare for your hearing.
Attend your hearing.
