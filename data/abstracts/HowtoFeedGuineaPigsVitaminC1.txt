
Don’t rely on hay and grass alone.
Choose a guinea pig food pellet that includes Vitamin C. Premium foods designed for guinea pigs will include Vitamin C, which is added during the manufacturing process.Don’t store the food pellets for more than a month.Vitamin C degrades over time.
Add leafy greens to your guinea pig’s diet.
Feed fruits and other vegetables to your guinea pig for a treat.Peppers, guava, broccoli, cauliflower, strawberry, peas, tomatoes, and kiwi are good sources of Vitamin C for guinea pigs.
Never give your guinea pig poisonous foods.
