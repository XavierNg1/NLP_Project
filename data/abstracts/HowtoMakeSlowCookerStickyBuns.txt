
Grease the inside of the slow cooker.
Combine some of the melted butter and brown sugar.
Spread half of the butter and sugar mixture in the slow cooker.
Add half of the pecans.
Add the remaining butter and cinnamon sugar to separate bowls.
Dip each biscuit in the butter.
Roll each biscuit in the cinnamon sugar.
Place the coated biscuits in the slow cooker.
Pour the remaining butter and brown sugar mixture and pecans over the biscuits.
Cook the biscuits for a couple of hours.
Remove the slow cooker’s insert.
Run a knife along the inside to loosen the buns.
Turn the insert over  over a plate to free the buns.
