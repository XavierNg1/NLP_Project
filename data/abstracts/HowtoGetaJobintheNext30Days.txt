Do The Job During The Interview.
If you are not getting interviews then your resume does not match the job description.
If you are not getting job offers then you have not yet warmed up to the interview process.
I take a manilla folder with blank paper in it to the interview and I write down the interview questions.
There are two types of interview questions, the technical and the Human-resource question.
Most jobs are slightly different.
