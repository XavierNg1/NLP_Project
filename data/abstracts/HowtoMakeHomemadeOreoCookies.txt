
Preheat oven to 350 °F (177 °C).
Combine the dry ingredients (except sugar) in a large bowl.
Mix the butter and sugar in a large bowl.
Add the eggs.
Scoop in dry ingredients with the mixer running.
Line a cookie pan with parchment paper.
Scoop the dough onto the parchment paper.
Flatten the cookie dough balls.
Bake the cookies for 15 to 17 minutes.
Add butter and powdered sugar to a large bowl.
Use hand mixer to combine butter and sugar.
Pour in 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract.
Wait for the cookies to cool.
Spoon about a tablespoon of frosting onto each cookie.
Place another cookie on top of the frosting.
Enjoy your homemade Oreos!
