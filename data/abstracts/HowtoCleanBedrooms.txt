
Start with the bed.
Remove everything from the bed that is still on it.
Dust the headboard.
Clean the bedside table or nightstand.
Sort out your wardrobe next.
Check for mildew, dust and fluff balls.
Vacuum or sweep the base of the wardrobe.
Deal with the clothing.
Go around the room and collect everything that's out of place.
Check the windows, curtains, and blinds.
Dust the room.
Vacuum or sweep the room.
Clean the desk, if relevant.
Use furniture cleaner as required.
Retrieve the washed and dried sheets or use a set from the linen cupboard.
Take all clothes out for washing.
Take out the garbage bag or bin.
Place something fresh and fragrant in the bedroom.
