
Bring your purchases to the self-checkout line.
Press the Start button on the touchscreen.
Confirm whether you brought your own bags.
Scan your purchases by lining up the bar-code (the series of black lines having varied thickness, on the label) with the red light of the scanner.
Place each scanned item onto the bagging platform immediately after you scan it.
When you are done, pay for your purchase.
Remove your items from the bagging platform.
