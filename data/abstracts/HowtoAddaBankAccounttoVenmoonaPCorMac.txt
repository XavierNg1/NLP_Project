
Point your browser to https://www.venmo.com.
Click Settings.
Click Banks & Cards.Click Edit payment methods.
Click Link Bank Account….
Search for your bank.
Enter your online banking login information.Click Continue.
Answer additional security questions.
Click the account you want to add.
Select options under “Use this bank for.”


Payment to friends when Venmo balance isn’t enough: This option gives Venmo permission to pay people with your bank account instead of your Venmo balance.
Click Save Settings.
