
Make a list of all the things you absolutely have to do during the day.
Similar to step one, make a list of things you do that need to be done.
Lastly, list all the things you would like to do, but are usually considered free time activities.
Go through and fill in the tiny details of all of your daily activities.
Now that you've listed all your daily activities, play around with what order would be most convenient for you to do them in.
After you're happy with the order in which you'll do things throughout your day, go ahead and assign the times of day you will do each activity in.
