
Preheat oven to 350ºF (180ºC).
Combine cake mix, 2 eggs, and 1/2 cup of vegetable oil in a large bowl.
Mix in chocolate chips or nuts if desired.
Spoon teaspoonfuls of the mixture onto cookie sheets, about a dozen cookies per sheet.
Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
Let cool and then take off cookie sheet using a spatula.
The recipe yields 2 to 3 dozen cookies.
