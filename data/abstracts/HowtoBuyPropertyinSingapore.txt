
Appoint a property agent.
Decide on a property.Based on your needs, the housing agent will search for suitable living spaces.
Option to Purchase / Offer to Purchase.Once you have your eyes on a property, you should prepare 1% of the purchase price as consideration in exchange for the Option to Purchase from the seller.
Complete the transaction.Leave things in the hands of your solicitor.
Apply for bank loan.As a foreigner earning an income in foreign currency, you can secure a loan from a Singaporean bank for up to a maximum of 80% of the purchase price.
File for property tax.You should file for property tax on an annual basis after gaining ownership of the property.
Let out the property.If you are purchasing the property as an investment, do take note of the rental yield of the unit.
