
Preheat the oven and prepare a baking pan.
Cream together the sugar and softened butter.Combine the two ingredients in a medium bowl and beat together using an electric hand mixer or stand mixer.
Add the eggs to the bowl one at a time.
Combine the dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
Gradually add the dry ingredients and the buttermilk to the egg mixture.
Mix in the sour cream, vanilla, and almond extract.
Pour the batter into your baking pan.
Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
Cool the cookie cake to room temperature.
Chill the cake.
Cut six large circles out of the chilled cake.
Repeat the cookie cutting process to cut out medium and small circles.
Frost all the cookie rounds.
Stack two same-sized cookie rounds together.
Stack differently sized cookie layers together.
Chill until ready to serve.
