
Gather your supplies.
Find a small, squat glass jar with a two part lid.
Use the metal disk part to trace a circle onto a sheet of scrapbook paper.
Cut the circle out using scissors and set it aside.
Fill the jar half way full with baking soda.
Add 10 to 20 drops of essential oil.
Close the jar.
Shake the jar to mix everything up.
Use a needle to poke holes into the paper.
Use your air freshener.
