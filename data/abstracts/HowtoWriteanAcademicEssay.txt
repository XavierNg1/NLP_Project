
Understand the question asked of you.
Examine the topic for your essay.
Research the topic.
Write an outline.
Read your rubric or question to help you figure out how many paragraphs the essay must contain, or how many pages it must be.
Write your thesis.
Write the first draft of the essay.
Divide your thoughts into paragraphs.
Try to follow a pattern of 'Claim' followed by 'Evidence'.
Write the second draft.
Use transitional phrases.
Edit the draft.
Write your final draft.
