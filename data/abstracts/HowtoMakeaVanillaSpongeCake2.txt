
Preheat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF.
Use an electric mixer to beat the ingredients.
Add the eggs one at a time.
Add the sifted flour, a half amount at a time.
Transfer the sponge cake batter to each pan.
Place the cake pans into the oven.
Remove from the oven.
Transfer the base half to a serving plate.
Serve.
