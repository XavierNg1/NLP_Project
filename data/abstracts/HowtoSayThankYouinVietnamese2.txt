
Smile when you receive a compliment.
Accept a smile instead of many common phrases.
Use other nonverbal expressions.
Avoid holding eye contact.
Say “you’re welcome.” In Vietnamese, the equivalent of the English phrase “you’re welcome” is synonymous with “there isn’t any problem.” Accordingly, say, “không có gì” to say “it’s nothing” or “không có chi” to say “you’re welcome” when someone thanks you.
