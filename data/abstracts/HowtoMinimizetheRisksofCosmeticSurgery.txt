
Approach cosmetic surgery as a serious medical procedure.
Make sure you fully understand the procedure/s you are considering.
Don’t be one of the first to try out a new technology.
Choose your plastic surgeon well.
Choose a qualified, board certified plastic surgeon.
Check whether the surgeon has hospital privileges.
Choose a plastic surgeon who is experienced in the specific cosmetic plastic surgery procedure you will have done.
Don’t be tempted by a low price tag.
Check the surgical facility and support staff.
Discuss your expectations and concerns with your plastic surgeon.
Make sure you are physically fit to have the surgery.
Tell your surgeon about any past and present medical conditions.
Diligently follow your surgeon's instructions before surgery.
Diligently follow your surgeon's instructions after surgery.
Avoid strenuous activity for several weeks after surgery.
Contact your surgeon immediately if you experience any post-operative complications.
