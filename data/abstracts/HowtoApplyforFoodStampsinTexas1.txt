Click on “Set up account,” and follow the on-screen instructions to create a username and password for the site.

Read and sign sections U through W on the SNAP application form.
Click on “Submit” at the end of the form to submit your online SNAP application to the Texas Health and Human Services Commission, which then forwards your information to the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA).
