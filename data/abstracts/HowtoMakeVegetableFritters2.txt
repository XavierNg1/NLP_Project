
Soak the chia seeds in water.
Peel and grate your vegetables.
Add in the rest of the ingredients.
Stir everything together with a rubber spatula.
Heat some oil in a large skillet.
Ladle the batter onto the skillet.
Fry the fritters for 4 to 6 minutes, turning once.
Place the finished fritters onto a wire cooling rack set atop a baking sheet.
Serve the fritters while they are still warm.
