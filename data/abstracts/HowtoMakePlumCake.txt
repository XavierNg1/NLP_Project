
Purchase some ripe plums.
Slice the plums in half.
Remove the pit of the plum.
Slice the plums.
Gather your baking ingredients.
Separate the egg whites from the egg yolks.
Beat the egg whites.
Cream the butter with the sugar.
Add the egg yolks and lemon zest to the butter mixture.
Sift the baking powder and flour into a bowl.
Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture.
Fold in the egg whites.
Preheat your oven.
Grease and flour a cake pan.
Pour in the batter.
Arrange the sliced plums.
Bake the cake for 40-45 minutes.
Place the cake on a pastry rack to cool.
Serve the plum cake.
