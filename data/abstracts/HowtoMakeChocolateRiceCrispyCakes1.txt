
Assemble a double boiler.
Melt the chocolate over low heat in the double boiler.
Add the butter and syrup into the chocolate.
Remove the bowl from the saucepan, then fold in the rice crispy cereal.
Pour the mixture into a greased baking pan.
Chill the rice crispy treats in the fridge for 1 to 2 hours.
Cut the rice crispy treats into squares, then serve them.
