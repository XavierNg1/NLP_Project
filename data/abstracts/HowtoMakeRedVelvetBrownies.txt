
Preheat the oven.
Line and grease the pan.
Melt the chocolate and butter in the microwave.
Combine the sugar with the chocolate mixture.
Mix in the eggs.
Add the flour.
Stir in the food coloring, baking powder, vanilla, and salt.
Transfer the batter to the prepared pan.
Bake the brownies until a toothpick comes out mostly clean.
Cool the brownies on a wire rack.
Combine the cream cheese and butter.
Mix in the powdered sugar and salt.
Stir in the vanilla.
Lift the brownies out of the pan.
Spread the frosting over the brownies and cut into squares.
