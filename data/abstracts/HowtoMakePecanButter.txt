
Buy pecans.
Gather your tools.
Gather other ingredients.
Toast the pecans in the oven or in a pan on the stove.
Pour the pecans in a food processor and blend.
Add a pinch of salt and a pinch of cinnamon to taste.
Add a touch of sugar, if desired.
Scoop the pecan butter into a jar, cover it, and store it in the refrigerator.
Make a pecan butter and jelly sandwich.
Add pecan butter to a smoothie.
Make pecan butter cookies.
Eat pecan butter with a spoon.
