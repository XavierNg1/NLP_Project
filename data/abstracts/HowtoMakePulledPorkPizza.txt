
Cook pork in a slow cooker.
Shred the pork.
Bake the pork with barbeque sauce.
Set it aside.
Sautee garlic and onions.
Add tomatoes and seasonings to the onion and garlic.
Bring the sauce to a boil over medium-high heat.
Preheat the oven.
Add sauce and toppings.
Bake the pizza.
Garnish with extras.
Serve the pizza.
