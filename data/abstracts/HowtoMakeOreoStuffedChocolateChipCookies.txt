
Make your cookie dough reach a nice consistency.
Spray your cookie tray with cooking spray.
Place the cookie dough on the tray.
Place an Oreo in the center of each chocolate chip cookie dough mound.
Place the second layer of cookie dough over the Oreo.
Bake the cookies for 10 minutes at 375ºF (190ºC).
Remove from the oven.
Serve.
