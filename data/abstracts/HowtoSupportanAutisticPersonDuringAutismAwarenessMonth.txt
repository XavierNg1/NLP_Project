
Prepare yourself first.
Limit general media exposure, particularly regarding autism awareness.
Celebrate Autism Acceptance Month instead.
Find media and gifts devoted to autism acceptance.
Paint autism in a neutral/positive light.
Listen to autistic adults.
Help them network with other autistic people.
Make it very clear that you accept them for who they are.
Set aside extra time to do things together.
Praise their strengths.
Engage with their special interests and therapy activities.
Keep an eye on their emotional state.
Offer plenty of reassurance.
