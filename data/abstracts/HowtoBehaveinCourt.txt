
Dress appropriately for your court appearance.
Let your friends know about any courtroom rules.
Know what time your hearing is and arrive early.
Be prepared to go through security.
Treat everyone you encounter with respect.
Listen to any instructions given by the bailiff or court attendant.
Wait quietly during the hearing until you are directed to speak.
Be aware of your body language during the hearing.
Do not speak unless directed to.
Stand up when it is your turn to speak.
Address the judge appropriately.
Answer questions clearly and carefully.
Speak in a respectful tone of voice, use polite language, and be aware of your body language.
Remain calm and collected during the hearing.
