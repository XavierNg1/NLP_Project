
Preheat the oven to 180°C/350ºF.
Stir the butter and salt together.
Add the sugar to the mixture.
Add the eggs to the mixture one at a time.
Mix in the self-rising flour.
Sprinkle a bit flour on a table or clean counter.
Roll the dough out.
Press cookie cutters into the dough to shape cookies.
Place the cookies in rows on the baking tray.
Sprinkle a little sugar over the cookies before placing them in the oven.

,
Put the baking tray in the preheated oven.
Remove from the oven once baked.
Serve when cooled.
