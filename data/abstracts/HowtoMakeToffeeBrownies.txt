
Preheat the oven to 160°C/Fan 140°C/Gas 3 and prepare the pan for the brownies.
Melt the chocolate with the butter on the stove.
Whisk the eggs and sugar together to form a thick and silky mixture.
When happy with your brownie batter, pour it into your lined cake tin.
Leave to cool for at least an hour after properly baked to firm the cake.
