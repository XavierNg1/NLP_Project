
Preheat the oven to 375°F/190°C.
In a skillet, fry the sirloin steak over medium heat with the butter.
Transfer the steak to a baking dish.
Reuse the same skillet for the cream sauce.
Add the cream sauce to the steak.
Bake the mixture.
Boil the pasta.
Drain the pasta completely and add it to a serving dish.

,
Place the sirloin steak on top of the pasta.
Enjoy this dish with toast or baguette.
