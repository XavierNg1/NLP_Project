
Preheat the oven and prepare the muffin tins.
Combine the dry ingredients and butterscotch chips.
Mix the wet ingredients.
Stir wet and dry ingredients together.
Pour the batter into the muffin tins.
Bake the cupcakes until the centers are set.
Allow the cupcakes to cool completely.
Melt the butter.
Cook the butter until it browns.
Mix in the brown sugar and bring the mixture to a boil.
Stir in the cream and return to a boil.
Remove the mixture from the heat and add the salt, vanilla, and cinnamon.
Allow the mixture to cool.
Add the powdered sugar and some milk.
Cover the cupcakes with the frosting.
