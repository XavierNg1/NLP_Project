
Choose a drop cookie recipe.
Grease the inside of one or two mini muffin tins with cooking spray.
Preheat the oven and prepare the cookie dough according to the recipe.
Scoop small balls of dough into the greased muffin tin.
Shape the balls.
Bake the cups for about 10 minutes.
Reshape the cups, if needed.
Remove the cups from the tin and allow them to cool.
Use a piping bag or a small spoon to fill the cups.
