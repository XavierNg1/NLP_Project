
Cook your vegan sausage according to package instructions.
Dice the onion and garlic.
Chop the potatoes.
Combine the sausage, onion, garlic, potatoes and vegetable broth.
Add the vegan bacon bits, the parsley, red pepper flakes, and black pepper.
Strip the kale leaves from their stems while the soup simmers.
Add the chopped kale and the almond milk to the soup.
Finished.
