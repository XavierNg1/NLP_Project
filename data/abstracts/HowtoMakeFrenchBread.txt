
Proof the yeast.
Place the remaining flour and the salt in a mixing bowl.
Add the proofed yeast.

,
Mix the dough and add water.
Stop mixing and let the dough rest.
Continue mixing.
Knead the dough.
Let the dough rise.
Punch down the dough.
Let the dough rise again.
Let the dough rise a third time.
Form batons or baguettes.
Put the shaped dough into a baking pan.
Let the dough rise a final time.
Preheat on the oven to 450 degrees.

,
Slit the loaves.
Put the loaves in the oven and add moisture.
After 10 minutes, turn the oven down to 350 degrees.
Bake the bread for 20 more minutes.
Remove from the oven and put on a cooling rack.
