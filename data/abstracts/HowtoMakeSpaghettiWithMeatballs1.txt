
Whip the eggs in a small bowl with a teaspoon of salt and your choice of seasonings.
Choose your ground meat and dump it into a big bowl.
Pour the egg, Parmesan, and bread crumbs in and mix thoroughly with your hands.
Roll the meat into 1-inch (2.5 cm) balls.
Fry the meatballs on the stovetop over medium heat until browned on all sides.
Alternatively, bake meatballs for 20-30 min at 350F (175C), turning once or twice.
