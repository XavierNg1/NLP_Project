
Determine if your employer's plan is one that allows a "roll over" to a Traditional IRA.
Choose a financial institution offering a Traditional IRA plan to receive your rolled-over retirement savings.
To choose the institution and plan right for you:


Obtain complete information on each plan you are considering.
Once you pick an administrator, begin the account set-up process.
Confirm with the administrator of your new IRA that they can receive rollover money.
Determine what type of roll over is best for you.
Advise the new administrator of your plans.
Complete the roll over forms and return them to your plan administrator or fill them out online.
