
Peel the potatoes and cut into evenly sized wedges or roasting chunks.
In a large heavy roasting pan, mix cut potatoes, oil, garlic, herbs, lemon and salt and pepper.
Pour the boiling water into the pan and place in a preheated 200C / 395F oven or barbecue.
After 25 minutes, check the potatoes.
Serve hot while still crispy.
