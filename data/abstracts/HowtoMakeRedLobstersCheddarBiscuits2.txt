
Preheat your oven to 475ºF (246ºC).
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper on a non-stick baking mat.
Whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, salt, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper together in a large bowl.
Stir the buttermilk and melted butter together in a medium bowl.
Add the buttermilk mixture to the flour mixture.
Place the dough on the baking sheet.
Bake the biscuits for 10-12 minutes.
Make the garlic butter mixture.
Brush the biscuits with the garlic butter mixture.
Serve.
