
Heat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (176.66 Celsius) and grease your baking pan.
Pour your red velvet cake mixture into the baking pan.
Bake the cake for 25 minutes.
Cool the cake for 20 minutes.
Poke holes across the top of the cake.
Pour condensed milk over the top of the cake.
Refrigerate the cake for two hours.
Spread your icing over the surface of the cake.
