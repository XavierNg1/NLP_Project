
Choose the container in which your starter will live.
Sterilize your container.
Add 30 grams each of flour and warm water to your container and mix vigorously.
Cover the container and let it sit in a warm place for 24 hours.

,
After 24 hours has passed, add another 30 grams each of flour and warm water (about 1/2 cup and 1/4 cup, respectively) to the container and mix vigorously.
Replace the cover and let it sit in a warm place for 24 hours.

,
Add 60 grams of flour and 60 grams of warm water, or 1 cup of flour and 1/2 cup of warm water, to the container, and mix as before.
Replace the cover and let it sit in a warm place for 24 hours.

,
At this point, you should have 2 cups of starter.
