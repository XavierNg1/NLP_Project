Take the saucepan off the burner when the milk reaches 180 degrees Fahrenheit (82.2 degrees Celsius).
Pour the heated milk into a glass or ceramic bowl that has a lid.
Turn on the oven light and allow it to start heating up the oven.
Combine the 2 tbsp.
Stir the milk and yogurt mixture into the cooled milk.
Check the oven to make sure it is between 100 and 110 degrees Fahrenheit (37.7 and 43.3 degrees Celsius).
Leave the yogurt in the oven under the steady heat for 8 to 12 hours.
Remove the bowl and check to see if it has thickened into yogurt.
Pour watery liquid off the top, if there is a large amount.
Line a metal strainer with 4 layers of cheesecloth.
Remove the yogurt from the refrigerator and pour it into the lined strainer.
Remove the yogurt from the refrigerator and pour out the whey from the bowl.
Remove the yogurt again after 1 hour.
Refrigerate your fresh Greek yogurt for up to 6 days.
