
Turn on the oven and prepare your pan.
Place the butter, light brown sugar, and dark brown sugar in a bowl.
Add the eggs.
Add the brandy, orange juice and zest, and lemon zest.
Fold in the ground almonds, mixed nuts, and fruits (candied and dried).
Place the flour, salt, and baking powder in a separate bowl.
Fold the dry ingredients into your wet batter.
Spread the batter in your pan.
Bake your fruitcake.
Reduce the oven temperature and continue baking.
Test the fruitcake and remove from the oven.
Let the fruitcake cool completely while it's still in the springform pan.
Remove the fruitcake and wrap.
Brush the fruitcake with brandy.
