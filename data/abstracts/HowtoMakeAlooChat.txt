
Boil, peel, and chop your baby potatoes.
Combine oil and dry seasonings into a bowl.
Add the potatoes to the mixing bowl.
Heat oil in a nonstick pan.
Pour your marinated potatoes into your pan.
Remove the potatoes from the heat.
Put your cooked potatoes into serving dishes.
Top your potatoes with the whisked curds.
Add your chutneys to the dish.
Sprinkle the remaining ingredients over your dish.
Top with your garnishes.
