
Get a large, microwave-safe mug.
Add the hot chocolate powder and flour into the mug.
Pour in the water, butter, and vanilla or peppermint extract.
Stir everything together to combine.
Add a handful of marshmallows on top.
Heat the brownie for 60 to 90 seconds.
Allow the brownie to cool.
Garnish the mug brownie, if desired.
