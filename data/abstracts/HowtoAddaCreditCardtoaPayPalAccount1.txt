
Download and open the “PayPal” app.
Tap the settings (gear) icon.
Tap “Banks and Cards”.
Add a new card.
Select “Debit or credit card” from the popup menu.
Enter your credit card information.
Tap the camera icon next to the credit card number field to take a photo of your card.
Align the box on screen with your credit card.
Select a billing address.
When all the correct information is entered, press “Link Card”.
Review your cards.
