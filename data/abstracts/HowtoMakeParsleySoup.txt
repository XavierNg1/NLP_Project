
Boil a large saucepan of water.
Cook the parsley for 1 minute.
Drain the parsley and set it in cold water.
Chop the parsley.
Melt the butter in a saucepan.
Add the garlic, onions, salt and pepper and cook until the onions are soft.
Stir in the stock and heavy cream and simmer until the mixture thickens.
Mix the parsley into the pan, and cook until warmed.
Pour the soup into a blender and puree with the lemon juice.
Strain the soup.
Place in bowls and serve.
