
Pour the heavy whipping cream into a mixer, and beat it on high until it turns solid, about 15 minutes.
Pour the mixture into a colander.
Squeeze the remaining liquid out of the butter.
Place the butter into a mixing bowl, and add the rest of the ingredients.
Beat the ingredients together until the butter is light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.
Transfer the butter into a sealable container.
Serve the butter at room temperature, or store it in the fridge for later enjoyment.
Finished.
