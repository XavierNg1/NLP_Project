
Assemble the ingredients listed above.
Make your desired brownie batter as usual.
Stick the peppermint brownie mix in the oven for the time it says on the box.
Once the brownies are done, take them out, using an oven mitt, and let cool for 5-7 minutes to set.
In the bowl, put one regular sized container of vanilla icing and about a teaspoon of peppermint extract.
Take the rest of the crushed peppermint/candy canes and sprinkle them onto the icing.
Place of all the finished, delicious peppermint brownies onto a decorative serving platter and enjoy with your family and friends!
