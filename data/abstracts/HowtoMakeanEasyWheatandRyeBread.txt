
Gather the ingredients and tools.
Dissolve the yeast in the water, in a large bowl.
Add the eggs, honey, and salt.
Add both flours and mix well, until dough starts to let go of the edges of the bowl.
Put the dough on a floured surface, dust your hands with flour, and start kneading.

.
Prepare the dough for rising.
Cover the four loaves with a towel.

Bake.
Have cool and serve.
