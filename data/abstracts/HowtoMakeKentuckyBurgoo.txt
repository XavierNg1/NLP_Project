
Heat your oil.
Salt the meats.
Add the meats to the pot.
Brown the meats on both sides.
Remove the meats and set aside.
Cook the onions, pepper, celery, and carrots.
Add the garlic.
Add back the meats.
Stir in broth and tomatoes.
Simmer for two hours.
Strip the meat from the bones.
Add the potatoes.
Stir in the worcester sauce.
Add the corn and lima beans.
Finish cooking your soup.
