
Preheat the oven and place a pizza stone on the top rack.
Roll the dough into a circle.
Mix some of the barbecue sauce with some olive oil, and brush the chicken.
Bake the chicken and cut into cubes when cool.
Spread the rest of the barbecue sauce on the dough, and add the chicken, cheeses, and red onion.
Slide the pizza onto the pizza stone and bake until the cheese melts.
