
Crack the eggs into the mixing bowl.
Add the sugar, beating it into the eggs.
Fold in the flour.
Sprinkle the work surface lightly with flour.
Divide then roll the dough into a sausage shape approximately 1.2cm/ 1/2" across.
Knot the biscuit dough.
Press both ends of the twist together firmly.
Fill the pot with water.
Once boiling, gently tip two to three of the biscuits into the water at a time.
Allow to boil for half a minute, then use a wooden spatula to free them from the base of the pan so that they can rise to the top.
After about five minutes of draining, transfer the cooled, drained biscuits to the cookie sheets.
Bake for 15 minutes.
Remove from the oven.
Serve.
