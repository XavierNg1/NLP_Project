
Use flaked coconut, not shredded coconut, for this recipe.
Take out a medium saucepan.
Brown the butter in the saucepan.
Pre heat the oven to 350 degrees F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper or a nonstick baking mat.

,
Scrape the chilled browned butter into a large mixing bowl.
Add 1/2 cup granulated sugar and 3/4 cup brown sugar to the butter.
Add the egg.
Take out another mixing bowl.
Pour half the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
Add 4 cups coconut flakes.
Use a 2-inch wide scoop to form the cookies.
Bake the cookies.
Cool the cookies on the baking sheet for 1-2 minutes.
