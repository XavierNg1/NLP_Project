
Crush the sandwich cookies with melted butter.
Assemble and chill the cookie crust.
Beat the cream cheese and sugar.
Dissolve the strawberry gelatin in milk.
Whisk the gelatin milk into the cream cheese mixture.
Fold in thawed whipped topping.
Fold in chopped strawberries.
Fill the crust and chill the pie.
Garnish and serve the creamy no-bake pie.
