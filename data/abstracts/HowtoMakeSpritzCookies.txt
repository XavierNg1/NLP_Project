
Preheat the oven to 350ºF/180ºC.
In a small bowl, combine the baking powder and flour.

,
In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar with an electric mixer or whisk until light and fluffy.

,
To the beaten butter mixture, add egg, milk, almond and vanilla extracts.
Slowly add the flour and baking powder mixture to the beaten mixture.
Fill the cookie press with dough.
Press the cookies onto the ungreased cookie sheet.

,
Place in the oven when you have a tray full.
Cool for 2 minutes on the cookie sheet sitting on the cooling rack.
Enjoy!,
If giving them as a gift, place them in a pretty box.
Finished.
