
Fill a medium sized pot with cold water.
Carefully place the spaghetti into the boiling water, making sure the spaghetti is completely covered with water.
Cook for 7-15 minutes, depending on your preference, the type of pasta and package directions.
Remove pot from heat.
Pour sauce into a small pan.
Allow to cook for up to 20 minutes, following package directions.
Remove from heat.
Place a frying pan on the stove on high heat.
Allow the butter to melt.

Remove pan from heat.
Serve and enjoy!
