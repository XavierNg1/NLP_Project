
Combine yeast and sour cream in a large mixing bowl.
Add the other ingredients in order.
Knead the dough for about 15 minutes.
Roll the dough into a ball.
Refrigerate the dough for at least 4 hours.
Meanwhile, make the filling.
Preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit.

,
Remove the dough from the refrigerator.
Roll each ball out to a rectangle measuring 10-by-6 inches (15.2 cm).
Spread 1 rounded tablespoon of the filling onto each rectangle.
Form each rectangle into a cylinder.
Slightly flatten each roll.
Cut the roll into 2 inch (5.1 cm) long slices.
Arrange the slices, pastry up, on the baking sheet.
Paint each nazook with the glaze mixture.

,
Bake for 15-25 minutes.
Allow the nazooks to cool.
