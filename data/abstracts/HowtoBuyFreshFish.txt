
Go to a reputable store or fishmonger.
Ask what is the freshest or check what the catch of the day is.

,
Don't be misguided by the term "fresh.",
Look for firm, shiny flesh.
Sniff the fish.
Check the eyes.
Check the gills.
Check cuts of fish.
On Fillets and steaks, look for flesh separation and gaps.Look for discoloration, brown or yellow edges, and a spongy consistency, these are all signs of aging fish.
