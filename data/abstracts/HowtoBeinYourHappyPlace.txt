
Find a physical place that is relaxing for you.
Make yourself comfortable.
Identify where your happy place is.
Prepare to use Progressive Muscle Relaxation.
Start the relaxation process by closing your eyes and tensing your toes on one foot.
Repeat this process with the toes on your other foot and then move up to your calves.
Recognize the power of the mind.
Begin visualizing by mentally seeing yourself in your happy place.
Incorporate your other senses into the visualization process.
Believe in the process.
