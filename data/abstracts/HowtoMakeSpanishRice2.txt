
Gather the ingredients for making spicy Spanish rice.
Turn on your grill to high.
Place the vegetables on the grill.
Char the vegetables, turning them frequently.
Dice the peppers once they’ve cooled.
Combine the peppers and corn together.
Heat the oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat.

,
Place 1 diced yellow onion, one peeled and diced chayote squash, and ½ diced yellow bell pepper in the onion.
Sauté the ingredients until they start to caramelize.

,
Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne to taste.

,
Stir in and sauté 6 minced garlic cloves and 2 cups of basmati rice for 3-4 minutes.

,
Add 5 cups chicken stock to the ingredients.

,
Cook the ingredients for 10 minutes.

,
Stir in the rest of the ingredients.
Cook these ingredients until the rice is tender.
Turn off the heat.

,
Garnish.
Serve.
