
Preheat the oven and grease a baking sheet.
Mix the flour, baking powder, basil, salt, and psyllium husk powder.
Combine the eggs, vinegar, oil, water, and zucchini.
Add the dry ingredients to the zucchini mixture.
Form the dough into balls and place on the baking sheet.
Brush the rolls with oil and bake them until golden brown.
Serve the rolls while still warm.
Preheat the oven and line a baking sheet.
Stir the tapioca flour, salt, and coconut flour together.
Mix in the oil and water.
Add the egg.
Roll the dough into balls and place them on a baking sheet.
Bake the rolls for 30 minutes.
Serve the rolls immediately.

Mix the ground chia seeds and water together.
Stir in the rest of the ingredients to form a thick dough.
Shape the dough into balls and place them on the baking sheet.
Bake the rolls until the bottoms are lightly golden.
