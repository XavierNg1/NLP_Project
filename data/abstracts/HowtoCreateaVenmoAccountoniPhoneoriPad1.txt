
Install Venmo from the App Store.
Open Venmo.
Click Sign up with Facebook.
Sign in to Facebook.
Tap OPEN.
Enter your personal information.
Enter a password and tap Next.
Enter your mobile phone number and tap Send Code.
Enter the code and tap Submit Code.
Enter a username and tap Done.
Tap OK to add your Facebook friends to your Venmo account.
Tap Next to access your contacts.
Select contacts and tap Send.
