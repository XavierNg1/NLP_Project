
Make the crust.
Form and chill the crust.
Beat the cream cheese and powdered sugar.
Whip the heavy whipping cream with vanilla.
Fold the whipped cream into the cream cheese mixture.
Chop the hazelnut chocolates and stir them into the filling.
Spread the filling in the crust and chill the cheesecake.
Drizzle the cheesecake with melted chocolate hazelnut spread.
