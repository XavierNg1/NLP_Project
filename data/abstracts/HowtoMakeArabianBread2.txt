
Place the yeast into a small bowl.
Add the flour, milk powder and salt to a large mixing bowl.
Pour the oil and yeast liquid into the dry ingredients, into the well.

,
Stir to combine.
Remove from the bowl and place on a floured surface.
Divide the dough into 18 even sized balls.
Gently warm up a heavy-based skillet.
Roll out a ball of dough to flatter it.
Remove the first pita bread and place on a plate.
Repeat with the remaining 17 balls of dough.

,
Serve warm.
