
Select a suitable pan for poaching.
Add the water.
If you'd like to help the eggs to set, add 5-10ml (1-2 teaspoons) of white vinegar to the water.
Select the eggs.
Poach at an easy pace.
Crack an egg into a ramekin, small bowl or soup ladle.
Turn down the gently boiling water to a simmer.
Spin the simmering water to cool down the water before you drop in the single egg.








,
Carefully lower or drop the egg into the center of the barely simmering whirlpool.
Wait 3-5 minutes until cooked.
If poaching multiple eggs at once, do not spin the boiling water.
Remove the poached egg with a slotted spoon.
Serve.
