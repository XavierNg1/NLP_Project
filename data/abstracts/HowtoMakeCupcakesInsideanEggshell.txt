
Remove egg from the shell.
Remove top of the eggshell.
Color or paint Eggshell.
Make cake batter.
Place cupcake liners in cupcake tin.
Add empty eggshell to each cupcake cup.
Fill each shell 2/3 way with cake batter.
Bake cupcakes according to package directions.
Top cooled cupcakes with frosting.
Add Peep candy to the top of each cupcake.
