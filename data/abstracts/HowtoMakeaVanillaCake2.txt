
Prepare to make the cake.
Cream the butter and sugar.
Pour the egg whites and vanilla extract.
Mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
Add ⅓ of the flour mixture to the batter.
Add ½ of the milk.
Continue the two steps, pouring the flour mixture and milk.
Pour the cake batter in a cake pan.
Bake the cake.
Let the cake cool.
Frost the cake.
Enjoy!
