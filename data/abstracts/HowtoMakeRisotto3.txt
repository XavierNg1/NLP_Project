
Make the broth mixture.
Melt 2 tbsp.
Add 1/4 cup chopped shallots to the pan.
Add 1/2 cup uncooked Arborio rice and 1/8 tsp.
Add 1 tbsp.
Stir in 1/2 cup of the broth mixture.
Add the rest of the broth mixture.
Stir in 1/2 cup of halved grape tomatoes.
Stir in the seafood.
Remove the seafood from the heat.
Serve.
