
Make sure the outdoor temperature is at least 95 °F (40 °C) before you try this project.
Remove any items that you may need from your car.
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Prepare the cookie dough if needed.
Place the pieces of unbaked cookie dough on the cookie sheet about 1 1/2 inches / 38mm apart.
Place the cookies on the baking sheet into the car.
Shut the doors and let the sun begin the baking.
Keep an eye on the baking process by looking through the windshield regularly.
Check now and then.
Remove the cookie sheet from your car.
Finished.
