
Mix the flours together.
Add the butter, cumin seeds, and salt.
Add a few drops of water.
Roll the dough into a ball.
Lay out a sheet of wax paper.
Screw the star attachment on the murukku mold.
Grease the murukku mold.
Fill the mold with dough.
Press the dough onto the wax paper.
Cover the murukku.
Place a pan of frying oil over medium heat.
Test the temperature of the oil.
Place the murukku in the oil.
Check for doneness.
Serve the murukku.
