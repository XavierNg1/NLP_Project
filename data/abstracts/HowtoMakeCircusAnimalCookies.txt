
Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
Cream the butter and sugars.
Mix in the egg, vanilla extract, and almond extract.
Add the flour mixture and mix until fully incorporated.
Divide the dough in half and form two discs.
Wrap the discs and chill until firm.
Preheat the oven and line a baking sheet.
Place one dough disc on floured surface and roll it out.
Use cookie cutters to cut the dough into animal shapes.
Transfer the cookies to the baking sheet.
Bake the cookies until they are lightly golden.
Cool the cookies on the tray briefly and then move to a cooling rack.
Repeat the process with the other dough disc.
Mix the powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and half and half.
Divide the icing between two bowls and add the food coloring to one.
Spread the icing over the cookies and add sprinkles.
Let the cookies sit until the icing sets.
