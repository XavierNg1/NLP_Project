
Roll the fondant flat.
Cut five to six circles.
Ruffle the edges using a veining tool.Place one circle on a piece of thin foam dusted with cornstarch.
Push one circle onto the tip of a bamboo skewer.
Crumple the flower around the end of the skewer.
Add the remaining circles.
Slide the flower off the skewer.
Fold the center petal in place using the veining tool.
