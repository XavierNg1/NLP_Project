
Preheat the oven and line a pan.
Mix the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter.
Press the graham cracker mixture into the pan and bake it until firm.
Beat the cream cheese.
Add in the yogurt, sugar, egg whites, and flour.
Stir in vanilla and coconut extracts.
Pour the filling over the crust and bake the mixture for 20 minutes.
Cover the pan with foil and bake for several more minutes.
Toast the almonds and coconut in a pan.
Remove the bars from the oven and let cool briefly.
Sprinkle the almonds and coconut over the bars and chill before cutting.
