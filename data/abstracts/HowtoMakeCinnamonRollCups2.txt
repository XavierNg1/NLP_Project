
Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C).
Beat the cream cheese until it is smooth.
Add in the vanilla extract and powdered sugar.
Take apart a cinnamon roll partway.
Press the cinnamon roll disk into the bottom of a muffin well.
Place a cinnamon roll strip into a muffin well to form a cup.
Spoon the cream cheese mixture into each cup.
Bake the cups for 18 to 20 minutes.
Let the cups cool before removing them.
Drizzle each cup with icing, if desired.
Serve the cups.
