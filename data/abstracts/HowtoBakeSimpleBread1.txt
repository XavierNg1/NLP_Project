
Proof the yeast.
Combine the flour and salt in a medium-sized bowl.

,
Add the yeast mixture to the flour mixture and stir to combine with a wooden spoon.

,
Sprinkle flour on your countertop or kitchen table.
Place the dough in an oiled bowl and cover it with a kitchen towel.
Knead the dough a second time, incorporating the olive oil.
Shape the dough into a loaf and place it in an oiled loaf pan.
Bake the bread for 40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown.
