
Preheat the oven to 350°F (176°C) and prepare your baking dish.
Cook the macaroni according to the directions on the package until it is just cooked.
Drain the macaroni and set it aside.
Prepare the roux in a large saucepan.
Whisk in the milk a little bit at a time.
Cook the milk mixture, covered, for about 6 minutes.You are ready for the next step when the milk mixture starts to bubble and thicken.

,
Stir in the shredded cheese, and cook until it is melted and smooth.The most popular cheese to use is cheddar, but you can also try other types of cheese as well, including sharp cheddar, fontina, gruyère, cheddar, and parmesan.You can even use a combination of different cheeses.

,
Add the macaroni and stir it again until the cheese sauce coats it evenly.
Transfer the macaroni into the casserole dish.
Bake for it for 40 to 45 minutes.The top will turn bubbly, crispy, and golden.
Let the macaroni and cheese cool for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.Cover and store any leftovers in the fridge, and reheat them the next day in the microwave.
