
Melt the chocolate chips in a saucepan over low heat.
Add the instant coffee/ground espresso beans.
In a separate mixing bowl, blend eggs, coconut sugar, vanilla and salt on low speed.
Pour the melted chocolate into the mixing bowl.
Pour into a 8x8 baking dish, either greased or covered with parchment paper.
Bake at 350 °F (177 °C) for 25-30 minutes.
