
Pre-heat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF/Gas 4.;
,
Put the butter, sugar and syrup in the saucepan.
Remove the pan from the heat.
Put the mixture in a bowl and put it aside to cool for 30 minutes.

,
Meanwhile lightly grease your baking sheet.

,
Take the cooled dough out of the bowl.
Use your cutters to cut out your biscuits.
Put them in the oven for about ten minutes.

,
When the figures are golden and slightly risen remove from the oven.
