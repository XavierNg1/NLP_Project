
Prepare the sauerkraut.
Combine the broth, ribs, bay leaves, peppercorns, carrots, and dried mushrooms in a large saucepan.
Simmer the soup for 1 hour and 30 minutes.
Add 4 more cups (950 milliliters) of water.
Heat the sugar over medium heat.
Add the sauerkraut and sugar to the soup.
Slice the ribs up.
Serve the soup while it is still hot.
