
Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit or 177 degrees Celsius.
Prepare your baking pan.
Mix the cake mix, egg, and vegetable oil.
Slowly pour the mix to the cake batter.
Add the white chocolate chips and sprinkles.
Pour the batter into the baking pan.
Bake for 25-30 minutes.
Cool for 30 minutes.
Serve.
Enjoy!
