
Cook the jam and brandy in a skillet over medium heat for about 2-3 minutes, or until it is a thick syrup.
Move the oven rack into the middle of the oven and pre-heat to 350 degrees.
Put the sugar and almonds through a food processor until the ingredients are finely ground.
Add the flour, baking powder, and salt.
Add the eggs, vanilla, butter, and almond extract.
Pour the batter into the pan that you previously greased.
Return to the pan containing the plums.
Place the cake in the pre-heated oven.
Loosen the cake around the edges with a knife, and allow cake to cool for 30 minutes or so.
