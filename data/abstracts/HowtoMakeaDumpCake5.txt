
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Grease a two 8-inch (20.32 centimeters) round cake pans.
Sift the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a large mixing bowl.
Add the butter, milk, and vanilla extract.
Beat the ingredients together using a hand mixer on medium speed for 3 to 4 minutes.You can also use an electric, stand mixer or even a food processor fitted with whisks.
Add the eggs, and beat the batter for another 3 minutes.Be sure to scrape the bottom of the bowl from time to time to ensure that you mix all of the ingredients.
Pour the batter into the pans, then bake the cakes for 30 to 35 minutes.Use a rubber spatula to scrape every last bit of batter out of the bowl and into the pans.
Cool the cakes.
Prepare the whipped cream frosting.
Assemble and frost the cake.
