
Peel and clean your vegetables.
Cut your vegetables into uniform pieces.
Put your pieces of vegetable into a pot.
Fill your pot with cold water, completely submerging your vegetables.
Set your stovetop on high and bring the water to a boil.
Reduce the heat to a simmer, and cover your pot with the lid.
Test if your vegetables are done by poking them with a fork.
Drain the pot of water.
