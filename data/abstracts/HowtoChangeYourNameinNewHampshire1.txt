
Choose your new name carefully.

Complete a Petition for Change of Name.
Make two copies of the Petition for Change of Name.
Have your Petition copies notarized.
File your Petition.
Attend your hearing, if required.
Change your name on your Social Security card.
Change your name on your driver's license or state ID card.
Change your name on all your other documentation.
