
Preheat the oven to 400ºF.
Roll out the dough ball on a floured piece of parchment paper until it is a circle about 12 inches (30.5 cm) in diameter.
Using the parchment paper, drape the dough over the rolling pin.
Ease into a pie tin, then remove excess dough around the rim with a knife, Simply unroll the crust off of your rolling pin and into your pie tin.
Pour your chilled filling into the mixture into the pie crust.
Top the pie with your crumb topping or roll out another dough ball into an identical sheet.
Lower the oven to 375ºF and bake for 35 minutes, or until golden brown.
