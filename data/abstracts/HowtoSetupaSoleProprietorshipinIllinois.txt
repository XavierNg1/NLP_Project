
Create a plan for your Illinois business.
Decide if you will run your business under your legal name or under an assumed business name.
File an assumed business name certificate with your local county clerk's office.
Run a newspaper announcement in a local paper.
Register for applicable business taxes with the Illinois Department of Revenue.
Open a bank account with your assumed business name.
Register with state and federal agencies if you plan to have employees.
File your taxes either annually or semi-annually, according to IRS guidelines.
