
Pluck the individual petals from the roses.
Wash the petals.
Set the petals out to dry completely.
Place a cooling rack on a baking sheet.
Whisk the egg white in a small bowl.
Fill a shallow dish with the sugar.
Brush each petal with the egg white mixture on both sides.
Coat the petals with the sugar.
Set the coated petals on the wire rack to dry completely.
Combine the water and half of the sugar and heat the mixture.
Bring the mixture to a boil.
Simmer the syrup for a few minutes.
Allow the simple syrup to cool.
Place the remaining sugar in a shallow dish.
Brush the petals on both sides with the syrup.
Cover the petals with sugar.
Place the petals on the cooling rack to dry completely.
