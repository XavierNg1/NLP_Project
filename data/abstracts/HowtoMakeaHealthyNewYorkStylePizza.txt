
Preheat the oven to 400ºF/200ºC.
Make the dough.
Add oil and salt to the dough.
Once the dough can be molded into a round ball, cover the bowl in plastic wrap.
Chop the vegetables.
In a large saucepan, heat the extra virgin olive oil over medium heat.
Add the freshly chopped basil, thyme, and oregano to the sauce.
Prepare the crust for baking.
Remove the dough ball.
Knead the dough.
Fold the dough.
Flavor the crust.
Spread the sauce.
Pay attention on where the sauce goes.
Sprinkle cheese.
Layer on the toppings.
Have the pizza bake.
Have it cool and enjoy!
