
Consult your doctor as soon as you notice signs of lymphedema.
Avoid having blood drawn from arms at risk for lymphedema.
Avoid taking long, hot baths or showers.
Don't carry heavy objects or shoulder bags.
Avoid wearing constricting clothes or jewelry.
Elevate your limb.
Change your position.
Wear protective clothing.
Protect your extremities from any kind of injury.
Eat a well-balanced, low-sodium diet.
Maintain a healthy body weight.
Cultivate a healthy lifestyle.
Don't smoke.
