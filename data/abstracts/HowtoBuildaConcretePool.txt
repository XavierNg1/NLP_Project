
Mark out and excavate the area of the yard to install the concrete pool.
Install plumbing, using PVC pipe and strong bonding material.
Install a steel frame into the outline of the swimming pool, which will provide stability for the concrete once the pool is filled.
Spray gunite onto the steel rebar grid using a power sprayer, creating the actual pool basin.
Choose a finish for the interior basin.
Install coping around the lip of the pool.
Fill the swimming pool with water, either from a source in your home or by having water shipped in.
