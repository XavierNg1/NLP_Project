
Choose your favorite drop cookie recipe.
Grease the inside of a standard-sized muffin tin.
Preheat your oven and prepare the cookie dough.
Scoop the dough into the greased muffin tin.
Bake the cookie cups for about 15 to 16 minutes.
Indent the cookie cups with a spice jar.
Allow the cups to cool before removing them from the tin.
Fill the cups.
