
Sift the salt and flour.
Cut the butter into small squares.
Blend the butter and flour.
Refrigerate the dough.
Add the buttermilk.
Preheat the oven to 450 °F (232 °C).
Turn the biscuit dough onto a floured surface.
Flatten and fold the dough.Use a floured rolling pin or the back of a plate to flatten the dough into a large circular shape.
Cut the biscuits.
Place the tray of biscuits in the preheated oven.
Bake the biscuits for 15 minutes.
Serve the biscuits.
