
Consider that when unpleasant things happen, there is always space for learning within the hardship.
Clear your mind of thoughts that bring unhappiness.
Stop distracting yourself with various forms of escapism.
Get a notebook or exercise book.
Turn to the next page and write down ten things you wished you could do or that you know you have the talent to do.
Close your notebook when this is done.
Don't open the notebook for the next 24 hours.
After 24 hours, open the notebook and start listing the ten things in order of preference until you get to your top three.
