
Read up on how to feed a rabbit.
Use a recycled paper bedding, such as Sunseed Bedding, but be careful.
Consider giving your rabbit a hut where he or she can feel safe and secure.
Do not use a cage with wire flooring.
Take you rabbit out of it's cage to run in the house or in a room.
If you have your cage on a floor, and there is a front door, cover the wire with cardboard or another material, and allow your rabbit to come out of his/her cage be his or her self when it is playtime.
If your rabbit has runny eyes, nose or crusty ears bring it to the vet.
If you follow this guide you will have a healthy, happy rabbit, and a wonderful companion.
