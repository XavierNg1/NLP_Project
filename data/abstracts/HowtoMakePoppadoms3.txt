
Heat 2 cups of oil in a deep pan.
Place a piece of dough in the oil and let it fry for about 2 minutes.
Flip it over and cook it for about 30 seconds on the other side.
Remove the poppadom from the oil with a slotted spoon.
Place the poppadom on a plate lined with a paper towel.
Deep fry the rest of the poppadoms.
Serve.
