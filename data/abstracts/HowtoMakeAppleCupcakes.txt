
Prepare to make the cupcakes.
Cream the butter and sugar.
Add the eggs and vanilla.
Mix the dry ingredients.
Add the dry ingredients and sour cream.
Fold in the apples.
Scoop the batter into the cupcake tray.
Bake the cupcakes.
Have the cupcakes cool.
Make the frosting.
Squeeze apple butter in the middle of the cupcakes.
Frost the cupcakes.
Serve and enjoy!
