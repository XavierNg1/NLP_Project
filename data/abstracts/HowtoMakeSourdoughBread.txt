
Select a container for your starter.
Fill the container with equal parts flour and water.
Place the container in a warm, dark location.
Feed your yeast every 24 hours.
Place the starter in the refrigerator.
Proof your sponge.
Mix in flour and salt.
Cover the bowl with a towel and let the dough rise for several hours.
Knead the dough.
Let the dough rise again.
Bake the bread.
