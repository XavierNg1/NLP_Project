
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
Generously grease a 9-inch (22.86-centimeter) pie pan.
Sauté the onion in a skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the spinach, and cook until there's no moisture left.
Combine the eggs, cheese, salt, and pepper.
Stir in the spinach mixture.
Pour the mixture into the prepared pie pan.
Bake the quiche for 30 minutes.
Let the quiche cool before you serve it.
