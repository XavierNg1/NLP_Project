
Take note of the information on your Notice of Assessment Change form.
Obtain an Annual Assessment Appeals form.
Complete and submit the Annual Appeal form.
Put the date of your appeal hearing into your calendar.
Request a copy of your Property Record Card.
Review the information on your Property Record Card.
Gather information on similar properties.
Take pictures of any internal/external problems with your home.
Have your home appraised by a certified appraiser.
Show up for your appeal hearing.
Point out any errors on your Property Record Card.
Question the assessor if applicable.
Present your evidence.
Answer any questions.
Wait for a decision.
Review the Board of Property Assessment's decision letter.
File your appeal with the Allegheny County Court of Common Pleas.
Attend your appeal hearing before the Board of Viewers.
Receive your decision.
