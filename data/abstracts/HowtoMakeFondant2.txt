
Wash your hands.

,
Spread shortening all over your table.

,
Spread 2/3 of the powdered sugar all over the shortening.

,
Put the whole bag of marshmallows in your mixing bowl.

,
Add two tablespoons of water to the mixing bowl.

,
Stir.

,
Microwave the water and marshmallows.
Cover your hands in shortening.

,
Pour the melted marshmallows on top of the sugar on the table.

,
Mix the sugar and marshmallow with your hands until it looks and feels like dough.
Wrap the fondant in clear plastic wrap.
