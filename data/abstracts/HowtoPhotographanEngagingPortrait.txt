
Ask yourself what sort of portrait you would like to create.
Consider what shape would you like the final image to be.
Decide where are you going to take the portrait.
Think carefully about what your sitter should be wearing.
Consider the lighting before the shoot.
At the location, consider your depth of field.
As the shoot commences, you will need your sitter to cooperate with you.
Talk to your sitter during the shoot.
Have fun and enjoy taking the pictures – it will show in your results.
