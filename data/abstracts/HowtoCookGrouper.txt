
Prepare the grouper fillets by either marinating for 1 to 2 hours before cooking or coating the fish in your favorite seasonings.
Grill the grouper fillets on a grill.
Fry the grouper fillets on the oven.
Pan sear the grouper fillets on top of the stove.
Poach the grouper fillets on the stove top.
Bake the grouper fillets in the oven.
Finished.
