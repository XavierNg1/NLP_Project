
Gather the ingredients.
Preheat the oven to 300ºF/150ºC.
Place the butter and caster or superfine sugar into a mixing bowl.
Sift in the flours in two lots.
Place the dough on a floured surface.
Transfer the dough to the baking sheet or pan.
Trace finger lines across the dough.
Place in the oven to bake.
Remove from the oven.
Remove from the tray and cool on a cooling rack.
