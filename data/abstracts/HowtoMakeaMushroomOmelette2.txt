
Crack two eggs into a bowl, and add the salt, pepper, and water.
Beat the eggs with a fork until they turn frothy.
Heat the rest of the butter in the frying pan over medium-high heat.
Tilt pan to coat the bottom with oil, then add the eggs.
Let the omelette cook until the edges turn firm, then pull the edges away from the sides of the frying pan.
Let the omelette cook for about 1 minutes.
Add the mushroom mixture when the omelette still has some raw egg on top.
Fold the omelette in half.
Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve it immediately.
Finished.
