
Line a colander with a double layer of cheesecloth.
Pour 1 gallon of milk into a large kettle.
Bring the milk to boil over medium heat.
Reduce the heat to low and stir in 1/2 cup of white vinegar.Pour the curds and whey into the lined colander.Rinse them with cool water and sprinkle the curds with 2 tsp.
Tie up the cheesecloth.Hang the cheesecloth for 1-2 hours.Open it up and chop it coarsely.
Serve.
