
Heat the oil to 375 degrees.
Remove the biscuit dough from can.
Punch a hole in the center of the biscuits.Using a bottle top, punch small holes in the center of the biscuit dough.
Place each donut in the hot oil.
Place on napkin to drain excess oil.
Place butter, corn syrup, vanilla extract, and milk in a pan and heat.
Add chocolate.Turn heat to low and slowly pour the chocolate.
Add powdered sugar.
Let it sit for 30 minutes.
Spread glaze onto donuts.
Allow to dry.
Enjoy!
