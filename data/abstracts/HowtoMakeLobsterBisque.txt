
Cut each tail in half.
Salt and simmer the water.
Steam the tails.
Remove the meat.
Melt half the butter.
Meanwhile, flatten the shells.
Saute the shells.
Add the aromatics.
Deglaze the pan.
Add the remaining base ingredients.
Simmer for 30 minutes.
Melt the remaining butter.
Saute the meat.
Strain the soup.
Puree the solids and some meat.
Cook the puree with the liquid base.
Strain the remaining solids.
Add the dairy.
Combine the water and starch.
Add the slurry to the soup.
Serve with the remaining lobster meat.
