
Soak the quinoa for at least 8 hours.
Drain and rinse the soaked quinoa.
Combine the crust ingredients in the food processor.
Preheat the oven and prepare a pan.
Spread the crust in the pan and bake it for 15 minutes.
Flip the crust and bake it for 5 to 10 minutes.
Top the quinoa pizza crust and bake it for 10 minutes.
