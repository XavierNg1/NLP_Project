
Hard-boil the eggs.
Peel and separate the eggs.
Press the yolks through a sieve.
Chop the egg whites.
Season the sieved egg yolks.
Whisk in the oil.
Season the dressing.
Spread the dressing on cold meats.
Serve the gribiche dressing with vegetables.
Offer the gribiche with seafood.
Store the gribiche dressing.
