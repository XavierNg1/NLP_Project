
Open the Venmo app.
Tap the Send/Request icon at the bottom right corner of the app.
Search for the person to whom you accidentally sent money.
Tap the person’s name in the search results.
Enter the amount next to the dollar sign.
Enter a message into the “What’s it for?” box.
Tap “Request”.
Tap “Confirm Request”.
Send a reminder.
Contact Venmo if your request is not accepted.
