
Choose your filing status based on your marital status first, and then consider your household status in that order as listed in IRS Pub 501, Exemptions, Standard Deduction, and Filing Information


Claim single status if you are unmarried and have no dependents.
Claim Married Filing Jointly, MFJ, if you are married, living together for more than 6 months, not legally separated.
Claim Married Filing Separately, MFS, if you are married, but legally separated, or not living together more than half of the year.
Claim Qualifying Widow(er), QW, if you are widowed during the year being reported.
