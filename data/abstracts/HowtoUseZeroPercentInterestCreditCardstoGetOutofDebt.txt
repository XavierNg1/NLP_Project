
Review all of your existing credit card statements.
Make a list of any zero-percent balance transfer offers you currently have access to and how much credit you have available on each offer.
Calculate how much you can afford to pay, per month, on any zero-percent balances you transfer.
Compare the fee you'd be charged for transferring the balance to the zero-percent card against how much interest you'd be charged if you leave that balance on the current card.
Call the customer service number on the back of the zero-interest credit card you want to transfer your balance to.
