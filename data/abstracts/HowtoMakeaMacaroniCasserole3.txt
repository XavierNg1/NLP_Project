
Butter the inside of your slow cooker.
Boil water in a large pot.
Add the dry macaroni and cook uncovered.
Drain the noodles.
Melt the butter in a large saucepan.
Make a roux by mixing in the flour.
Warm up your milk.
Whisk the milk into your saucepan.
Season with salt, pepper, mustard, and paprika.
Stir the grated cheese into your sauce.
Turn off the heat and add your noodles.
Sprinkle with extra cheese and broil.
Dish up and let the casserole sit a few minutes.
