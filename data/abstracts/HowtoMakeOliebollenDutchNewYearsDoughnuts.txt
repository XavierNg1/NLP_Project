

Mix the yeast with the tepid water.

Add the flour to the watery yeast.

Add the salt and the sugar.

Leave the batter to rise for 45 minutes in a warm place.
,

Use a large bucket to let the batter rise.

After the dough has risen, start making doughnuts.

Fry the Dutch doughnuts about six minutes.

Remove from the oil using the ladle or slotted spoon.

Before you serve the Dutch doughnuts, sprinkle them with powered sugar.
