
File general consumer complaints about businesses with the Nevada Better Business Bureau (BBB).

Submit your complaint online.
Identify the business by name.
Select the correct business from a list provided.
Enter your contact information.
Describe the nature of your complaint.
Identify how you would like to resolve the matter.
Provide details about your transaction with the business.
Review and submit your final complaint.
