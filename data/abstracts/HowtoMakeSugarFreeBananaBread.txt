
Place your oven rack in the center.
Preheat the oven.
Grease a loaf pan.
Mix the bananas, vegetable oil, honey, vanilla, almond extract, milk, cream, and eggs.
Combine the flours, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
Stir in the walnuts.
Pour the batter into the loaf pan.
Bake the bread until golden brown.
Cool the bread on a wire rack.
