
Choose a cookie recipe where you have to roll out the dough.
Cover the back of two standard sized muffin tins with foil.
Prepare the cookie dough according to the recipe.
Roll half of the dough between two sheets of lightly-floured wax paper.
Cut the dough into six circles.
Drape the cut dough circles over the prepared muffin tin.
Chill the tin in the freezer while you prepare the other one.
Bake the cookies in a preheated oven at for about 12 to 15 minutes.
Let the cookie cups cool off completely on the tin.
Fill the cookie cups.
