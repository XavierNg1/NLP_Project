
Preheat the oven and line a baking dish.
Mix the cookie crumbs, melted butter, and sugar.
Press the cookie mixture into the dish.
Combine the sugar and cream cheese.
Stir in the vanilla and coconut milk.
Add the eggs one at a time.
Pour the filling over the cookie crust and bake the bars until the center is set.
Cool the bars completely and refrigerate them.
Heat the chocolate and whipped topping in the microwave.
Whisk the mixture and pour it over the bars.
Top the bars with the coconut and nuts and cut into pieces.
