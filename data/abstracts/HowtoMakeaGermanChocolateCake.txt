
Prepare the cakes pans.Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F/ 180 degrees C.
Melt the chocolate (both) with 6 tbsp of water in a double boiler.
Add the butter and 1 1/4 cups of sugar to the mixing bowl.Add the egg yolks one at a time and fold through.
Sift the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a separate bowl.
Beat the egg whites to the point of forming soft peaks, and add the 1/4 cup of sugar.Pour half of the cake mixture into one pan, and the other half into the other pan.
Place in the oven and bake for 45 minutes or until a cake test comes out clean (use a toothpick or a skewer to test).
Make the filling.
Make the syrup after the filling as been made.
Make the frosting.
Assemble the cake.
Cut the cake pieces into additional layers.
Start layering.
Frost the whole cake.

Decorate the top of the cake.

Serve.

Keep refrigerated for up to 24 hours if not eaten immediately.
