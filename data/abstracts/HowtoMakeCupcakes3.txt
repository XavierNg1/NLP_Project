
Preheat your oven to 325ºF (162ºC).
Line standard muffin tins with paper liners.

Sift together the cake flour, baking powder, and salt.
Halve one vanilla bean lengthwise.
Heat 1/4 cup milk and vanilla-bean pod and seeds in a small saucepan over medium heat.
Whisk in 4 tbsp.
Strain the milk mixture through a sieve into a bowl.
Whisk together the whole eggs, yolks, and sugar.
Place the mixing bowl over a pan of simmering water.
Whisk the mixture with an electric mixer on high heat.
Fold the flour mixture into the egg mixture in three batches.
Fill each cup 2/3 full with the batter.
Bake the cupcakes for 20 minutes.
Make the syrup.
Brush the tops of the cupcakes with the syrup.
Make the frosting.
Dollop the frosting onto the cupcakes.
Serve.
