
Mix the sugar, water, ginger, and vanilla seeds and pod.
Add the zest from the lemon and an orange.
Bring the mixture to a boil and simmer.
Refrigerate the syrup.
Separate the oranges and release the juice.
Mix in the mangoes, bananas, kiwis, kumquats, and pomegranate seeds.
Pour the syrup over the salad and refrigerate.
Remove the citrus zest, ginger, and vanilla pod and serve.
