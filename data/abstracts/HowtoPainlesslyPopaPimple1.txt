
Know when a pimple is ready to be popped.
Apply skin-softening lotion the night before.
Wash the pimple with soap and warm water.
Wrap your hands in clean tissues.
Lightly squeeze the edges of the pimple until it pops.
Once pus stops easily draining from the pimple, stop squeezing.
Wash the pimple with soap and water.
Never try to dig out a pimple, pop a red pimple, or squeeze out deep pimples.
