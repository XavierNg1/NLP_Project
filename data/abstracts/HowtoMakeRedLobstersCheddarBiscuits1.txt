
Preheat your oven to 400ºF (204ºC).
Combine the Bisquick® and cold butter in a medium bowl.
Add the cheddar cheese, milk, and garlic to the mixture.
Place the dough on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake the dough for 15-17 minutes.
Make the garlic butter.
Spread the garlic butter over the tops of the biscuits.
Serve.
