
Before all else, make sure you are not currently embroiled in a heated argument when you do the following steps.
State clearly and calmly that you choose to smoke.
Explain that this is ultimately your decision and no one else.
When they bring up the health risks , point out that you are aware of the risks.
Tell them you will not be swayed on the subject.
If they simply CANNOT be persuaded to leave you alone: As a last ditch effort to save your rights AND relationship with this person suggest a mutual compromise.
