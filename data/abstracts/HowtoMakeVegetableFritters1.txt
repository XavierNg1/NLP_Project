
Chop or grate 4 cups (600 grams) of your desired vegetables.Hard vegetables, such as potatoes, carrots, and broccoli, work the best.
Cook the vegetables in the microwave.
Add the rest of the ingredients.
Stir everything together with a rubber spatula.
Heat some oil in a large skillet.
Ladle some batter onto the pan.
Cook the fritters for 4 to 6 minutes, turning once.
Place the fritters onto a wire cooling rack as you finish them.
Serve the fritters.
