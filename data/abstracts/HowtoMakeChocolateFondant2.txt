
Beat the eggs in a separate bowl with the sugar.
Place the chocolate and butter in the heat-proof bowl or saucepot.
Melt the chocolate and butter together, without smoothing, in the double boiler.
Remove the melted mixture from heat and stir until well mixed.
Beat the cooled chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
Slowly add the flour and a pinch of salt, folding it in with a rubber spatula.
Pour your batter into the prepared pudding molds.
Refrigerate or freeze the tin filled with batter for 15-30 minutes.
Cook the fondants for 7-9 minutes.
