
Use this method to drain freshly-made ricotta, or to prepare ricotta for use in recipes.
Line a strainer or colander with cheesecloth or paper towels.
Place the strainer over a bowl.
Spoon the ricotta atop the cheesecloth.
Cover the ricotta.
Place anything heavy over the setup.
Time the draining according to intended use.
Stir the ricotta if it is slow to drain.
