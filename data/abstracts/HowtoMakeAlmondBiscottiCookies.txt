Mix the eggs, sugar, almond extract, and oil in a bowl.
Break the dough into two even sections.
Place each dough roll onto your cookie sheet, parallel from each other.
Place the cookie sheet into the oven for 25 to 30 minutes.
Cut the cooled rolls into 1⁄2 inch (1.3 cm) pieces, as if you are slicing a roll of bread.
Finished.
