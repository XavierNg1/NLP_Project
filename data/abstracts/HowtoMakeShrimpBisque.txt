
Mix the shrimp shells and seafood stock.
Simmer the mixture for several minutes.
Strain the stock and add water.
Heat the olive oil.
Saute the leeks.
Mix in the garlic.
Stir in cayenne pepper and shrimp.
Add the brandy and sherry.
Transfer the shrimp mixture a food processor and process it until coarse.
Melt the butter.
Add the flour and cook the mixture briefly.
Stir in the half-and-half.
Mix in the shrimp puree, stock, tomato paste, and seasonings.
Serve the bisque hot.
