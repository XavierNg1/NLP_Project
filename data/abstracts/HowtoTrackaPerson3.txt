
Get a referral for an investigator, if possible.
Check the investigator’s license.
Set up an in-person consultation with the investigator.
Discuss the investigator’s experience, background, and education.
Ask the investigator about their fees.
Talk to the investigator about a deposit or retainer fee.
Sign a contract with the private investigator.
Be prepared for any information the private investigator may uncover or not uncover.
