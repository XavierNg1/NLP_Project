
Place one orthodontic rubber band around the flat end of a pen.
Cut two pieces of ribbon.
Fold the 1-inch (2 1/2 cm) ribbon.
Fold the 2-inch (5 cm) ribbon.
Place the smaller ribbon on top of the larger ribbon.
Place the ribbons over the pen.
Pull the top front of the rubber band over the ribbon.
Pull the bottom back of the rubber band off the pen.
Gently yank the ends of the bow to tighten the rubber band.
Pull the rubber band off the pen.
Glue the plastic rhinestone in the center of the bow.
