
Preheat the oven.
Grease the pan.
Combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Cream the butter and sugar together.
Mix in the eggs, sour cream, and vanilla.
Stir the dry ingredients into the batter.
Combine the sugars, ground cinnamon, and nuts.
Pour half of the batter into the pan.
Sprinkle half of the topping over the batter.
Add the remaining batter to the pan.
Cover the batter with the rest of the topping.
Bake the cake for half an hour.
Allow the cake to cool.
Mix the powdered sugar and milk.
Add the icing to the cake and serve.
Finished.
