
Combine the sugar, eggs, vanilla, and salt in a bowl.
Add the melted butter to the bowl and mix until blended.
Mix the flour and cocoa powder in a separate bowl.
Sift the flour mixture into the sugar, butter, and egg mixture, and stir until evenly mixed.
Pour the batter into a lightly greased glass baking dish.
Microwave the brownies, uncovered, on high until cooked through, about 3 to 4 minutes.
Take the brownies out of the microwave and set them down on the counter so that they can cool.
Cut the brownies into squares or rectangles and serve them.
Finished.
