
Bring the water, soy sauce, mirin, vinegar, garlic and ginger to a boil.
Simmer the broth.
Heat and oil a pan.
Sear the beef slices.
Mix the carrot, bean sprouts, and scallions into the broth.
Add the zucchini noodles and cook them until tender.
Stir the beef into the ramen.
Divide the ramen between bowls and top with Sriracha.
