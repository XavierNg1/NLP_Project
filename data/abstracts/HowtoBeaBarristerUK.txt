
Get an A Level in law.
Choose, apply for and gain a place in an appropriate university.
At University you will study seven subjects for three years.
Get a three year qualifying law degree to move onto the next stage of training.
Study the Bar Professional Training course for one year.
During this you will have to join one of the Four Inn's of Court: Lincoln's, Gray's, Middle Temple and Inner Temple.
Once the course is complete, you will be called to the bar where you officially will be a barrister but cannot practice yet.
