
Take the halter off the horse.
Hold up the bridle and align everything to avoid tangles.
Put the headstall on the horse gently, bit first, remembering to push the ears forward one at a time, not reaching through and pulling them forward uncomfortably.
Fasten the throatlatch.
Fasten the noseband (or cavesson) approx one inch below the cheek bone, about half-way between the corner of his eye and top of his nostril.
Fasten the flash and situate the buckle.
Examine the bridle from the front and both sides.
Store the bridle.
