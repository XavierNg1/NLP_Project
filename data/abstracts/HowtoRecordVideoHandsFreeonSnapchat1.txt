
Open your iPhone's Settings.
Tap General.
Tap Accessibility.
Scroll down and tap AssistiveTouch.
Slide the AssistiveTouch switch right.
Tap Create New Gesture.
Tap and hold the center of the screen.
Tap Save.Type in a name for your gesture.
Tap Save.
