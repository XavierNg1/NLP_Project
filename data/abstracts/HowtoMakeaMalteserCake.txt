
Boil a pot of water.
Grease the pan.
Crush the cookies.
Add the butter.
Put in some chocolate.
Add 3 tablespoons of treacle to the butter & chocolate bowl.

,
Put the treacle, butter, and chocolate bowl inside of the pot with the boiling water.
Add the crushed cookies to the melted treacle, butter, and chocolate.
Add some Maltesers.
Scoop the mixture into the pan.
Refrigerate the mixture.
Add the rest of the Maltesers.
Let it cool.
