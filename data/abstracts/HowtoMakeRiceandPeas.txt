
Open the kidney beans and drain the liquid.
Combine the bean liquid with the coconut milk and water.
Heat the oil in a pot until shimmering.
Add the garlic and onion, and cook until translucent.
Combine the liquid, beans, and thyme with the garlic and onion.
Season with salt and pepper, and bring to a boil.
Add the rice, and boil for another couple of minutes.
Lower the heat, and wait for the liquid to be absorbed.
Fluff with the mixture with a fork and let stand.
Season with salt and pepper and serve.
