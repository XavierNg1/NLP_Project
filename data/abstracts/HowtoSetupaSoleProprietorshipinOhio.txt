
Designate a name for your business.
Register the name of your business with the state of Ohio by filling out Form 534A from the Ohio Secretary of State.
Contact the Ohio Business Gateway organization to determine which business licenses you are required to have.
Obtain workers compensation coverage if you want to protect your business from any possible liabilities.
Register your business with the Ohio Department of Taxation for sales taxation purposes.
Apply for an Employment Identification Number (EIN) with the IRS.
Inform the IRS and the State of Ohio of any new employees that you hire for your business.
Open a bank account for your business using your EIN number.
