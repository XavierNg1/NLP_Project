
Prepare the starter.
Prepare the milk.
Dilute 3-5 drops of Annatto in 20 ml.
Add the Annatto mixture to the ripened milk.
Stir the diluted Rennet up and down for 1 minute.
Cut the curds.
Drain the whey.
Drain the whey to the level of the curds.Line the cheese basket with cheesecloth.
Fill the cheese basket with the warm curds.
Redress the cheese.
Press the cheese again.
Brine the cheese.
Air dry the cheese.
Wax the cheese.
Age the cheese.
Serve.
