
Chop the beetroot coarsely.
Boil the beetroot until soft (30 minutes to one hour).

,
Blend the boiled beetroot until thoroughly pureed.

,
Preheat the oven to 170ºC/325ºF.
Chop or grate the chocolate coarsely.

,
Cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

,
Crack the eggs.
Add the sifted flours and cocoa.
Mix in the chopped or grated chocolate, and combine with the beetroot puree.

,
Pour the batter into the baking pan.
Place in the oven.
Remove from the oven.
Ice or frost the cake.
Enjoy!
