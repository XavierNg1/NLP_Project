
Be clear about your reasons for avoiding high fructose corn syrup.
Avoid fast food.
Read food labels.
Understand what "natural" or "organic" means on labels with regard to HFCS.
Avoid canned or bottled beverages.
Lower your sweetener consumption altogether.
Buy fresh produce and learn to cook it.
You don't have to go without your favorite foods to avoid high fructose corn syrup.
