
Read the recipe through before baking.
Preheat as requested by the recipe.
Don't over mix the cookie or biscuit dough.
Keep the flour used for rolling down to a minimum.
Roll or shape the cookie or biscuit dough to the same consistency.
Use a suitable cookie sheet or baking tray.
If you need to grease the sheet or tray, consider using parchment paper (baking paper) to avoid adding more grease to the cookies or biscuits.
Place cookies or biscuits on the sheet/tray with consideration for expansion during baking.
Place the cookie sheet/baking tray in the middle of the oven.
Know how to cool cookies and biscuits.
Finished.
