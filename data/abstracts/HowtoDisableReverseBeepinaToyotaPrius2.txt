
Purchase the adapter.
Locate the OBDII port in your car.
Insert the adapter into the port.
Download a code application for your phone.
Tie the phone to the adapter.
Locate the setting for the reverse beep.
Change the setting to “off” or “beep once.” If the setting appears as an option in your phone, you will be able to change the options from “on” to either “off” or “beep once.” If you choose the “beep once” setting, the vehicle will honk the horn when put into reverse, but that’s all.You can go back in and change this setting again any time you want.
