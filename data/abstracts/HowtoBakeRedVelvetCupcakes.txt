
Preheat the oven and prepare the cupcake pans.
Combine your dry ingredients.
Beat the butter, sugar, food coloring and vanilla.
Alternate stirring in the dry ingredients and eggs.
Finish mixing the batter and divide it into your pans.
Bake your cupcakes.
Beat the cream cheese, butter, and vanilla.
Beat in the powdered sugar.
Frost the cupcakes.
Serve or store the cupcakes.
