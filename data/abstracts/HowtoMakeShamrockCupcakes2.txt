Preheat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF.
Prepare the muffin pan.
Prepare the cupcake batter.
Dye the batter green.
Spoon or pipe the batter into each cupcake case.
Place in the preheated oven.
Remove the cupcakes from the oven.
Add a little green food coloring to the frosting.Pipe the cupcake with the green frosting.Shape stems from the green fondant.
Stick the stems into the end of the cupcake to serve as the base.
