
Remember a few things when playing naturally with your dog.
Do not scold your dog.
Play Wolf Tag

Wolves play a game like tag in which they take turns chasing each other around their territory barking excitedly.
Play Wrestling .
Play Adventure

Take your dog on a long walk or to a place where you have never been together.
Play Hide Toys

Hide your dogs favorite toy around the garage, house, or pen.
Play Sing

Everybody knows that wolves howl.
Play Go to a Dog park

Wolves, although sometimes aggressive, will often enjoy the site of a new wolf.
