
Find a hummingbird feeder.
Have only the one feeder.
If it has perches, remove them.
Plug in the holes of all but one of the feeders.
Use a zoom or telephoto lens.
If at all possible, use a tripod.
Put yourself in the right position.
Use the auto focus on your lens.
Be sure your lens is fairly fast.

,
Don't move around a lot.
Take lots of pictures.
