
Decide whether your are going to make your own red sauce or use a store bought kind.
Combine one pound of Italian sausage with a 1/2 pound of ground beef.
Drain the meat once it is fully cooked.
Put 16 oz of ricotta cheese into a bowl.
Shred a block of mozzarella cheese with a cheese grater.
Combine one cup of Parmesan cheese with the shredded mozzarella.
Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
In a 9" x 13" pan, spread 1 and 1/2 cups of sauce.
Top your layer of sauce with four sheets of lasagna oven noodles.

,
Spread one cup of the ricotta cheese mixture on top of the noodles with a knife.
Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan mixture over the ricotta.
Top the cheese mixture with 1/3 of the meat mixture.
Repeat the layering process.
Top your final layer with the last four sheets of lasagna oven noodles.
Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese onto the sauce.
Sprinkle the top layer with a pinch of garlic powder or garlic salt.
Wrap the lasagna tightly with foil.
Bake the lasagna for about 30 minutes while it is covered with tin foil.
Finished!
