
Preheat the oven and prepare the pan.
Toast the almonds.
Heat the coconut oil with the semisweet chocolate.
Grind the almonds with the salt, cocoa, and shredded coconut.
Beat the eggs.
Whisk in the sugars and vanilla.
Beat in the chocolate mixture.
Add the ground almond mixture and spread the batter in the pan.
Bake the cake.
Cool the cake and remove it from the pan.
Preheat the oven and make almond-coconut clusters.
Make a chocolate ganache.
Beat the chocolate ganache and spread it on the cake.
