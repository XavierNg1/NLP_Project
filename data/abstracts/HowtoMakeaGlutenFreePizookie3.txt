Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C).
Grease a 5-inch (12.7-centimeter) cast iron skillet.
Mix together your dry ingredients.
Cream the coconut oil and the coconut sugar.
Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
Stir the flour mixture into the coconut oil mixture.
Add in the chopped chocolate.
Pat the dough into the prepared pan.
Bake the pizookie for 15-18 minutes.
Garnish the pizookie with dairy-free ice cream.
