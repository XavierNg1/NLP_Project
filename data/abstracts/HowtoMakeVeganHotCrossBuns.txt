
Begin with making the yeast in a small bowl.
Sift the flour into a large bowl.
Add the spice, yeast, sugar, currants, mixed peel, lemon rind and melted margarine in a bowl.
Mix together using a wooden spoon until a dough forms.
Turn dough out onto a floured board or workspace.
Clean the mixing bowl and grease it or line with baking paper.

,
Put the kneaded dough into the bowl and cover with plastic wrap or a tea towel.
Make the mixture for the crosses as the dough is rising.
Return to the dough that has been aside to double.
Using a knife or your hands, cut the dough into 12 evenly sized buns.
Prepare the glaze by combining the water and sugar in a heavy-based saucepan.
Preheat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF about 20 minutes into the second rising of the buns.

,
Prepare the buns for cooking.
Place in the oven.
Serve.
