
Break the head of cauliflower into florets.
Blend your cauliflower in a food processor.
Put your ground cauliflower in a pan with water.
Cook your cauliflower on medium-high heat.
Allow your mixture to cool.
Squeeze the water out of the cauliflower.
Combine your cauliflower, cayenne pepper, Parmesan, goat cheese, and egg.
Press out your dough on a baking sheet.
Bake your cauliflower crust.
Build your pizza on the crust.
Bake your pizza for 10 to 12 minutes.
