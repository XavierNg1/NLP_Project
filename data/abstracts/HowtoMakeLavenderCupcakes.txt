
Put the milk and dried lavender flowers into a jug.

Mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder and butter.
Strain the lavender infused milk (for the cupcake) and throw away the flower buds.
Spoon the mixture evenly into the paper cases (cupcake liners) until each case is two-thirds full.
Beat together the icing sugar and butter until there are no lumps.
Strain the lavender infused milk, throw away the leaves and add this to the icing mixture.
Spoon the icing (frosting) on top of the cupcakes when cooled.
Finished.
