
Preheat the oven and line the muffin tins.
Mash the bananas.
Whisk together the dry ingredients.
Cream the butter and sugar.
Beat in the eggs and vanilla.
Combine the dry ingredients and buttermilk.
Bake the cupcakes.
Thaw and puree the strawberries.
Make the frosting.
Frost and garnish the cupcakes.
Finished.
