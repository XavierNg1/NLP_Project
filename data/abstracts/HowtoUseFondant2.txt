
Choose a cake that has a flat top and base.
Fill in any holes or dents with fondant before starting.
Warm up a little jam of a flavor that matches the cake's flavor.
Make a choice about using marzipan.
Knead the fondant into a ball.
Place the cake next to the rolled fondant.
Turn one side of the fondant back over the rolling pin.
Pat the fondant around the cake gently.
Remove excess edges.
If adding any indented pattern to the cake, do it before the fondant sets.
Add motifs if using.
Allow time to dry.
