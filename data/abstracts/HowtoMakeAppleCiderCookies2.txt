
Mix together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and spices.
Cream together the butter, sugar, salt, and the powdered apple cider drink mix in a separate bowl.
Add the eggs in one at a time, beating well after each one, then add the vanilla.
Add the flour mixture in two additions, beating well after each addition.
Cover the dough, then refrigerate it for 1 hour.
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line your cookie sheets with parchment paper.
Scoop out a tablespoon of the dough, and insert a caramel candy.
Continue making the cookies in a similar manner, then place them on the baking sheet, 2 inches (5.08 centimeters) apart.
Bake the cookies for 12 to 14 minutes.
Transfer the parchment paper, with the cookies on it, to a wire cooling rack.
Allow the cookies to cool enough so that they are firm, then flip them over and let them finish cooling.
Serve the cookies.
