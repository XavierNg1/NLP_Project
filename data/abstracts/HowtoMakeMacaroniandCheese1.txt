
Gather all of your ingredients.
Bring 4-6 quarts of water to a boil in a large pot.
Stir in the macaroni after the water reaches a boil.
Place the strainer in the sink and pour the pasta and water into it.
In a separate, large saucepan, heat oil and butter over medium heat.
Slowly add the milk to the mixture, and continue to stir.
Stir in the shredded cheese, one half a cup at a time.
Season the sauce to taste.
Pour the sauce over the cooked macaroni.
Serve.
