
Preheat the oven to 350ºF (180ºC).
In a cup, break the eggs carefully, removing any shell that falls in.
Pour all the ingredients apart from the chocolate chips into a large bowl.
Mix using an electric mixer.
Use a spoon or spatula to transfer the chocolate cake mixture to each cake case.
Sprinkle the chocolate chips into each individual patty.
Place in the oven.
Remove from the oven and leave to cool for a few minutes.
Finished.
