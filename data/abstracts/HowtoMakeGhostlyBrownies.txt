
Gather your ingredients and materials for baking, listed above.
In a large, dry bowl, mix all the dry ingredients together.
Lightly grease your pan with olive, sunflower, or vegetable oil or butter/margarine.
Evenly pour the brownie batter into a pan.
Bake the brownies for around twenty-five minutes.
Allow the brownies to cool for a little while.
Create or print a ghost template.
Place the ghost template onto the brownie.
Sprinkle the icing sugar inside the template.
Remove the template.
Repeat the steps above.
Finished.
