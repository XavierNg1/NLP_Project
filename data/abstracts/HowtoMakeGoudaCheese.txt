
Obtain some fresh milk.
Add a suitable culture to acidify the milk.
Let the milk sit quietly.
Add rennet.
Cut the curds at the right time.
Heat up the curds by adding hot water in a couple of steps (see recipe below).
Drain the whey.
Use a simple weight of a gallon of water to yield about 8+ lbs.
Place the cheese in saturated brine for 18 - 24 hrs the next morning.Remove from brine.
Wax or prepare natural rinds when cheese is dry to the touch (3-7 days).
Age your Gouda: For aging requirements, targets are 56–64 °F (13–18 °C).
Elapsed time for the Gouda process: Add culture 0 min.
