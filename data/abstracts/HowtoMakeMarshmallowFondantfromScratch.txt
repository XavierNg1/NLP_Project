
Dust your counter or workspace with powdered sugar.
Place the marshmallows and water in a microwave safe bowl.
Stir the marshmallows with a rubber spatula until smooth and melted.
Add all the powdered sugar to the marshmallow mixture and begin to stir with the spatula.
Knead the fondant with your hands like bread dough.

,
Knead the fondant until it is smooth and looses its stickiness.
If you want to make multiple colors and flavors out of one batch, you can do it now.
Your fondant is ready to use!
