
Discuss with your employees and business partners how it would benefit the company to adopt sustainable business processes.
Determine which incentives exist in your country or legislation pertaining to sustainable business solutions.
Decide what your company's overall goals are for its environmental management plan and write them down.
Develop your policy by describing the processes necessary to achieve the desired results, as well as how you will measure them.
Outline how you will communicate the progress of your plan to your employees and shareholders as well as the public.
