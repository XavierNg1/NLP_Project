
Preheat the oven and grease the pans.
Place the tortillas on the baking sheets and into the oven.
Flip the tortillas to cook the other side.
Brown the ground beef in a pan.
Add the water and taco seasoning and bring to boil.
Reduce the heat and simmer the beef mixture.
Heat the refried beans in the microwave.
Place the refried beans and ground beef on a tortilla.
Place second tortilla on top and cover with red enchilada sauce.
Sprinkle the cheese and other toppings over the sauce.
Repeat with the other tortillas to form 3 pizzas.
Place the pizzas on a baking sheet.
Bake the pizzas for approximately 10 minutes.
Remove the pizzas from the oven and let them stand before serving.
Finished.
