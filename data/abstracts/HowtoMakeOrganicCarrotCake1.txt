
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (177 degrees Celsius).
Combine the shredded organic carrots and 2 Tbsp (30 ml) organic apple juice in a medium pot.
Puree the softened carrots.
Add the organic raisins and walnuts to the carrot puree.
Whisk together the dry ingredients.
Mix the remaining apple juice, coconut oil, eggs, egg whites, and vanilla extract.
Add the wet ingredients to the dry.
Fold in the puree and organic crushed pineapple.
Pour the cake batter into the prepared pans.
Bake the cake in the preheated oven.
Cool the cakes.
Frost the cakes.
