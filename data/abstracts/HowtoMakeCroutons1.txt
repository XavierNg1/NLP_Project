
Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
Cut your bread into cubes or rectangles, then set them aside.
Pour the olive oil or melted butter into a large mixing bowl, then stir in your desired seasonings.
Put your bread pieces into the bowl, then toss to mix.
Spread the croutons in an even layer across your baking sheet.
Bake the croutons for bout 10 minutes, tossing them after 5 minutes.
Allow the croutons to cool, then use them in your recipe.
