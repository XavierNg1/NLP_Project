
Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
Make the flour mixture.
Make the dough.
Add one egg to the creamed ingredients.
Add the flour mixture to the dough mixture.
Fold the granola and fruit into the mixture.
Place a piece of parchment across the bottom of a 9 x 13-inch baking pan.

,
Spray the pan with nonstick spray.

,
Place the dough on the pan.
Gently press the dough into the pan until it’s spread out.
Bake the dough for 35-40 minutes.
Remove from the oven.
Lift the parchment onto a cooling rack and let it cool completely.
Move the granola to a cutting board and cut it into bars.
Serve.
