
Boil the quinoa in a small pot.
Sautee onion, garlic and mushrooms.
Cook ground chicken with the veggies.
Add spinach, tomatoes and seasonings.
Combine quinoa, cheese and sauce as the oven preheats.
Add the topping to the lasagna.
Bake the lasagna for 30-35 minutes.
Refrigerate or freeze leftovers.
