
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (177 degrees Celsius).
Add the sugar and softened butter into your mixer.
Crack the egg and add it to your mixture.
Add the molasses and mix for one to two minutes.
Measure the flour, salt, baking soda and spices into a bowl.
Sift them into another bowl to add air.








,
Add the dry ingredients slowly into the wet ingredients while mixing.








,
Pour one cup of hot water into the gingerbread batter.
Butter your cake pan or two loaf pans.








,
Pour the batter into the pan.








,
Place the pan in the oven and bake for 50 minutes to one hour.
Insert a butter knife into the center to test if it is done.
