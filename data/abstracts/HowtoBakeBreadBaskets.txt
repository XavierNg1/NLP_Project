
Switch on the oven.
Take the rolling pin and roll the bread out flat.
Keep rolling until the bread is about 1/8th of an inch (3 mm) thick.
Take a cookie cutter, a knife or the rim of a small, round plastic container.
Make as many rounds as you need.
Brush both sides of each round generously with olive oil.
Press the bread rounds into the muffin tin hole.

,
Place the tin in the oven.
Remove from oven and allow to cool on a wire rack.

,
Add the filling.
Finished.
