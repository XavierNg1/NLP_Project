
Preheat the oven to 180 °C (356 °F).
Add ingredients into bowl.
Combine dry ingredients.
Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients.
Fold in the carrots.
Stir in the almonds and hazelnuts.
Pour the mixture into the greased muffin tray.
Bake for 20 minutes.
Remove from the oven when a knife inserted comes out clean.
Make the icing.
Serve.
