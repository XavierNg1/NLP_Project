
Mix the flour, yeast, salt and sugar together in a large bowl, stirring to combine and remove any lumps.
Meanwhile, prepare the syrup.
Heat oil to 170C / 330F in a deep saucepan.
Spoon tablespoons of batter into the hot oil.
Cook 3-4 minutes or until puffed and golden.
Serve warm with syrup and garnish with pistachios and sprinkled cinnamon.
