
Unroll the pizza dough onto a baking sheet and bake briefly.
Apply some of the Buffalo wing sauce and the cheeses over the crust.
Cook the chicken, garlic salt, pepper, and chili powder in a skillet.
Add the wing sauce to the skillet and cook for several minutes.
Top the pizza with the chicken mixture, oregano, and remaining cheese.
Bake the pizza until the cheese melts and the crust browns.
