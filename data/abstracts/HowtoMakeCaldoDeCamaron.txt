
Combine the water, herbs, and vegetables.
Add the fish head and bones.
Simmer for 15 minutes.
Strain.
Cover the shrimp in cold water.
Let the shrimp simmer in fresh water.
Strain the liquid.
Remove the heads, tails, and legs.
Remove the stems and seeds from the chiles.
Toast the chiles.
Add the other sauce ingredients.
Puree the sauce.
Heat the oil in a large stockpot.
Pour the sauce into the pan, directly over the oil.
Add the cooking liquid from the shrimp.
Add the remaining soup ingredients.
Serve hot.
