
Powder your cardamom and coriander.
Prick the chicken pieces and mix together with the powdered spices, chili powder, turmeric, ginger-garlic paste, yoghurt and salt.
Marinate the chicken for about an hour.
Mix the onions and the oil in a large pot.
Start grinding the onions into a paste and place it into the pot of chicken.
Cook covered on high heat for 5 minutes turning once.
Lower the heat to medium-high and cook covered for 12 minutes or until cooked through.
Serve garnished with the browned onions.
