
Preheat the oven and melt the butter with the unsweetened chocolate.
Prepare a baking pan and stir sugar into the chocolate mixture.
Stir in the eggs and vanilla.
Add the dry ingredients and chocolate chips.
Spread the batter in the pan and bake the brownies.
Cool the brownies and make the vanilla frosting.
Cut the brownies into football shapes.
Pipe the frosting onto the brownies.
Serve the football brownies.
