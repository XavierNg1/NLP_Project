
Contact credit card companies right away when you are in arrears from late or missed payments.
Consider making a lump sum payment if you have the funds available.
Explain your problem to a customer service agent, and ask to speak to an account manager if the agent can't help.
Find out if you can negotiate a monthly payment that is within your means and a lower interest rate.
Check out other credit cards that have lower rates and see if you qualify.
Seek the help of credit counseling or debt a management program.
Determine whether you could be viewed as a potential bankruptcy risk.
