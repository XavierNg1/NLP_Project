
Place the flour, ground pepper, cumin, and salt in a bowl.
Toss in 1 minced garlic clove and mix well.
Pour in the water.
Mix the ingredients together until a firm, dry dough forms.
Knead the dough for approximately 2-3 minutes or until it is smooth.
Break off one walnut-sized piece of dough at a time and then roll it.
Sprinkle each thin circle with cayenne pepper.
