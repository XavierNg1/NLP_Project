
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and cover the baking sheets with parchment paper.
Combine the flour, coconut, and salt.
In a separate bowl, stir together the condensed milk and vanilla extract.
Fold the condensed milk mixture into the coconut mixture.
Scoop the mixture onto the baking sheet in little pounds.
Bake for 12 to 15 minutes.
Let the macaroons cool.
Serve the macaroons.
