
Heat 1/4 cup water and 1 cup sugar over medium-high heat.
Allow sugar mixture to cook and brown without stirring.
Coat the bottom of 9-inch round pan with the caramelized sugar.
Allow the caramel to set.
Blend 8 oz cream cheese and 5 large eggs until smooth.
Add condensed milk, evaporated milk, vanilla, and salt.
Pour mixture over the hardened caramel and cover the pan tightly with foil.
Preheat the oven to 350 °F (177 °C).
Place the 9-inch pan into the larger pan and pour water into the large pan.
Bake 40-45 minutes, or until flan is set.
Allow flan to cool, then move it to the refrigerator.
Invert the flan onto a serving plate with a lip.
