
Focus on what you can do.
Let go of the stigma you perceive others may have towards you.
Look at your recovery as an adventure.
Ask for support.
Take care of your body, as well as your mind.
Eliminate unneeded stressors from your life.
Maintain your recovery by practicing relaxation techniques.
Know that recovery from mental illness can differ from other illnesses.
Know that recovery isn’t the same as being “cured.” When it comes to mental illness, recovery doesn’t mean that the problem is solved.
