
Prepare your ingredients and supplies.
Peel 4 bananas.
Add 1 1/2 cups of flour into the mixing bowl.
Add 1/2 tsp of baking soda, 1/2 tsp of baking powder and 1/4 tsp of salt into the mixture.
Add 1 1/2 cups of rolled oats, 1 cup of smooth peanut butter, 1/2 cup of brown sugar and 1/4 cup of cane sugar into the mixing bowl.
Cut 1/4 slice of butter.
Microwave the slice of butter.
Take the 1/4 cup of melted butter and pour it into the mixing bowl.
Add 2 tsp of vanilla.
Make sure hands are clean.
Take the ball of batter and roll it into a small bowl of oats.
Take the oat covered ball of batter and place it on a cookie sheet.Repeat this process for each cookie until all of the batter is used.
Bake your cookies.
Take the cookie sheets out of the oven once they are finished baking.
