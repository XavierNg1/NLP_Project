
Place the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt into a mug.
Stir the ingredients with a fork until everything is mixed together.
Add the milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
Stir until the mixture again until everything is smooth.
Place the mug into a microwave, and heat on high until cooked, about 1 minute and 40 seconds.
Let the brownie cool for 1 minute.
Serve the brownie.
