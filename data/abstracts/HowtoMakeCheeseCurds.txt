
Clean everything.
Pasteurize the milk, if necessary.
Dilute the calcium chloride.
Dilute the rennet.
Set up a double boiler.
Warm the milk to 90 degrees Fahrenheit (32 degrees Celsius).
Add the mesophilic culture.
Hold the temperature.
Add the calcium chloride.
Add the rennet.
Let it sit.
Check for a clean break.
Cut the curd.
Allow the curds to set.
Gradually increase the cooking temperature.
Cook for another 30 minutes.
Drain the curds.
Cut the curds and let them rest.
Flip and restack periodically for 2 hours.
Slice the curd.
Toss the curds with salt.
Enjoy the finished curds.
