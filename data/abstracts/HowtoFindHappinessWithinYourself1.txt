
Write down what happiness means to you.
Try to spot any triggers that lead to either positive or negative thoughts.
Set meaningful goals for yourself.
Visualize your “best possible self.” This has been shown to increase feelings of happiness and well-being.It involves visualizing how the “future you” looks after achieving your goals, and then picking out the characteristics you need to use/learn to get you where you want to be.Pick a few goals and imagine that you have achieved them.
