
Contact the three main credit bureaus (links provided below) to begin the process.
Be prepared to pay a fee in some states.
Wait for each credit bureau to send you a PIN.
Continue to safeguard your credit and identity after placing the freeze.
Understand that freezing your credit merely prevents access to your credit report.
Know that freezing your credit has no impact on your existing lines of credit.
Know that placing and/or keeping a credit freeze will affect timely approval of certain financial requests.
Remember to lift a credit freeze before shopping for a new line of credit.
