
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).Meanwhile, prepare a 13-inch by 9-inch (33-cm by 23-cm) baking dish by coating the bottom and sides with nonstick cooking spray.
Simmer the water.
Add the fresh tuna, lemon, and thyme.
Poach for 10 minutes.
Boil the water.
Cook the pasta until firm.
Drain.
Melt the butter.
Cook the onion.
Add the flour.
Stir in the milk.
Add the seasonings and most of the cheese.
Combine the prepared ingredients.
Mix the bread crumbs and oil.
Top the tuna pasta bake.
Bake for 25 minutes.
Serve hot.
