
Preheat the oven and prepare the baking sheets.
Cream the butter, sugar, and spices.
Add the remaining ingredients.
Shape the dough into logs.
Bake the biscotti logs.
Remove the baked biscotti from the oven.
Slice the biscotti.
Bake the biscotti for a second time.
Chop and melt the white chocolate.
Drizzle the biscotti.
Serve and store the biscotti.
