
Preheat the oven.
Whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and seasoning.
Put the vanilla, sugar, oil, honey, egg into a different bowl.
Finish the cake mix with orange juice and whey.
Pour the mix into a cake pan.
Bake for 45-50 minutes.
Take the cake out of the oven after 45-50 minutes.
Make the frosting.
Spread the frosting on the cake.
Decorate the cake.
Store the cake in the freezer.
