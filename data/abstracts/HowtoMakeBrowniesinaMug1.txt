
Use a clean, ceramic, microwave-safe mug.
Mix up the 1/4 cups flour/sugar, and 2 tablespoons cocoa in a mug.
Add the 1/4 cup of water and stir in.
Stir in the 3 tablespoons of oil and teaspoon of vanilla.
Stir until all the dry ingredients are blended and the batter has an even consistency.
Place the mug on a plate in the microwave.
Microwave the brownie for 60 seconds.
Eat the brownie when it is cooked to your liking.
