
Preheat your oven to 425ºF (220ºC).
Spray four custard cups with baking spray.
Place the cups on a baking sheet.
Melt the butter and chocolate in the microwave for 1 minute.
Add the remaining ingredients.
Divide the mixture into the four cups.
Bake the cakes for about 13 minutes.
Let stand for 1 minute.
Place the cakes on a plate.
Serve.
