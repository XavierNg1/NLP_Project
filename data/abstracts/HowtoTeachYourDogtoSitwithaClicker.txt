
Bond with your dog before training.
Gather some small treats and a clicker.
Get the attention of your dog by saying his or her name in a clear but normal voice, and coax the dog to you.
Click as soon as your dog moves in your direction, then give the dog a treat when he or she reaches you.
Train your dog.
Lean over your dog just a little so that he or she leans back.
Be patient.
Teach the dog to stay seated by having him or her sit, but wait a few moments, then click and treat.
Move to the next command.
Try again.
Stay still.
Move around and try the clicker.
Add the verbal commands for sit and down.
Teach more commands.
