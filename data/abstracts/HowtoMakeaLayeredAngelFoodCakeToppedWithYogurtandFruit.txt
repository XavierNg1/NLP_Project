
Line the bottom of a medium to large circular glass bowl with a non-waxy but coated lining.
Slice up and place about half a layer of the angel food cake into the circular bowl.
Cut up and place several sweet fruits on top of the angel food cake in the bowl.
Add a layer of yogurt over top of the fruit mixture.
Add a third layer of a different fruit to the bowl.
Place the other half of the angel food cake on top.
Top this cake with a layering of whipped cream or Cool Whip or whipped cream.

,
Sprinkle some nuts on top as decoration if desired.

,
Finished.
