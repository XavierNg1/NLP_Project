
Begin by selecting someone to be the first dealer.
Organize your cards by suit.
The player to the left of the dealer will have the option to either pick up the card that is face up on the table (discard pile) or to pass.
Play continues until one of the following happens:


At the beginning of their turn, if a player feels they have a high enough hand to at least beat one person, they may "knock" on the table.
If a player has paid their final chip to the pot, they are considered to be on "welfare".
