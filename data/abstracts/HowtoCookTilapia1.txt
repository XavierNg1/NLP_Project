
Gather your ingredients.
Preheat your oven to 375ºF (190ºC).

,
Spray a 13 x 19-inch glass baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Rinse the tilapia fillets in cold water.
Place the fillets in a baking dish.

,
Combine the melted butter and olive oil in a small bowl.
Brush the butter mixture over the tilapia fillets.
Sprinkle the filets with minced garlic, sea salt, and pepper.
Bake the fish for 20-25 minutes.
Serve.
