
Spray a custard cup with nonstick cooking spray.
Roll the fondant out flat.
Cut out five ovals.
Flatten one edge of each petal.Use a round ball tool to flatten one of the long edges of each petal.
Put a dot of melted chocolate in the bottom of the custard cup.
Set the first petal into the chocolate.
Overlap the remaining petals.
Remove the tulip once dry.
