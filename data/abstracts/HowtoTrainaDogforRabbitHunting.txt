
Train your dog in basic obedience.
Begin training when the puppy is between 4 and 6 weeks old.
Scatter pieces of treats through the yard and allow your pup to sniff around to find them.
Tie a string around a piece of meat (such as a hotdog) and drag it around the yard.
Introduce your puppy to a tame rabbit.
Let the dog sniff the rabbit thoroughly while holding the rabbit by the scruff.
Release the rabbit.
Run the puppy.
Remove the rabbit.
Repeat the process with lots of praise every time your pup runs the rabbit to exhaustion.
Move your puppy to a running pen.
Let your puppy run with older, trained dogs.
Prepare your dog for gunfire.
Find the rabbits.
Let the dogs out.
Prepare for the shot.
Take the shot.
