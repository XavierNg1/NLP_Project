

Understand that weather is a special concern to farmers, both in the respect to its effect on crop/livestock production, and as a potential danger to individuals who may be working in a remote area.
,
Watch weather forecasts before going to remote areas that are prone to flooding, tornadoes, hurricanes or mudslides.
Stay out of danger during thunderstorms.

Do not work on exposed conductive equipment like wire fences or grain augers during a thunderstorm.
,
Dress suitably for the weather conditions you are working in.
Be aware of frostbite dangers in extreme cold.
Do not work beyond your ability in extreme heat.
Be aware of fire dangers in your area.
Respect the daylight hours.
