
Pick mature squash.
Wash the squash.
Peel the squash.
Chop up the squash.
Clean 9 glass pint canning jars and metal lids with soap and hot water.
Bring a pot of water to boil.
Ladle the squash into the jars.
Clean off the rims of the jars with a clean cloth.
Fill the pressure canner with 3 US quarts (3,000Â ml) of hot water.
Heat the canner.
Process jars in the pressure canner for 55 minutes.
Stop the heat.
Take out the jars.
Let them cool.
Label the jars with the ingredients and the date.
