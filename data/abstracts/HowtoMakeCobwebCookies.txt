
Sift the flour into the mixing bowl.
Cut the butter into small pieces.
Add the sugar and stir through.

,
Add cocoa until the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs.

,
Tip in the milk.
Mix until it forms a dough.
Wrap the dough in plastic food wrap.
Preheat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF.
Remove the chilled dough from the refrigerator.
Cut out circles in the dough.
Arrange the cut cookies on the baking sheets.

,
Place in the oven.
Remove the cookies from the oven when baked.
Begin in the middle of each cookie.
Continue piping the spiral.
Use the toothpick to add the web effects.
Repeat with the remaining cookies.
Serve.
