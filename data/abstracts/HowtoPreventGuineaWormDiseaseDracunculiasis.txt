
Drink bottled water, if possible.
Drink water from bore-hole wells, hand dug wells, and springs.
Look for flowing water.
Use a pipe filter if you must drink from an unsafe source.
Prevent people with guinea worm sores or ulcers from entering ponds containing drinking water.
Filter water with a cloth after collecting it.
Use an approved larvicide on unsafe water.
Recognize the symptoms of an infection.
Remove the worm slowly.
Take medicine to manage pain.
Find someone to take care of your work and family obligations while you recover.
