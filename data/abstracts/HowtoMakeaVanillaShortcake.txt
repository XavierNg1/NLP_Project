
To prepare shortcakes: Preheat oven to 400 °F (204 °C).
Whisk cake flour, white whole-wheat (or whole-wheat pastry) flour, sugar and baking powder in a large bowl.
Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface.
Bake the shortcakes until puffed and lightly golden, about 20 minutes.
To prepare filling: Meanwhile, toss strawberries with sugar in a medium bowl.
To serve, split shortcakes horizontally.
