
Place 12 ounces (342.85 grams) of cooked and chilled pasta into a large bowl.
Prepare your vegetable ingredients.
Prepare the salad dressing in a jar.
Pour the dressing over the salad.
Toss the salad to mix everything together.
Chill the salad for 2 to 24 hours.
Toss the salad with 1 cup (225 grams) of crumbled feta cheese before serving it.
