
Determine how long and how wide you want the dog house to be.
Sketch the dog house.
Pick a good spot in your yard.
Prepare the ground for your dog house.
Get all of the appropriate tools.
Buy wood.
Decide on and buy roofing material.
Pick a paint color.
Cut your wood, unless you had it pre-cut at the store.
Attach the base.
Complete the rest of the frame.
Build the roof.
Attach the plywood to the main frame and roof frame of the dog house.
Nail on your roofing material.
Paint the dog house.
Inspect the dog house.
