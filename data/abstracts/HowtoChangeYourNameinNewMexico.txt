
Verify your eligibility to file to change your name.
Determine where you will file your name change petition.
Be ready to pay the fees.
Gather your information.
Choose the publication newspaper.
Choose a method to prepare your application.
Prepare your court documents.
Sign the petition and other documents.
File your documents with the court clerk at the county courthouse.
Request a hearing date.
Arrange for publication of your intent to change your name.
Submit your proof of publication for at least two consecutive weeks to the court before your hearing.
Dress neatly and be on time to court.
Be ready to answer the judge.
Receive your name change order.
