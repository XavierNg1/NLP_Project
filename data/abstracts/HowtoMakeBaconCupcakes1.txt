
Prepare making the cupcakes.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Cream the butter well.
Add the vanilla extract and eggs.
Slowly pour the flour mixture, maple syrup, and half-and-half.
Repeat.
Add the rest of the flour mixture and stir until all the ingredients are combined.
Add the bacon.
Pour the batter into the muffin tins.
Bake for 40 minutes.
Make the frosting.
Frost the cupcakes.
