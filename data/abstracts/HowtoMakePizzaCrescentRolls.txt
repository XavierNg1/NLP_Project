
Preheat the oven according to the crescent roll packages.
Separate the crescent roll triangles.
On each crescent roll triangle, spread the sauce.
Sprinkle cheese.
Roll up the crescents.
Arrange the rolls on a baking tray.
Bake the rolls according to the package on the crescent rolls.Remove from the oven once they look golden brown.Transfer to a plate, and enjoy!
