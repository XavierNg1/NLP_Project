
Choose your scallops.
Rinse the scallops.
Remove the muscle.
Cut the bacon strips in half.
Wrap the bacon around the scallop.
Secure the bacon with a toothpick.
Preheat the broiler.
Season your scallops.
Broil the scallops for 10-15 minutes.
Saturate your skewers.
Prepare the grill.
Grill the scallops.
Finished.
