
Combine the shortening, sugar, egg yolk, and molasses.
Mix the flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, and spices.
Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
Divide the dough in half and refrigerate.
Preheat the oven and line a baking sheet.
Remove half of the dough and roll out.
Use a plate to cut out a circle from the dough.
Cut out the center of the round with a cookie cutter.
Transfer the wreath to the cookie sheet and bake it until golden brown.
Cool the wreath on a rack.
Roll out the remaining dough.
Use cookie cutters to cut out cookies in various shapes.
Transfer the cookies to a baking sheet and bake them until golden brown.
Cool the cookies on a rack.
Combine the powdered sugar and milk.
Decorate the cookies with the icing.
Allow the icing to set.
Add icing to the back of the cookies and press them onto the wreath.
Allow the icing to set.
Place the wreath on a platter to display or serve.
