
Preheat the oven and prepare the pans.
Whisk together the dry cake ingredients.
Beat in the wet ingredients.
Stir in the boiling water and spread the batter in the pans.
Bake the moist chocolate cake.
Cool the cake.
Cream the mint buttercream base.
Beat in the powdered sugar, food coloring, and chocolate.
Frost the mint chocolate chip cake.
