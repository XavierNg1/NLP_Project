
Preheat oven to 175ºC (350ºF).
In a saucepan over low heat, melt together butter and chocolate.
Stir in flour and cocoa powder until mixture is uniform.

,
Stir in Oreos and pour batter into the prepared tin.
Bake for about 35 minutes, until a skewer inserted comes out with moist crumbs, but not coated with batter.

,
Cool brownies completely on a cooling rack, then slice.
