
Make wise spending decisions.
Pay the full balance each month.Paying your full balance each month ensures you’re using the card as it’s meant to be used: a convenient alternative to cash.
Pay as much of the balance off as you can.
Organize your credit card statements.Organizing credit card statements and going over them carefully will help you see how you’re spending your money.
Understand how recurring billing works.
Limit the number of credit cards you enroll for.Most people apply for multiple credit cards to finance their needs.
Do not get cash advances.A cash advance is a service offered by credit card companies in which you can withdraw cash and pay it back later.
