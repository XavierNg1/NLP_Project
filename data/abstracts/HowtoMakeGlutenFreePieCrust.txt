
Start with the right gluten-free flour.
Blend the dry ingredients with butter.
Add liquid gradually to moisten the dough.
Refrigerate the dough to allow it to set up.
Spread some flour on your work surface.
Flatten the dough to prepare it for rolling.
Roll the dough to the desired thickness.
Mold the dough into the baking dish.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Bake the pie crust for 15 minutes.
Continue baking for 30-40 minutes for filled pies.
Finished.
