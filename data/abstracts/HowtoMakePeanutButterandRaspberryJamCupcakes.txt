
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit or 190 degrees Celsius.
Mix the dry ingredients.
Cream the butter and sugar.
Add the peanut butter and sour cream.
Mix the dry ingredients in with the wet ingredients.
Bake the cupcakes for about 10-15 minutes until they rise and are lightly browned.
Decorate and frost the cupcakes.Serve and enjoy!
