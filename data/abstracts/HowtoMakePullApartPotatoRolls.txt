
Wash the potato.
Boil potatoes.
Mash potatoes with a ricer.
Mix milk and potato in a stand-up mixer.
Add the melted butter.
Mix in flour, yeast, and sugar.
Let the dough rise for 30 minutes.
Add and mix eggs, egg yolk, and flour.
Brush with butter and let dough rise for 30 minutes.
Divide dough into 18 pieces.
Roll each piece into a ball.
Brush with butter and let the dough rise.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit (204 degrees Celsius).
Bake for 15-20 minutes.
Let cool for 10 minutes.
Remove from dish and cool for another 30 minutes.
