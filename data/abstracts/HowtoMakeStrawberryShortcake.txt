
To make the scones, preheat the oven to 375° F (190° C) and place the rack in middle of oven.
Get out a large bowl.
Whisk together the egg, cream and vanilla extract.
Transfer to a lightly floured surface.
Bake for about 15 - 20 minutes or until nicely browned.
Wash, hull, and slice the strawberries.
Place 1/3 of the strawberries in a large bowl.
Give the strawberries time to macerate.
Chill an empty bowl and whisk in the freezer for about 15 - 30 minutes.
Using a mixer, beat the heavy cream, sugar, vanilla, and lemon zest until soft peaks form.
To assemble the strawberry shortcakes, cut the scones in half.
Top with strawberries and whipped cream.
Finish with more whipped cream and a few more strawberries.
