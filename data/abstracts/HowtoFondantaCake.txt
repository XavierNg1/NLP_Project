
Prepare your butter cream and set aside.
Cover the cake with a thin layer of buttercream using a pallet knife.
Put the cake into the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Clear a large, smooth work surface and dust it with powdered sugar.
Allow your fondant to reach room temperature.
Use your hands to flatten the fondant into a rough pancake shape.
Roll out the fondant until it is about 1/4 to 3/8 inch (0.64 to 0.95 centimeters) thick.
Measure the fondant with the piece of string.
Loosely roll the fondant onto the rolling pin.
Gently unroll the fondant over the cake.
Smooth the fondant over the cake.
Finish smoothing the fondant.
Finish decorating the cake.
Finished.
