
Familiarize yourself with Allen Carr.
Purchase a copy of The Easy Way to Stop Smoking.
Set a date and time to stop smoking.
Understand cigarettes do nothing for you.
Light your final cigarette.
Prepare for nicotine withdrawal.
Cope with situations and stimuli that make you crave cigarettes.
Do not try to avoid thinking about cigarettes.
Understand there is no such thing as "one cigarette".
Do not use any substitutes - including those which contain nicotine.
Do not keep emergency cigarettes on hand.
Prepare for life to return to normal.
Seek professional help if you're having issues.
Ask for continued support from friends and family members.
