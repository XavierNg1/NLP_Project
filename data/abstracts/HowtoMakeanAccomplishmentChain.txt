
Cut the paper into strips.
Write a task or goal on each piece of paper.
Decorate the strips of paper.
Arrange the goals in the order that you want to accomplish them.
Form the first goal into a circle.
Form the next goal into a ring that passes through the first.
Repeat with each strip of paper.
Remove goals once you've accomplished them.
Add more goals onto the chain when necessary.
Finished.
