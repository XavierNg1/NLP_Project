
Preheat the oven and line a baking sheet.
Beat the egg whites until they become foamy.
Mix in the salt and beat until soft peaks form.
Stir in the cream of tartar and sugar.
Add the vanilla.
Sift the cocoa powder into the meringue.
Mix until the cocoa powder is fully combined.
Transfer the meringue to a piping bag.
Pipe the meringue onto the baking sheet.
Bake the cookies for a couple of hours.
Turn the oven off and leave the cookies inside for a couple of hours.
Remove the cookies from the baking sheet when dry.
