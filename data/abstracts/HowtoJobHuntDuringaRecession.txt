
Start by compiling your resume and your references.
Use the major job search engines Monster.com, Yahoo!,
Move on to additional job sites including your state's job search site.
Watch the newspaper classified ads.
Visit http://www.unemploymentoffice.net/ to look up the unemployment office address in your area.
Keep an eye out for stores that post help wanted signs in their windows.
Work the numbers in your favor.Visit local job fairs, but come dressed and ready to interview as well as have plenty of resumes on hand to distribute.
Contact the college that you graduated from to access their job search site and/or to assist you with your job hunt.
