
Prepare the baking sheets.
Sift the flour into the mixing bowl.
Add the butter.
Create a well in the center of the flour mixture.
Mix to form a dough.
Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
Transfer the kneaded dough to a lightly greased bowl (the original mixing bowl can be used if washed out and dried properly, then greased).
Tip the risen dough out of the bowl onto a newly floured surface.
Divide the dough into 12 even pieces.
Place the dough balls onto the prepared baking sheets.
Preheat the oven while the rolls are rising.
Bake the dinner rolls.
Remove from the oven.
