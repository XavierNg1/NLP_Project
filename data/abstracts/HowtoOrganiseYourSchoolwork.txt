
Realize what an issue disorganized schoolwork is.
Open up your backpack, school bag or book bag and take everything out.
Clean.
Throw your week-old lunches in the trash.
Take your school schedule and see what lessons you have.
Get a lunchbox and keep your lunch in it so it does not get mixed in with your school books.
Start studying.
Choose a lesson to study (not your weakest subject, start with an easy one that you're good at).

Go back to studying.
Repeat this everyday and finally come to your weakest subjects.
Talk to your teachers.
Revise what you learned.
Do the pop quiz in the same area where you studied.
