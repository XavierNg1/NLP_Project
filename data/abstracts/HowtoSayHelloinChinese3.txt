Answer the phone with "oi" in Hakka Chinese.The Mandarin and Cantonese phone greetings do not work with Hakka Chinese.
Greet a crowd with "dâga-haó" in Shanghainese.This greeting translates into "hello everybody" and can be used when greeting more than one person.
