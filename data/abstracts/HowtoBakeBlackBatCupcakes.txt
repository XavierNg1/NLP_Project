
Preheat oven to 350ºF or 180ºC.
Line two 12 capacity (standard) muffin pans with cupcake papers.

,
In a small bowl, intermix the flours.
In a larger bowl, soften the butter until it is smooth.
Insert the dry ingredients in 3 parts, mixing with the milk and vanilla.
Carefully ladle the batter into the cupcake liners, filling them about 3/4 full.

,
Let the mix bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
For frosting part, place the butter in a large mixing bowl.
When entirely cooled down, frost the cupcakes.
Arrange the black bat cupcakes on a stand for display.
Finished.
