
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
Prepare a 9 inch (22.86 centimeters) baking pan.
Beat together the sugar and butter until they are light and fluffy.
Add in the egg and the molasses.
In another bowl, combine the rest of your dry ingredients.
Mix the dry ingredients with the molasses-butter mixture.
Stir in the hot water.
Pour the mixture into the baking pan.
Bake for 1 hour or until a pick is inserted and comes out clean.
Let the cake cool before taking it out of the pan and decorating it.
