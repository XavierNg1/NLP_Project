
Prepare the oven and baking sheet.
Whip the egg whites and salt.
Add the sugar gradually.
Add the remaining ingredients.
Drop the mixture onto the baking sheet a teaspoonful at a time.
Bake the meringues for 45 minutes.
Turn off the oven and leave the meringues to cool.
Serve or store the meringues.
