
Combine the marinade ingredients.
Pierce the steak.
Let the steak marinate 4 to 24 hours.
Preheat the broiler and the broiler pan.
Transfer the steak to the broiler pan.
Broil the steak for 8 to 12 minutes, turning once.
Serve warm.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit (204 degrees Celsius).
Transfer the steak to the aluminum foil.
Cook the steak for 50 minutes.
Remove the steak and serve warm.
Preheat a cast-iron skillet and the oven.
Brown the steak in the skillet.
Transfer the steak to the oven.
Serve warm.
Finished!
