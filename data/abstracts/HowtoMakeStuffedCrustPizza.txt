
Preheat the oven to 425° Fahrenheit (218° Celsius).
Unroll the dough out and press it on the pan.
Line up string cheese all around the edges of the pizza.

,
Fold up the sides and tuck them over top of the cheese.
Season the crust.
Spread the sauce, cheese, and pepperoni or other ingredients over the pizza.

,
Bake the pizza.
Serve.
