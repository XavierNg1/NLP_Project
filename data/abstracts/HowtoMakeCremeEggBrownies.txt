
Preheat the oven.
Line a baking dish.
Melt the butter and chocolate together.
Stir the sugars and vanilla to the chocolate mixture.
Beat the eggs in one at a time.
Mix in the flour.
Fold in the chopped mini creme eggs.
Pour the brownie batter into the dish.
Bake the brownies for 25 minutes.
Press the large creme eggs into the brownies and bake them for another 20 minutes.
Allow the brownies to cool completely before serving.
