
Open the Control Panel.
Click or tap the "System and Security" header.Do not select the links below the header as they will take you to a completely different page.
Select the "System" header to open the System Properties page.You can also select "View amount of RAM and processor speed"or "See the name of this computer"from below the header.
Open Advanced system settings.
Go to the Performance Settings.
Select the settings you want.
Save your changes and exit.
