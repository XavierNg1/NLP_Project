
Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
Cook and drain the macaroni noodles.
Melt the butter in a two-quart saucepan.
Remove the saucepan from heat to add milk.
Add spices and the canned pumpkin to the saucepan.
Add the shredded cheddar cheese.
Add the cooked macaroni noodles to the saucepan.
Pour it into a casserole dish and bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
