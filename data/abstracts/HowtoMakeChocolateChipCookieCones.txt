
Preheat the oven and combine the dry ingredients.
Cream the butter and sugars.
Stir in the egg and vanilla.
Stir in the dry mixture and mini-chocolate chips.
Scoop the cookie dough on a sheet.
Spread the dough into a circle.
Bake the cookie cone circles.
Flip the cookie circle over onto parchment paper.
Roll the cookie circle around a mold or sugar cone.
Let the cone set up and remove the paper.
Serve the chocolate chip cookie cones.
