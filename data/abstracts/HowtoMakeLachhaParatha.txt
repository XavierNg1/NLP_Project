
Combine the wheat flour, ghee, and salt.
Knead in the water.
Rest the dough for 20 to 25 minutes.
Divide the dough into 7 pieces.
Roll the dough pieces into circles.
Brush the circles with ghee and sprinkle them with flour.
Fold the circle of dough like a fan.
Swing the pleated dough.
Roll the pleated dough into a circle and seal it.
Roll the paratha dough into circles.
Heat a skillet.
Brush the parathas with more ghee.
Cook the paratha until they're slightly brown.
