
Decide if you will file a joint petition with your spouse or if you will file alone.
Determine if you are eligible for a divorce in Nevada.
Determine what your grounds for divorce are.
Determine what Court your divorce should be filed in.
Obtain the appropriate forms.
Enlist a friend or family member to sign the Witness Declaration.
Complete the rest of your divorce forms.

File your forms with the Court.
Serve your spouse, if you filed for divorce alone.
