
Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
Cook and drain the pasta.
Add pumpkin, garlic, thyme, salt and coconut milk to a saucepan.
Simmer the mixture for 30 minutes.
Puree the pumpkin-coconut milk mixture.
Put the cooked pasta and puree into the greased pan.
Bake it for 30 minutes.
Combine the ingredients for the herbed bread crumb topping.
Add the topping and broil the dish.
