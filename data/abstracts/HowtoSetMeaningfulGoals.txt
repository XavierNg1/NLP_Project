
Start with broad ideas.
Try free writing to come up with ideas.
Define specific aspects of your broad idea.
Write out a concrete goal.
Prioritize your goals.
Ask yourself why you find this goal meaningful.
Judge if your goal is sufficiently challenging but not impossible.
Determine what is influencing your goal.
Avoid goals based on external factors.
Set up a system to review your progress.
Break up your goal into sub-goals.
Create strategies to accomplish your sub-goals.
Set up a timetable for your goal.
Identify obstacles you might encounter.
