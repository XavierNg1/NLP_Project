
Whisk the dry ingredients.
Cream the butter and sugars.
Beat in the egg, vanilla, and lemon zest.
Stir in the flour mixture and refrigerate the dough.
Preheat the oven and prepare the baking sheets.
Make the egg wash. Get out a small prep bowl and crack one egg into it.
Combine the cinnamon and sugar for the topping.
Roll and cut the dough into rectangles.
Brush the cookies with the egg wash and sprinkle them with the topping.
Bake the Swiss cinnamon crisp cookies.
