
Heat a large skillet.
Toast the millet for several minutes.
Combine the millet, water, and salt and bring to a boil.
Simmer the millet mixture until it is tender.
Let the millet sit and cool.
Mix the lemon zest, juice, water, oil, and remaining salt.
Stir in the remaining ingredients.
Allow the mixture to sit for several minutes.
Add the millet to the seasonings.
Chill the mixture for a half hour.
Toss the tabbouleh and serve.
