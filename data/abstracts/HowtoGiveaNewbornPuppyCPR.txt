
Call a vet, if possible.
Verify that the puppy’s airway is clear.
Lower the puppy’s head.
Remove fluid from the puppy’s mouth and throat.
Decide how to handle the puppy.
Put your mouth around the puppy’s nose and mouth.
Provide two or three small breaths.
See if the puppy begins breathing.
Search for a heartbeat.
Position your hands.
Compress the puppy’s chest.
Repeat breathing.
Continue chest compressions.
Perform CPR as needed.
Care for the puppy after resuscitation.
