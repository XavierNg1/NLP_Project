
Preheat oven to 375ºF/190ºC.
In a large bowl, mix the cookie dough pouches, eggs, oatmeal, and butter.
Using the potato masher, blend them thoroughly.








,
While blending everything, add in the Adams peanut butter.
Add the chocolate candies.
On a large cookie sheet, scoop out large spoons of cookie dough (the monster cookie part).
Cook for 13 minutes.
Enjoy your monster cookies!
