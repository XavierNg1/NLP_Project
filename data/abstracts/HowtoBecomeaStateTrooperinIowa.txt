
Be a citizen of the United States.
Be 18 years old to apply for an Iowa Trooper I position.
Meet Iowa's physical requirements, which include vision and hearing mandates.
Have a good driving record.
Meet the standards of Iowa's Department of Public Safety's drug policy.
Pass a physical fitness test that measures strength, flexibility and endurance.
Take the written Police Officer Selection Test.
Attend an oral interview with 3 employees of the board.
Take a polygraph and a psychological test.
Have a final medical evaluation.
Pass a background check.
