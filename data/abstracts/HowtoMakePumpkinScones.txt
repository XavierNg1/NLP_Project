
Preheat your oven to 425°F (215°C) and prepare your baking sheet.
Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and spices together.
Cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
Mix together the pumpkin, half-and-half, and egg in a separate bowl.
Fold the pumpkin mixture into the flour mixture.
Roll the dough into a ball, then turn it onto a lightly-floured surface.
Cut the dough into scones.
Transfer the scones to the prepared baking sheet, then bake them for 14 to 16 minutes.
Move the baked scones to a wire cooling rack.
Sift the powdered sugar into a large mixing bowl.
Stir 2 tablespoons (30 milliliters) of milk into the sugar.
Use a pastry brush to spread the icing over each scone.
Wait 15 minutes for the icing to set.At this point, your scones are done.
Sift the powdered sugar into a clean bowl.
Add the spices, and stir everything together.
Stir the milk into the sugar mixture.
Use a spoon or whisk to drizzle the glaze over the scones.
Allow the icing to set for at least 1 hour before serving the scones.After this, you can serve your scones.
