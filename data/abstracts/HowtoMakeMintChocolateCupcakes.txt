
Preheat the oven.
Combine the flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Beat together the oil, sugar, eggs and vanilla.
Stir in the sour cream, half the buttermilk and half the flour.
Finish adding the flour and buttermilk.
Line muffin tins with cupcake liners.
Spoon the batter into the muffin tins.
Bake the cupcakes for 15-17 minutes.
Cool the cupcakes for ten minutes.
Place the cupcakes on a wire rack.
Beat the butter until it is fluffy.
Gradually add half cups of confectioners sugar.
Beat on high and add the rest of the sugar.
Beat in whipping cream gradually.
Add mint extract.
Add the frosting to a piping bag.
Frost the cupcakes.
