
Dissolve the yeast in warm water.
Stir in the remaining dough ingredients.
Knead the dough.
Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
Knead in the garlic.
Separate into smaller portions.
Let rise before gently flattening.
Grill the naan.If you intend to cook the naan on the grill, begin preheating it to high heat during the second rising period.
Alternatively, bake the naan.If you plan on baking the naan in the oven, preheat it to 500 degrees Fahrenheit (260 degrees Celsius) while the dough rises for a second time.
Enjoy.
