
In a saucepan, whisk together 1 cup of milk, 3 level tablespoons of flour, and 1/4 cup of sugar.
Bring to boil over medium heat.
Add a teaspoon of vanilla extract and cut the heat, letting it cool completely.


,
Cream together 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 cup of vegetable oil as the flour mixture cools.
Combine the two mixtures and beat until smooth.
Finished.
