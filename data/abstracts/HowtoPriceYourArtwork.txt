
Ask yourself these questions: Do you need to sell your art (like do you really need money fast) or do you just want to sell it so others can have it?,
You can price it based off of size - for example an 8x10 is $100, then a 16x20 is $200.

,
You can price it based off of how long you spent on it - for example you spent 5 hours on it so you price it at $100 or you spent 10 hours on it so you price it at $200.

,
Price it with a mixture of the above factors.
Take into account, are you just starting out as an artist, or have you been around a while.
