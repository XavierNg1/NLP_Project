
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
Grease two 9 x 5 inch loaf pans.








,
Mash 3-4 very ripe bananas.
Drain 1 8 oz.
Mix 3 cups flour, 3/4 tsp.
Mix 3 beaten eggs into the mixture.
Add 1 cup chopped walnuts, 1 cup vegetable oil, 2 cups banana, 1 8 oz.
Pour the batter into the two pans.








,
Bake for an hour.
Serve.
