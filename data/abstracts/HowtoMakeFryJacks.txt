
Combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
Cut in the shortening or butter.
Stir in the milk.
Knead the dough.
Cut the dough into pieces and let them rest.
Roll each piece into a circle.
Cut the circles in half and cut slits in the center.
Heat the vegetable oil.
Place 2 or 3 halves of dough into the hot oil.
Remove the fry jacks.
Serve the fry jacks with sweet things.
Serve the fry jacks with savory foods.
