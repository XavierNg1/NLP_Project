
Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
Peel and cube the butternut squash.
Puree the squash in a food processor.
Put the butter and sugars into the bowl of a standing mixer.
Add eggs, squash and butter rum flavoring.
Combine the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Alternate adding the flour mixture and buttermilk to the mixer bowl.
Pour the batter into the paper cupcake cups.
Bake the cupcakes for 20 to 25 minutes.
Make the frosting while the cupcakes are baking.
Frost the cupcakes once they’ve cooled.
