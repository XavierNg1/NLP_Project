

Put the flour, salt, fine sugar, and yeast into a large bowl.

Mix the wet ingredients into the dry quickly, until you have a rough, sticky dough.

Sprinkle a little of the flour over your work surface.

Dust the top of the dough with a little flour.

Rub a little oil around the inside of a large bowl.

Flour the work surface and your hands.
For the filling, spread the remaining soft butter over the dough, then sprinkle with the brown sugar, cinnamon, and raisins.
Take hold of one of the long sides.
Using a sharp knife dipped in a little flour, cut off the very ends of the roll and discard them.
Use a little butter to grease the inside of a baking or roasting pan measuring around 10 x 9 inches.
Rub a little vegetable oil over a sheet of plastic wrap.

Bake the rolls for 25 minutes, until risen and golden.
