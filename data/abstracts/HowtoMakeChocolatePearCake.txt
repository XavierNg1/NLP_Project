
Preheat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF.
Peel the pears.
Make a simple syrup.
Poach the pears in the syrup for 10 minutes.
In the mixing bowl, cream the butter or margarine and the remaining sugar.
Add the small chocolate pieces and the pear pieces.
Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
Remove from the oven.
Finished!
