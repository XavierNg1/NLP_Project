
Preheat the oven.
Grease a baking pan.
Combine the cocoa and margarine.
Add the sugar, eggs, and vanilla to the cocoa mixture.
Mix in the flour and salt.
Fold in the nuts.
Pour the brownie batter into the greased pan.
Bake the brownies for 20 minutes.
Combine the chocolate chips, nuts, and marshmallows.
Remove the brownies from the oven and sprinkle the topping over them.
Bake the brownies for several minutes more.
Cool the brownies in the pan.
Cut the brownies into squares and serve.
