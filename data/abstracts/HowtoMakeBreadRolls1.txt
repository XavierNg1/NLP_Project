
Activate the yeast.
Melt the butter and allow to cool to lukewarm temperature.
Sift the flour into a separate mixing bowl.
Rub the butter or alternative fat into the flour.
Transfer the liquid yeast mixture to the flour mixture.
Cover the bowl with a sheet of lightly greased plastic wrap.
Prepare the baking sheets while the dough is rising.
Remove the cover from the dough bowl.
Divide the dough evenly into 45 to 50 individual pieces.
Preheat the oven while the rolls are rising.
Place the risen rolls in the oven for 12 to 14 minutes, or until golden.
