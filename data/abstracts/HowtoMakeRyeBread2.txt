
Combine the rye flour, salt, and yeast.
Stir in the molasses and some of the cool water.
Add more water to make a shaggy dough.
Knead the dough.
Shape the dough into a ball.
Prove the dough for at least eight hours.
Prepare the oven and a steaming pan.
Set the dough on a sheet and slash the top.
Bake the dark rye bread.
