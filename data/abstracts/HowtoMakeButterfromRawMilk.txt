
Separate the cream from the milk.
Using a dipper, skim the cream off the top.
Let your cream ripen or culture (optional).
Agitate the cream until butter solids separate from the buttermilk.
Pour off the buttermilk, which should have separated from the butter.
Wrap the butter in cheesecloth or butter muslin.
Knead the butter with a wooden spoon or another slightly rounded implement.
Add salt, herbs, or other flavorings to the butter at this point (optional).
Press the butter firmly into the container of your choice.
Enjoy!
