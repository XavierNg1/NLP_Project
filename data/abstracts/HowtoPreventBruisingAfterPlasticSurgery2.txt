Eat foods rich in vitamin K. Vitamin K is a natural coagulant; having a deficiency can lead to thin blood, which can lead to bleeding.Eating foods rich in Vitamin K before and after your surgery can help decrease your chances of bruising.Green leafy vegetables, such as kale, collard greens, turnip greens, and spinach, are high in Vitamin K. You'll also find vitamin k in soybeans, carrot juice, and pumpkin.
