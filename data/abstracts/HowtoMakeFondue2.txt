
Use 1 lb (450 g) of cheese.This is usually enough for four people, when used as an appetizer, or two people, when used as a main meal.
Choose an acid to add to the cheese fondue.
Mix the acid with a thickener.
Combine the cheese and acid mixture on the stove.
Rub the fondue pot with a garlic clove.
Transfer the cheese to your fondue pot.
Serve the fondue with a variety of foods.
