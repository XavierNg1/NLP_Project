
Preheat the oven to 375ºF or 190ºC.
Combine the yogurt, egg, oil, and vanilla extract in a small mixing bowl.
Sift and combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in another medium mixing bowl.

,
Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients.
Gently fold the blueberries into the mixture.

,
Fill in muffin tins or cupcake liners about 2/3 full.

,
Bake in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
Let the muffins cool on a cooling rack for 5 minutes before serving.

,
Finished.
