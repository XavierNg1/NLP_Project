
Preheat your oven to 350°F.
Cream the butter and honey in your mixer with the paddle attachment.
Add the eggs and mix together, scraping down the sides regularly.
Add the flour and salt, mixing until combined.
Allow it to sit in the bowl, covered, for one hour.
Place balls of dough into a buttered 13×9-inch baking dish.
Cover the pan and let rise for 1 hour.
Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
