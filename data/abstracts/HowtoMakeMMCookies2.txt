
Preheat the oven to 375ºF (191ºC).
Mix the dry ingredients.
Melt and brown the butter.
Whisk in the sugar and vanilla extract.
Whisk in the eggs in stages.
Stir the wet and dry ingredients together, adding M&Ms.
Drop the cookies onto the baking sheet.
Bake until they start to brown.
You now have chewy M&M cookies.
