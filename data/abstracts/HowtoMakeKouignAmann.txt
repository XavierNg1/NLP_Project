
Activate the yeast.
Stir in the salt and some of the flour.
Knead the dough for 3 to 4 minutes.
Prove the dough for 1 hour.
Chill the dough for at least 30 minutes.
Pound the butter.
Pound the butter into a small rectangle.
Chill the butter.
Roll the dough into a rectangle.
Wrap the dough over the butter.
Roll and fold the dough.
Complete another turn.
Chill the dough for 30 minutes.
Sprinkle the dough with sugar and do a turn.
Sprinkle the dough with more sugar and do the final turn.
Chill the dough for 30 minutes.
Prepare the pan or rings.
Roll the dough into a rectangle.
Shape 12 kougin amann.
Place the kouign amann in the pans or rings.
Prove the kouign amann for 30 to 40 minutes.
Preheat the oven.
Bake the kouign amann for 40 to 45 minutes.
Remove and serve the kouign amann.
