
Follow the cake recipe carefully.
Keep these "rules of substitution" in mind when working out the readiness of a cake:

For a cake that you have substituted wet ingredients for dry (for instance, adding berries, fruit pieces, honey etc.
Wait before opening the oven door.
Remove the baked cake.
Try one of two methods to test the cake:


The implement method: Take a fork, a cocktail stick, a skewer or a toothpick.
Check the surface of the item used to poke the cake.
Place the cake back in the oven quickly if it has not been baked adequately.
