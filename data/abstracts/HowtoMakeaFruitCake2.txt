
Turn on the oven and prepare your pan.
Place the butter, sugar, cinnamon, ginger, baking powder, salt, and vanilla in a bowl.
Add the eggs.
Add the flour.
Stir in the pineapple.
Mix in the dried fruit, nuts, and candied cherries.
Spread the batter in your pan.
Bake your fruitcake.
Cover the fruitcake with foil and finish baking.
Test the fruitcake and remove from the oven.
Turn the fruitcake onto a rack.
Let the fruitcake cool completely.
