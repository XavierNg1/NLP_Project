
Make sure you have one male and one female chinchilla.
Confirm the weight and appearance of the chinchillas.
Check that the chinchillas have good medical histories.
Make sure the chinchillas are at least nine to ten months old.
Have separate wire cages for each chinchilla.
Set up wire cages for the baby chinchillas, or kits.
Choose a quiet, isolated area for the breeding.
Introduce the chinchillas to each other slowly.
Wait for the female to be ready to mate.
Allow the male and female to mate.
Check the male for a “hair ring”.
Confirm the female is pregnant.
Take the chinchillas to the vet if they have difficulty breeding.
