
Mix ⅔ cup (158 ml) of white vinegar and ⅔ cup (158 ml) of flour in a bowl.
Add ½ cup (118 ml) of salt to your mixture and stir.
Spread the paste on your bronze.
Rinse your bronze with warm water.
Use a soft cloth, such as a chamois leather cloth, to dry the bronze.
Rub a dab of olive oil onto the bronze.
