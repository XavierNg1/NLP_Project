
Know the return policy.
Log into the Groupon site.
Click your name in the upper-right corner and select "My Groupons".
Click the "View Details" button next to the Groupon you want to return.
Click "Cancel Order".
Click "I'd like to cancel my purchase".
Select a reason for the return.
Click "Cancel Order" to process the refund.
Consider using the Groupon if you can't refund it.
