
Choose three bakugan capsules.
Select three gate cards to use in the battle.
Pick three ability cards.
Get a pen and piece of paper for each person.
Lay gate cards facedown in the middle of the Bakugan arena.
Roll your Bakugan capsule toward the gate cards.
Allow your opponent to roll their Bakugan.
Battle when two Bakugans open on the same card.
Play ability cards if you want to.
Award the gate to the player with the higher final score.
Remove used Bakugans and cards from the play area.
Roll for the gate card still in play.
Lay down two more gate cards.
Play until one player wins three gate cards.
