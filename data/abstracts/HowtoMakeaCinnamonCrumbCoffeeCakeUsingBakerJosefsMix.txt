
Preheat the oven to 350 °F (177 °C).
Butter or lightly flour an 8-inch round cake pan or a 9" square or 9" round cake pan.

,
Open the box of cinnamon crumb coffee cake mix.
Take the cake mix packet and pour it carefully into a mixing bowl.

,
Put in the milk or water, melted butter or vegetable oil, and egg.

,
Mix the ingredients together with a whisk or an electric mixer until combined.
Get another mixing bowl and pour in the crumb packet.
Once that is complete, set it aside.
Finished.
