
Tie your horse.
Groom the horse.
If you want to do the bridle first, tie your pony's head-collar so that it is around his neck.
Put on the saddle pad/blanket/cloth.
Place the saddle gently on the horse's back.
Put on his cinch or girth.
Put on the horse's boots.
Unbuckle the cross ties.
Hold the crown, noseband, and throat latch in your right thumb, under and over the horse's head.
Put the crown/headpiece of the bridle over the horse's ears.
Buckle the throat latch.
Buckle the nose band.
If you have a flash, buckle it.
Mount your horse and you are ready to ride!
