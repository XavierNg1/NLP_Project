
Think about what you would like to do.
Decide how much time you'd like to spend each day or week making money part time.
Ask around for needed services.
Develop a flyer with a list of services you are willing to provide.
Post your skills and services on bulletin boards in the public areas of churches, at local cleaners or in nail salons.
Use a planner to keep track of your appointments.
Always be on time and polite.
Use a receipt book to keep track of payments.
