
Preheat the oven.
Prepare the pan.
Combine the lemon zest and granulated sugar.
Whisk in the eggs, 3 tablespoon of lemon juice, and the buttermilk.
Mix the dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
Whisk the dry ingredients into the egg, lemon juice, and buttermilk mixture.
Bake the cake.
Cool the cake.
Prepare the glaze.
Glaze the cake.
Serve the cake.
