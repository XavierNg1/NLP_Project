
Preheat your oven to 425°F (218°C).
Combine the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Cut the butter into the flour mixture, and mix it until you get coarse, pea-sized crumbs.
Stir together the buttermilk and pumpkin in a separate bowl until evenly blended.
Pour the buttermilk mixture over the flour mixture and stir it until just combined.
Transfer the dough onto a lightly-floured surface and knead it 8 to 10 times.
Gently pat the dough into a 1-inch (2.54 centimeters) thick sheet.
Cut the dough using a round, 2-inch (5.08 centimeters) cookie cutter.
Transfer the biscuits to the baking sheet.
Bake for 18 to 22 minutes or until the tops turn golden brown.Take them out of the oven and set them down into a basket or a serving plate.
Serve the biscuits while they are still warm.
