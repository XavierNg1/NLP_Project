
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).Prepare two baking sheets by lining them with parchment paper.
Combine the water, oil, honey, egg, and salt.
Slowly mix in the flour.
Divide the dough.Using your hands, separate the dough into two even halves.
Roll out each piece.
Poke holes in the surface.
Bake until golden brown.Place the individual portions of dough on your prepared baking sheets, then sit them inside the preheated oven.
Serve.
