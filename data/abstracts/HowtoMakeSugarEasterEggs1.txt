
Decide on how you wish to decorate the egg.
Prepare the workspace.
To make a simple fondant egg, take the fondant and roll it out to 5mm (0.19") thick.
If you wish to color the fondant, either buy it colored or knead food coloring into the fondant until the color is consistent throughout it.
Prepare the Easter egg molds.
Press the fondant gently into the Easter egg molds.
Allow the eggs to dry until completely set.
Place the two halves together to check for a good fit.
Glue the two sides using royal icing.
Decorate the sugar eggs.
At this stage, the eggs are finished as a simple decoration.
Use the sugar eggs as a decoration and/or gifts.
