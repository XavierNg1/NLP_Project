
Visit the AWS (Amazon Web Services) website.
Click the “Create an AWS Account” button.
Provide your email address or mobile number.
Sign up.
Fill up the Contact Information form.
Provide your credit card information.
Verify your identity.
Select the support plan you want.
Sign into AWS Management Console.
Select “S3.” After signing in, you will be re-directed to the AWS Management Console page.
Create a Bucket.
Open the newly created Bucket.
Upload directly to the Bucket.
Upload into a folder.
