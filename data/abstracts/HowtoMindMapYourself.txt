
Understand the benefits of mind mapping.
Find a mind map program online or as software.
Start somewhere on the screen, or paper, with your name, or whatever you choose to go by.
Be honest to yourself.
Come up with some type of color scheme.
Keep pushing yourself.
Don't get hung up on making the "perfect" mind map.
Consider using the mind map to create the future you to contrast with the present you.
Revise the mind map regularly.
