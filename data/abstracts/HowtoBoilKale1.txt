
Rinse your kale under a stream of cold water.
Tear or cut the away from the woody center stem.
Place the leaves in the basket of a salad spinner.
Rinse the basket under cold water again.
Heat 3 cups (0.7 l) of water to a boil in a saucepan.
Submerge the kale leaves in the water, when it reaches a boil.
Turn the burner down to medium low or low.
Drain the boiled kale.
