
Meet the federal requirements for an FFL license.
Acquire the FFL license application.
Collect 2 personal photographs of yourself.
Acquire fingerprint identification cards.
Gather the application fee for the FFL license.
Fill out the FFL license application.
Fill out the Certificate of Compliance.
Send your FFL license application to the ATF.
Have an interview with a representative from the ATF regional office.
Receive your FFL license from the ATF.
