
Combine the water and yeast.
Mix in the flour, sugar, butter, egg, and salt.
Stir in enough additional flour to make soft dough.
Grease a container and transfer the dough to it.
Cover the dough and chill in the refrigerator overnight.
Punch down the dough.
Divide the dough in half and allow it to rest.
Grease the baking sheet(s).
Roll the dough into balls.
Transfer the rolls to the baking sheet(s).
Cover the rolls and allow them to rise.
Preheat the oven.
Bake the rolls until they are golden.
Brush the tops of the rolls with butter and serve warm.
Add herbs to the dough.
Sprinkle the rolls with cinnamon sugar.
Create salt and pepper rolls.
