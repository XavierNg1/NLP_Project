
Decide on a reasonable amount to spend each month.
Divide your budget into categories.
Label an envelope for each category.
Place the respective amount of cash in each envelope.
Take your envelope or cash with you.
Prioritize your monthly purchases.
Avoid impulsive purchases.
Don’t “borrow” cash from one envelope for another.
Make sure to save money.
Give yourself a couple of months to adjust to the budget system.
Consider your credit score.
