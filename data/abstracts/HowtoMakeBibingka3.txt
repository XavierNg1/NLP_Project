
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (180 degrees Celsius).
Cream together the cream cheese and 2 cups (500 ml) sugar.Combine the two ingredients in a large bowl and beat them together with an electric mixer on low speed for roughly 30 seconds, or until thoroughly creamed together.
Stir the eggs in.
Mix in the remaining ingredients.
Pour the batter into your prepared pan.
Bake for 1 hour.
Serve warm.
