
Peel and chop the potatoes.
Boil the potatoes.
Hard-boil the eggs while the potatoes cook.
Drain your potatoes.
Chop your onion and celery.
Mix your chopped veggies, eggs, and potatoes in the mixing bowl.
Add the sweet relish, mayonnaise or Miracle Whip, yellow mustard, and sugar.
Sprinkle in salt, pepper, and paprika to taste.
