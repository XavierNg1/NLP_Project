
Preheat oven to 140C fan forced or 160C for non fan forced.
Gently melt butter over low heat and stir in cocoa.
In a food processor, pulse the still frozen berries to make berry "crumbs".
Add sugar, berries, vanilla, yogurt & eggs (1 at a time)to the cocoa butter.
Spoon the batter into the lined tray and bake 30-35 minutes.
Allow to cool before slicing and serving.
Finished!
