
Combine the milk and ghee in a mixing bowl.
Add your arrowroot, flour, and water to the mix.
Adjust batter consistency as necessary.
Color your ghevar batter, if desired.
Prepare your frying station.
Check your ghee heat by dropping in batter.
Pour your batter into the heated ghee.
Fry your batter until it browns.
Make ghevar until you run out of batter.
Allow frying components to cool before cleaning.
Add water and sugar to a sauce pan or pot.
Heat the mixture for 5 - 6 minutes.
Remove your mixture from the heat and coat your ghevar.
