
Preheat your oven to 200C / 395F.
Liberally butter the inside and top rim of a soufflé dish.
Coat the soufflé dish with breadcrumbs.
Have your soufflé dish and base sauce ready at room temperature.
Whisk the egg whites in a clean bowl with a clean whisk until stiff peaks form.
Using a large spoon, stir 1/4 to 1/3rd (precision is not essential) of the white into the sauce.
Scoop deep into the sauce bowl to ensure it all gets mixed.
