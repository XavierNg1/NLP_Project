
Boil the spaghetti with a dash of olive oil.
Preheat the oven to 400ºF/200ºC.
Add the crushed garlic, chopped onion, sieved tomatoes and basil pieces to a large frying pan or skillet.
Add the spaghetti to a round baking dish.
Pour the tomato mixture over the top of the spaghetti spirals.
Sprinkle the top of the dish with the grated cheese.

,
Place in the oven.
Serve.
