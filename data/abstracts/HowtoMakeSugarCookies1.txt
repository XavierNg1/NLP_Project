
Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C) and prepare your baking sheet.
Cream the butter, shortening, and sugar until the mixture turns light and fluffy.
Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
Whisk together the flour, baking powder, and baking soda in a separate bowl.
Slowly beat the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
Form the dough into 1-inch (2.54 centimeters) balls.You can do this by hand or with a cookie scoop.
Roll the balls in sugar before placing them on the baking sheet.
Gently flatten the cookies with a glass.
Bake the cookies for 10 to 12 minutes, then place them on a wire rack to cool.
Let the cookies cool completely before decorating them if desired.
