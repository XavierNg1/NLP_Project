
Preheat the oven and grease the pan.
Combine the butter and sugars.
Stir in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla.
Mix in the dry ingredients.
Add most of the chocolate chips and combine.
Press the batter into the pan.
Add the remaining chips to the top of the batter.
Bake the cookie cake until the top is golden brown, and cool.
Loosen the edges of the cake with a spatula and transfer to a platter.
Combine the butter, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, and vanilla.
Add milk until the frosting is smooth.
Place the frosting in a piping bag and pipe along the edges of the cake.
Use the frosting or a decorating gel to write on the cake.
Accent the cake with sprinkles.
