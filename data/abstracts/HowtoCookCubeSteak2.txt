
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (177 degrees Celsius).








,
Remove the meat from the packaging.
Rinse each steak with cool water.
Sprinkle the steaks with flour, salt and pepper on both sides.
Place one tablespoon of butter in a glass or metal baking pan.
Remove the pan from the oven.
Place the pan in the oven and bake for 55 to 65 minutes.
While the steaks are cooking, place a saucepan over medium heat.
When the butter is melted, add the shallots and sage.
Add the sage, the sherry, and the cream.
To serve, place each steak on a plate.
