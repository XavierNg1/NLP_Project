
Gather your ingredients.
Mix the dry yeast and water.
Add in the shortening and sugar.
Mix in the flour and salt.
Knead the dough.
Wait for the dough to rise.
Roll out the dough.
Cut the dough into triangles.
Heat your oil.
Place the dough in the hot oil.
Wait for each side to turn golden brown.
Remove from the sopapillas from the oil.
Add your toppings and enjoy!
