
Clean the lentils.
Fill up a large pot of water almost all the way.

,
Clean 4-5 pieces of garlic and put them into the pot.
Add 4 bay leaves into the pot.
Heat the water and other ingredients over high heat.

,
Pour the lentils into the pot.
Boil the water.
Check the lentils periodically for hardness.
After cooking for 20 minutes, add the vinegar, olive oil, and salt to the pot.
Turn off the stove and serve the soup.
