Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
Dump the canned fruit into a 9 by 13-inch (22.86 by 33.02 centimeters) baking pan.
Pour the cake mix evenly over the cherry and pineapple mixture.
Slice the butter, then arrange the slices on top of the cake mix.Again, do not stir the butter.
Bake the dump cake for 45 to 60 minutes.
Allow the dump cake to cool for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
