
Work with your companion regularly.
Keep lessons and work session short enough that your pet (not to mention you yourself) doesn't get overwhelmed, frustrated, tired, or distracted.
Don't move on to the next set of exercises until you and your pet have mastered the present ones.
Have fun, but don't treat it as play time.
Praise is your most powerful teaching technique; so use it frequently and enthusiastically!,
Correction is used stop the wrong action and show the right one.
Use short phrases or words for commands that tell your dog what you want her to do.
