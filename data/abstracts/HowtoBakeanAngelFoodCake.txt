
Separate the eggs before heating the oven, adding the whites to a liquid measuring cup until you have 1 1/2 cups.
Preheat the oven to 350ºF/180ºC.

,
Sift the flour before measuring.
Beat the egg whites in a large bowl, adding the salt and the cream of tartar to the whites as soon as they become foamy.
Use a spatula, gently fold the flour and sugar mixture into the egg white foam with “over and up” motions.
Scrape the batter into a ten-inch tube pan and bake immediately.
Finished.
