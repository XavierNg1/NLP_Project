
Preheat the oven to 450ºF (230ºC).
Add the baking soda to the Bisquick.
Mix in the butter, then add the milk.
Stir the ingredients until a soft dough forms.
Roll and knead the dough.
Cut the dough into circles about 3 inches across.7.5 centimeter (3.0 in) cutter.
Place on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown.
Allow to cool slightly, serving while still warm.
