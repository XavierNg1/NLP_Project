
Preheat your oven to 425°F (219°C).
Boil the macaroni, drain it, then set it aside.
Prepare the roux in the same pot.
Add the water and milk.
Add some seasoning, then cook the sauce until it thickens.
Combine the bread crumbs with the parsley and garlic.
Melt the rest of the butter.
Combine the melted butter with the breadcrumbs along with some salt.
Combine the macaroni with the grated Parmigiano Reggiano and the sauce.
Bake the macaroni for 25 to 30 minutes.
Let the macaroni rest for 10 minutes before serving it.
