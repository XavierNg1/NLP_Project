
Set up your flower nail with a daffodil template and parchment paper.
Set up your icing bag.
Make a mound in the middle of your parchment paper.
Make a pull-out dot in the center of the mound with small round tip.
Make 5 to 6 shorter pull-out dots around the first one.
Create your first petal with a curved petal tip.
Pipe out your next two petals.
Make second row of petals.
Chill the tulip for 15 minutes.
