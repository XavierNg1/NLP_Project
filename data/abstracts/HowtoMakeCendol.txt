
Blend the pandan leaves with water.
Strain the water into a shallow pot.
Add the rice, mung bean and tapioca flour.
Simmer and stir to thicken.
Combine the ingredients in a saucepan.
Cook the mixture until the gula melaka and sugar melt down.
Reduce the heat and simmer.
Set aside to cool.
Strain the cendol into cold water.
Ready the coconut milk.
Transfer the cendol noodles to a cup or bowl.
Top with coconut milk and other ingredients.
Ladle on the palm sugar syrup.
