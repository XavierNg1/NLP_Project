
Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C).
Blend the sugar, butter, lemon rind, lemonade concentrate, and vanilla extract.
Gradually add the eggs and egg whites.
Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and baking soda separately.
Alternate between adding the flour mixture and buttermilk into the butter mixture.
Pour the batter into the two prepared pans.
Bake the cakes for about 20 minutes.
Allow the cakes to cool in their pans for 1 hour.
Level the cakes off as much as you can.
Poke holes in the cake using a fork.
Prepare your glaze.
Pour the glaze over the cakes.
Cover and chill the cakes.
Let the cakes cool off before removing them from the pans.
Beat the first five frosting ingredients.
Add in the powdered sugar.
Chill the frosting for 1 hour.
Spread some frosting on top of the first cake.
Place the second layer on top and finish frosting the cake.
