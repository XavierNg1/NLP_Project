
Look for any of the following psychological signs in a child.
Watch for clinging to people or objects.
Notice nighttime fears.
Notice if the child keeps asking if the event will reoccur.
Consider how much the child trusts adults.
See if the child is afraid of certain places.
Watch for guilt or shame.
Notice how the child interacts with other children.
Pay attention if the child becomes startled more easily or afraid of sounds that didn't scare them before.
Notice fears that they report.
Watch for thoughts of self harm or suicide.
A psychologist or psychiatrist might see signs of anxiety, depression or fearlessness in the child.
