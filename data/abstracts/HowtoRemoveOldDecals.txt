
Clean the decal and the surrounding area.
Rinse and dry the area.
Start lightly picking at the weaker points of the decal.
Apply heat with a hair dryer to loosen the adhesive.
Use rubbing alcohol or WD-40 before you pull the decal.
Peel or scrape away the decal.
Carefully use a glue removal product.
Clean the area again.
Apply a fresh coat of polish and wax.
