
Go to the United States Small Business Administration website at www.SBA.gov.
Find the section that says "Register Your Fictitious or "Doing Business As" (DBA) Name.",
Review the state's requirements for a business name.
Find the search engine for the state's registered DBA names.
Fill out the appropriate registration form online or print out a paper copy.
Pay a fee to register your fictitious name.
Submit the form and payment online, or print the form and send it to the address indicated on the form.
Wait 1 to 4 weeks to receive confirmation that your assumed business name has been registered.
Submit a newspaper ad to your local newspaper announcing your assumed business name, if it is required.
Re-file your business name according to your state's requirements.
