
Preheat your oven to 350ºF (176ºC).
Boil 20 jumbo-sized pasta shells.
Add 2 tbsp.
Add 1/3 cup of shredded carrots to the oil.
Add 1/3 cup of sliced mushrooms and 2 minced garlic cloves.
Remove from the heat.
Make the cheesy mixture.
Combine the veggie mixture with the cheesy mixture.
Spread 3 tbsp.
Stuff the shells with the mixture.
Spoon the rest of the tomato sauce over the shells.
Cover the shells with aluminum foil and bake it for 20-25 minutes.
Serve.
