
Prepare the oven and muffin tins.
Grind flax seeds.
Mix flax meal and water.
Combine dry ingredients.
Replace half the all purpose flour with cake flour if you prefer somewhat lighter, cake-ey brownies.
Melt remaining wet ingredients together.
Stir all ingredients together.
Fill the muffin tins.
Bake for 22 to 27 minutes.
Let cool.
