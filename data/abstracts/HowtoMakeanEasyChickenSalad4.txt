
Gather your ingredients.
Preheat oven to 350° F (177° C).

,
Combine 1 tablespoon sesame oil and 1⁄2 tablespoon (7.4 ml) soy sauce, brushing over chicken breasts completely.

,
When oven is finished preheating, place chicken breasts in oven for 13 to 15 minutes, or until fully cooked through.
Cut chicken into slices.
In a salad bowl, combine Napa cabbage, red cabbage, carrot, scallions, Mandarin orange, noodles, and sliced chicken.

,
Whisk together the salad dressing ingredients in a separate bowl.
Incorporate dressing into salad and toss.

,
Top or garnish with toasted almonds.
