
Prepare the cabbage.
Cut the mushroom stems off and thinly mince them.

,
Remove the skin from the ginger and use a grater to grate it.

,
Devein the shrimp by cutting along the backs of them, following the vein to the tail.
Blanch the cabbage by adding them to a pot filled with a few inches of boiling water.
Mix everything together.
Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for 20 minutes.

,
Create the wontons.
Boil the wontons.
Put the wontons in a large bowl and then add a 1/2 teaspoon of sesame oil to the bowl.
