
Treat the early stages of cirrhosis before the liver fails/is ruined.
Stop all underlying causes of continued damage such as from alcohol, saturated fats, excess sugar, salt (called sodium on food labels) and stop using OTC pain-relievers (limit all such diet and self-medicating behaviors) immediately.
Limit your sodium intake.
Eat a well-balanced, healthy diet.
Begin a regular exercise program and sleep too.
Avoid non-steroidal, anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs).
Get help on chores and routine.
Seek a support group.
