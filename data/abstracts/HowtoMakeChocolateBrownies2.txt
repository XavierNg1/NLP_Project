
Preheat the oven to 300° F (150° C).
Melt the butter over low heat.
Add the rest of the wet ingredients and the sugar.
In a separate bowl, mix the dry ingredients.
Incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
Pour the brownie batter into the greased and floured pan.
Cook in the oven at 300° F for 30 minutes.
Once brownies have cooled, dust them with confectioners' sugar.
Cut the brownies and enjoy!
