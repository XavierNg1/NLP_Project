
Assemble the items needed.
Preheat the oven to 150ºC/300ºF.
Toast the almonds.
Once cooled, place the toasted almonds in a heatproof bowl.
Place the small saucepan on the heat source.
Cook the syrup until it turns the soft ball stage.
Pour the syrup over the nut mixture in the bowl.
Spoon the mixture into the prepared pan.
Place into the oven to bake.
Remove from the oven.
Dust heavily with icing sugar (confectioners sugar).
Finished.
