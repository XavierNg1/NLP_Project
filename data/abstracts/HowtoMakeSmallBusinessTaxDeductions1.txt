
Speak with a bookkeeper or certified public accountant (CPA) to develop a way to keep track of business expenses.
Study the current year's possible tax deductions.
Ensure you keep a tax diary on computer software or a journal.
Keep receipts of anything that could be considered a "reasonable" business expense.
Do not deduct anything that you do not have a receipt or bank statement of, to show as proof.
Take tax credits as well as tax deductions.
