
Purchase a roll of pre-made biscuits from your favorite brand.
Take two biscuits and roll them in between your hands into ten-inch ropes.
Twist biscuits together and pinch ends.
Alternatively, use an icing pipette or icing bag to pump out the strips.
Pour vegetable oil into a medium-sized frying pan.
Drop the dough twists into the pan, turning occasionally.
Remove the churros with the slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel.
Roll the twisted dough in cinnamon and sugar mixture.
Serve warm, with chocolate dip if desired.
