
Activate your yeast in warm water.
Transfer the yeast to a large mixing bowl and add salt and flour.
Add olive oil to the dough once you've added the flour.
Knead the dough.
Leave the dough alone for 1 hour to rise.
Set the dough out on a floured countertop.
Using your fingers, pull and flatten the dough into a crust.
Toss you dough for a perfect, round crust if you feel confident.
