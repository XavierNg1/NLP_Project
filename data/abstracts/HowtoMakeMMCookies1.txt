
Preheat the oven to 350ºF (180ºC).
Mix the dry ingredients.
Cream the butter and sugar in a new bowl.
Stir in the eggs and vanilla extract.
Mix together the wet and dry ingredients.
Stir in the M&Ms.
Chill the dough (optional).
Drop the cookies onto the baking sheet.
Bake the cookies until they start browning.
Enjoy your puffy M&M Cookies.
