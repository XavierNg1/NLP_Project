
Spray the inside of a 12 to 16-ounce (350 to 475 milliliters) microwave-safe mug with non-stick cooking spray.If you don't have any cooking spray, you can also simply rub some butter inside the mug.
Add the eggs, salt, and pepper into the mug and beat them together with a fork.
Microwave for 1 minute.
Add any fillings, if desired.
Give the eggs a quick stir and microwave them for another 1 to 2 minutes.How long you microwave the eggs for depends on the strength of your microwave.
Serve the omelet.
