
Heat the milk, butter, and sugar.
Pour the liquid into another container.
Stir in the yeast.
Add flavor.
Add in the rest of the flour.
Add the oil to a bowl, smoothing it up the sides.
Heat the oven to 500 °F (260 °C).
Rub a baking sheet with olive oil.
Flour an area to work.
Create baguettes.
Let the dough rise again.
Top with egg white.
Humidify the oven.
Start with 10 minutes.
Lower the temperature to 400 °F (204 °C).
Remove it to a cooling rack.
