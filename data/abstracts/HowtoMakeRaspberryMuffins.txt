
Position the oven rack in the middle.
Preheat the oven.
Line a muffin pan with paper liners.
Stir the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together.
Beat the butter and sugar.
Mix in the eggs, sour cream, and vanilla.
Add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture.
Fold in the raspberries.
Mix some sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg for the topping.
Spoon the batter into the muffin pan and add the topping.
Bake the muffins for 18 to 20 minutes.
