
Keep an eye on your loved one after a potentially traumatic event.
Compare the person's current behavior with what is typical for them.
Notice signs of withdrawal from the world.
Watch for avoidance behaviors.
Pay attention if the autistic person loses interest in relationships and activities they ordinarily love.
Notice if the autistic person begins clinging to a person, place, or favorite object.
Consider their ability to focus.
Watch for a loss of confidence in themselves and/or others.Your loved one may become more insecure and withdrawn.
Consider what your loved one talks about.
Recognize that grief and trauma move at different speeds for different people.
