
Soak the chickpeas in cold water for at least 6 hours.
Cook the chickpeas in a pressure cooker.
Fry the potatoes in a saucepan with ghee.
Fry the coriander leaves, cumin, and chili powder in the same saucepan for a few seconds.
Add the boiled chickpeas to the saucepan and fry for 5 minutes.
Add the chole masala, mango powder, and black pepper powder and cook for 2 minutes.
Add the diced potatoes and tomato to the pan and cook for 2 minutes.
Add garnish.
Serve.
