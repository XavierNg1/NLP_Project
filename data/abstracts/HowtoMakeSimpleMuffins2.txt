
Preheat the oven to 375°F/190°C.

,
Prep the muffin pan.
Mix the butter and sugar with an electric mixer until it’s light and fluffy.
Add the egg to the sugar and butter mixture.
Stir together the remaining dry ingredients.
Combine the milk with both wet and dry mixtures alternately.
Spoon the batter into muffin cups.
Bake the muffins for 20-25 minutes.
Check to see if they’re done after 25 minutes.
Remove the pan from the oven and let the muffins cool.
Eat them while they're nice and warm or freeze for later!
