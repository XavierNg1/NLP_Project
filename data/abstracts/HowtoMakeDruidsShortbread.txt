
Prepare the baking pan.
Cream the butter and sugar in the mixing bowl.

,
Add the egg yolks.
Add the peel or glazed/candied angelica pieces.
Transfer the shortbread dough to the prepared baking pan.
Break the egg into a bowl.
Paint the surface of the shortbread with the egg wash using a pastry brush.

,
Place in the preheated oven.
Remove from the oven.
Serve.
