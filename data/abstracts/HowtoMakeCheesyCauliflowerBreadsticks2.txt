
Prepare cooking area.
Chop cleaned cauliflower into chunks and soften in microwave or on the stove.
Strain water and place cauliflower into the food processor.
Combine 2 cloves of garlic (minced or chopped) with 2 large beaten eggs, 4 oz.
Pour mixture into the pre-coated baking pan.
Bake at 450 for 20 to 25 minutes or until tops are golden brown.
Remove from oven and cut into breadstick strips.
Finished.
