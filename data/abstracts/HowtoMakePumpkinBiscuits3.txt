
Preheat your oven to 450°F (232°C).
Combine all of the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Add the coconut oil gradually to the dry mixture and blend it until you get coarse crumbs.
Stir together the pumpkin puree and coconut milk in a separate bowl.
Pour the pumpkin mixture into the flour mixture, and stir with a spoon until combined.
Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and pat it into a 1-inch (2.54 centimeters) thick sheet.
Cut the dough out using round, 2-inch (5.08 centimeters) cookie cutters.
Transfer the biscuits to the baking sheet.
Bake for 15 minutes, or until the edges turn golden brown.Take them out of the oven when they are done and set them onto a platter or tuck them into a basket.
Serve the biscuits.
