
Grease lightly and sprinkle a little flour on the baking tray/sheet.
Sieve the self raising/rising flour into a mixing bowl.
Rub in the soft butter until the mixture forms the consistency of breadcrumbs.

,
Make a well in the middle of the mixture.
Add the dried fruits and golden syrup.
Roll the dough into a large ball, then break off into small balls to form the cookie shapes.
Transfer the cookie shapes to the baking tray/sheet.
Place in the preheated oven.
Turn off the oven heat.
Take the cookies out of the oven.
Serve once cooled.
Finished.
