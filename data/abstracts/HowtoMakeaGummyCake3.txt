
Preheat the oven to 180ºC/350ºF.
Melt the chocolate with the water in a double boiler.
Sift the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and sugar into the mixing bowl.
Stir or beat to combine.
Add the eggs.
Transfer the mixture into the cupcake cases.
Place in the preheated oven.
Remove from the oven.
Frost the top of each cupcake.
Add the gummy candies.
Allow to set.
