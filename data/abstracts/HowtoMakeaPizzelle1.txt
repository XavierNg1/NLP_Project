
Sift the flour and salt into the large mixing bowl.
In another bowl, pour the lukewarm water.Mix the ingredients together until you form a soft, sticky dough.
Place the dough onto a floured surface.
Divide the dough into 16 even pieces.
Arrange the floured dough balls onto a baking sheet that has been floured.
Turn the balls into pizzelle shapes.
Heat the oil for deep frying.
Fry.
Remove each pizzelle and drain on absorbent paper or paper towel.
Serve immediately.
