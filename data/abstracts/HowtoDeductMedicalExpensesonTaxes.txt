
Review the list of deductible medical expenses on the IRS website.
Keep your receipts from medical costs throughout the year.
Keep a spreadsheet of all your medical expenses.
Keep track of family members' medical expenses.
Remove any medical expenses that are reimbursed by the doctor, an agency or an insurance company.
Calculate your Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) at year's end.
Total your deductible medical expenses from the current tax year.
Multiply your AGI by 7.5 percent, or 0.075.
Request a Schedule A from the IRS, or print a copy online at IRS.gov.
Fill in the top of the Form 1040, which says "Medical and Dental Expenses.",
Complete your taxes and send them in before the due date in April.
