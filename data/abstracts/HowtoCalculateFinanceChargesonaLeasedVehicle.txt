
Determine the net cap cost.
Establish the residual value of the vehicle.
Find out the dealer’s money factor.
Add the net cap cost and the residual value.
Multiply that sum by the money factor.
Apply the monthly finance charge.
Figure the full monthly payment.
Ask for the data you want.
Press the dealer for the “money factor.” The money factor is a decimal number that car dealerships use to calculate the finance charges.
Ask the dealer to show you the calculation worksheet.
Threaten to leave if the dealer is not forthcoming with information.
