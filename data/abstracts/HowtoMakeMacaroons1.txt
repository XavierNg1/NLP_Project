
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and cover your baking sheets with parchment paper.
Beat the egg whites in an electric mixer.
Slowly add the sugar then add the vanilla extract and salt.
Fold the coconut into the egg white mixture using a mixing spoon.
Form the macaroons into small balls and place them 1 inch (2.54 centimeters) apart on the parchment paper.Try to make the balls about 1½ inches (3.81 centimeters) wide.
Bake the macaroons for 15 to 20 minutes.
Let the macaroons cool.
Serve the macaroons, or dip them in melted chocolate first.
