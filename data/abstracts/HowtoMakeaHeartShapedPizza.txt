
Make or purchase pizza dough.
Knead the dough.
Create the heart shape.
Spread on the pizza sauce.
Add your favorite toppings.
Place in the oven at the temperature suggested by the recipe suitable for your dough and chosen toppings, usually around 160ºC or 320ºF degrees for 15 to 25 minutes.
Remove it from the oven when baked.
Present it to your favorite person.
Finished.
