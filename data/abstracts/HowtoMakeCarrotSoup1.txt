
In a 6-quart pan, add the butter and onion to your carrots.
Stir in the stock, ginger, and any other desired add-ins now.
Reduce to a simmer until carrots are tender.
Pour the soup through a strainer, catching the solids and preserving the broth.
Add the solids and about a pint of the broth to a blender.
Return the soup and the remaining broth to the pan and add the cream, stirring constantly.
Ladle into bowls for serving.
