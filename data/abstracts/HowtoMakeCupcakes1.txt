
Preheat your oven to 325ºF (162ºC).
Line a cupcake pan with paper liners.
Combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
Add 4 sticks of unsalted cubed butter to the mixture.
Add 4 large eggs to the mixture, one at a time.
Add 1 cup of whole milk and 1 tsp.
Fill each baking cup 2/3 full with the batter.
Bake for 17-20 minutes.
Make the frosting.
Cool the cupcakes.
Decorate the cupcakes with the frosting.
Serve.
