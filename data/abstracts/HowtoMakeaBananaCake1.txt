
Preheat the oven to 180ᴼC/350ºF.
Grease and line a 2-pound loaf tin.
Sift the flour into a bowl, then add the milk, vanilla, sugar, and eggs.
Melt the 1/2 cup of butter and mash the overripe bananas in, mixing well.
Combine butter and flour mixtures, stirring just until they are completely blended.
Pour all the batter into your tin and cook for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
Let the cake cool for 5-10 minutes in the pan before serving.
