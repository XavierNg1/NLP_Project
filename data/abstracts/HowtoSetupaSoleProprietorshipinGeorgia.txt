
Create a business plan.
Decide if you will operate your business under your own name or under a "trade name.",
Publish an announcement of your Georgia trade name in the local paper for 2 weeks.
Apply for Georgia business tax collection, sometimes called business licenses, or zoning permits with the county that are deemed necessary to operate your business.
Apply for a federal Employee Identification Number (EIN) if you plan to have employees.
Apply for a bank account.
Apply for worker's compensation insurance if you intend to have 3 or more employees.
Report new hires on the IRS website and at Georgia's new hire reporting website, Newhire-reporting.com/GA-Newhire.
Report your earnings on your personal IRS Form 1040, Schedule C. Consult IRS Publication 535 for a list of business expenses that you can also report on this form.
