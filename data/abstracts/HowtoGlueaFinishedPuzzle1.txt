
Get liquid jigsaw puzzle glue.
Use parchment paper to avoid gluing the puzzle to the table.
Slide the parchment paper under the finished puzzle.
Flatten the puzzle with a rolling pin.
Apply glue to the picture side of the puzzle.
Spread the glue evenly across the surface of the finished puzzle.
Let the glue bond and dry for 5 hours.
Apply a second coat of glue.
Apply glue to the back of the puzzle and let it dry.
