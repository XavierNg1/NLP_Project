Get 1 to 3 9-inch cake pans, depending on how many layers you want.
Make the batter.
Pour the batter into the cake pans.
Bake the cakes for 22-24 minutes.
Place the pans on cooling racks for about 10-12 minutes.
Frost each cake layer with cream cheese frosting and decorate the top layer and sides with pineapple flowers.
