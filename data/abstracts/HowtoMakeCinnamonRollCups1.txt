
Preheat your oven to 375°F (191°C).
Flatten a cinnamon roll between two sheets of wax paper.
Tuck the cinnamon roll circle into a cup on your pan.
Bake the cinnamon roll cups for 8 minutes.
Flatten the cinnamon rolls.
Bake the cinnamon roll cups for 6 more minutes.
Allow the cups to cool completely in the pan.
Use the cinnamon roll cups.
