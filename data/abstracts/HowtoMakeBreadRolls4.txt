
Prepare the baking sheets.
Prepare the yeast.
Sift the flour into the mixing bowl.
Add the milk or water and the frothed-up yeast.
Tip the dough out onto a floured surface.
Place in a lightly oiled bowl.
Remove from the bowl.
Preheat the oven while the rolls are rising.
Shape the pieces into round balls.
Remove the wrap and place the rolls in the oven for baking.
Remove from the oven.
