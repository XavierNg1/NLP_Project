
Wet some paper towels or a piece of cotton cloth with cold water.
Run the shirt under cold water from the back.
Treat with dishwashing liquid.
Rinse away the soap with white vinegar.
Repeat the dishwashing liquid and vinegar treatments.
Use a pretreatment laundry stain remover.
Rinse the shirt thoroughly, then wash. Use cold water for both the rinsing and washing, as hot water can cause a stain to set.
Allow the shirt to air dry.
