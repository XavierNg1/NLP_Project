
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (180 degrees Celsius).Meanwhile, prepare a large baking sheet by covering it with parchment paper.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Add the butter and egg.
Mix in the buttermilk.
Beat the dough.
Flatten the dough.
Cut the dough into squares.
Prepare the surface.
Bake until lightly browned.
Cool and serve.
