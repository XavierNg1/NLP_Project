
Clean your dog with a warm, damp cloth.
Remove all soiled bedding from whelping area.
Allow your dog to rest.
Make sure that your dog has access to plenty of fluids during and immediately following the whelping process.
Monitor your dog's health carefully for the first weeks after giving birth.
Look for signs of infection.
Check your dog's mammary glands twice per day for signs of healthy nursing glands.
Look for signs of metritis during the first 24-48 hours following birth.
Look for signs of eclampsia during the first few weeks following birth.
Watch closely to ensure that your dog is attentive to her new pups.
Assist in the weaning process.
Offer stimulating toys.
