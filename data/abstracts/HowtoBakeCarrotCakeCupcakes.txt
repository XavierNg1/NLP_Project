
Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.
Start by measuring out all the ingredients into separate bowls.
Gather dry ingredients first.
Add the carrots, oil, eggs, and vanilla.
Still using the paddle, turn the speed down to a simple stir and add nuts.
Assemble three muffin tins lined with paper cupcake cups.
Place filled tins into the oven and bake for about 40 minutes or until the cakes look spongy and brown.

,
After pulling them from the oven, allow cakes to cool on a wire rack.

,
To make the icing, place cream cheese, butter, and vanilla extract in a large bowl.
Frost the cupcakes.
