
Consult an attorney.
Use a copyright template if desired.
Understand the rights you have and are willing to grant.
Start with your name or company name.
Name and/or describe the work.
Name the other person.
Make note of what permissions you grant.
Include a time frame.
Specify the format.
Define any royalties or fees to be paid.
Note that other copyrights stay with you.
Sign and date the document.
