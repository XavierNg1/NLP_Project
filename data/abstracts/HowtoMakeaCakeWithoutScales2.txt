
Before starting, turn on the oven to preheat at 360F/180C.
Arrange the pear slices on top of the maple syrup.

,
Make the cake batter.
Take the mixture off the heat immediately the butter melts.
Add in the beaten egg and mix well.
Toss in the flour and combine till there are no lumps.
Pour the batter over the sliced pears.
As soon as it cools down enough to handle, upturn the cake onto a serving platter.
Enjoy as is, or with cream or ice-cream.
