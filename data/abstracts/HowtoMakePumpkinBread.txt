
Preheat the oven to 375 °F (191 °C).
Using your sharpest knife, cut the pumpkin lengthwise, through the stem.
Scoop out all of the seeds and stringy bits inside the pumpkin.
Roast on a foil-lined baking sheet, face down, for 45 minutes.
When cooler, but still warm, scrape all of the flesh from the skin.
Pulse the pumpkin in a food processor or blender until it is a smooth, even consistency.
Preheat oven to 350 °F (177 °C) and grease a bread pan.
Mix together the flour, salt, cinnamon, baking soda, nutmeg, cloves, and ginger.
In a separate bowl, beat together butter and sugars with an electric mixer.
Keeping your mixer running, add the eggs one at a time and mix.
Whip in the pumpkin puree.
Slowly work in the flour, mixing the entire time.
Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan and cook for an hour.
Consider adding 1 cup of nuts or toasted pumpkin seeds for a little extra crunch.
Add dried fruit for an autumnal pumpkin bread.
Frost the bread with a cream cheese frosting or orange glaze.
Use flax meal in place of eggs to make a vegan pumpkin bread.
Finished.
