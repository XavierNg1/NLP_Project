
Start cooking your pasta.
Melt your butter in a large saucepan.

,
When fully melted, add flour and stir.
Add the milk, half a cup at a time.
Stir the milk until it begins to boil.

,
Turn off the burner and remove the milk from the stove.

,
Add the cheese.
If you haven't already, strain your pasta and put it back in the pot.
Add the sauce to the pasta.
Scoop into bowls or onto plates for a tasty side dish.
