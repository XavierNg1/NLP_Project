
Talk to the owner and/or landlord.
Talk to a lawyer.Before you sign any documents, talk to a business lawyer, preferably one who has dealt with cafe sales before.
Check the paperwork.Before buying a cafe, you’ll need to ensure that it conforms to all current zoning, environmental, and food safety rules.
Submit a letter of intent.A letter of intent is like a pre-contract.
Get a bill of sale.The bill of sale is the document that proves the previous owner sold the business to you.
Have the prior owner sign a non-compete agreement.A non-compete agreement (or “covenant not to compete”) states that the previous owner won’t open up another cafe nearby.
Don’t cancel the lease.
