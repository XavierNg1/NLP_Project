
Chop the fresh rhubarb into one-inch pieces, stopping about an inch from the leaves on top.
Combine all remaining ingredients in a mixing bowl and toss to coat.
Refrigerate the mixture for 20-30 minutes.

Cut the rhubarb into 1" pieces, discarding the leaves.
De-stem the strawberries, rinse, and cut them into slightly larger chunks than the rhubarb.
Rinse all of the fruit and combine with the remaining ingredients.
Chill the filling for 20-30 minutes before using.
