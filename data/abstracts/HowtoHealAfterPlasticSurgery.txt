
Know what to expect in terms of recovery.Some plastic surgery procedures only take days to recover from (the more minor ones), while others take weeks or even months.
Adhere to your plastic surgeon's recovery plan.It is key that you follow the specific instructions for recovery, which may include any of the following:


Medication use to control the pain and swelling, and to promote healing.
Consult your doctor about medication use.It is key to let your doctor know any regular medications or supplements that you generally take.
Be cautious and allow your body time to recover.
Plan your healing process in advance of your procedure by eating properly and getting plenty of rest.
Manage your expectations.Plastic surgery is generally done in hopes of altering one's appearance.
Seek out the support of family and friends.
