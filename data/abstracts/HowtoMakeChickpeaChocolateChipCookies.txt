
Gather your ingredients and supplies.
Open the can of chick peas and dump them into your colander or strainer.
Carefully portion out the salt, baking soda, and vanilla extract.
Grab your measuring cups and use the correct cups to measure out your peanut butter, milk, oats and brown sugar.
Blend the ingredients until the mixture is very smooth.
Empty the mixture into a mixing bowl, if you want to.

,
Add chocolate chips to the chickpea dough and stir until the two are evenly mixed together.

,
Put plastic wrap over the top of your food processor or the bowl you've moved the dough into.
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees when your dough is almost done chilling.
Ready your baking sheets.
Pull the dough out of the fridge and remove the plastic wrap.

,
Use a spoon to carefully roll a ball of the chickpea dough onto the cooking tray.
Wait for the oven to heat fully.
Allow the cookies to bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes, or until you have dark brown molasses.
Carefully transfer the cookies to a cooling rack.
Let the cookies cool for at least 20 minutes.
