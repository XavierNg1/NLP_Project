
Acquire two small, ripe avocados.
Buy ripe, ready-to-eat avocados if you plan to make avocado butter immediately.
Use one tablespoon of fresh-squeezed lemon juice.
Use four tablespoons of room-temperature butter.
Choose your seasonings.
Wash the fruit before preparing it.
Cut the avocado.
Pit the avocado.
Peel your avocados.
Mix and mash the avocado with the other ingredients.
Eat your avocado butter.
Store uneaten avocado butter in the fridge.
If you didn't add citric acid to the recipe, make sure to add it before storing.
