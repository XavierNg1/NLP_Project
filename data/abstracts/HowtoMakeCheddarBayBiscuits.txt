
Preheat your oven.
Mix all of the Bisquick, water, and cheese.
Flatten and cut your dough.
Place dough on an ungreased cookie sheet.
Melt the butter.
Add the parsley, garlic, and Italian seasoning.
Brush butter onto the dough.
Cook biscuits for eight to ten minutes.
Brush with leftover butter.
Serve immediately.
