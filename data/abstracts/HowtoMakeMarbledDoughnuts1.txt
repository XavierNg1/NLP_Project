
Have some doughnuts ready.
Sift the icing sugar into a large mixing bowl.
Stir in some milk with a whisk.
Add a few drops of food coloring.
Swirl the food coloring together using a clean toothpick.
Dip the doughnut into the icing.
Let the excess icing drip off before setting the doughnut onto a wire cooling rack.
Continue dipping the doughnuts into the icing, one by one.
Let the icing dry.
