
Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
Cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Mix in the eggs and vanilla extract.
Add in the flour and baking powder and continue mixing.
Stir in the milk, lemon juice, and lemon zest until everything is just combined.
Fill the cupcake liners two-thirds of the way with the batter.
Bake on the center rack for 20 to 25 minutes.
Let the cupcakes cool before decorating them.
Beat the butter until it turns smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes.
Mix in ¾ cup and (95 grams) of confectioners'/icing sugar.You will be adding the rest of the sugar later, because the consistency of the icing may change depending on what ingredients you add.

,
Add the heavy cream and vanilla extract.
Mix in the rest of the sugar, a little bit at a time, until the buttercream is thick and creamy.
Frost the cupcakes once they are completely cool.
Sift the confectioners'/icing sugar into a small mixing bowl.
Mix in 2 to 6 tablespoons of milk or water.
Stir in ½ teaspoon of vanilla or almond extract.
Whisk until the mixture is smooth and syrupy.
Drizzle or pour the icing over the cupcakes and let it set.
