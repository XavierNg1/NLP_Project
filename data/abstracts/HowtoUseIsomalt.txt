
Prepare a bowl of ice water.
Combine the isomalt and water.
Boil over high heat.
Add food coloring at 280 degrees Fahrenheit (82 degrees Celsius).
Cook until the syrup reaches 340 degrees Fahrenheit (171 degrees Celsius).
Dunk the bottom of the pan in ice water.
Keep the isomalt warm in your oven.
Place the nibs in a microwave-safe bowl.
Microwave on high, working in 15 to 20 second increments.You will need to stir the isomalt nibs after each increment to make sure that they are heating in an even, thorough manner.
Stir well.
Reheat as needed.
Coat the molds with cooking spray.
Transfer the syrup into a pastry bag, if desired.Only add about 1/2 cup (125 ml) of melted isomalt to the pastry bag.
Pour or squeeze the syrup into the molds.
Let the syrup set.
Use as desired.
