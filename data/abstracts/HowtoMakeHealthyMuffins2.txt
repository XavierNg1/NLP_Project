
Preheat your oven 425°F (220°C).
Mix together the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt.
Stir in the grated and chopped apple.
Beat together the oil and maple syrup in a separate bowl.
Add in the eggs, yogurt, applesauce, and vanilla, whisking well after each item you add.
Gently stir the wet mixture into the dry mixture with a rubber spatula.
Scoop the batter into the muffin tin.
Bake the muffins on the center rack in the oven for 13 to 13 minutes.
Let the muffins cool on a wire rack before serving.
