
Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C) and grease a doughnut pan.
Mix the dry ingredients, except for the sugar, together in a large bowl.
Mix the sugar and the wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
Stir the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
Separate the batter into four small bowls.
Stir food coloring into each bowl.
Transfer the batter into piping bags.
Pipe the red batter into each well.
Fill the doughnut pan with the remaining three colors.
Swirl the colors together.
Bake the doughnuts for 8 to 9 minutes.Allow the doughnuts cool for about 5 minutes in the pan, then take them out.
Prepare the glaze.
Dip the doughnuts into the glaze.
Let the icing dry for 1 hour before you serve the doughnuts.
