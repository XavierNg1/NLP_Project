
Preheat the oven and line muffin pans.
Place the cupcake ingredients in a mixing bowl.
Mix the cupcake batter and stir in some of the watermelon gelatin.
Fill the muffin pans and bake the cupcakes.
Combine the frosting with the gelatin and food coloring.
Whisk the whipped topping into the frosting.
Frost and decorate the quick watermelon cupcakes.
