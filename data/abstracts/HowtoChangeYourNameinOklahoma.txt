
Verify your eligibility to file to change your name in Oklahoma.
Determine where you will file your name change petition.
Estimate the fees and other costs.
Gather the information you'll need to compete the petition.
Choose the newspaper for publication and verify the cost of the notice.
Complete the application for legal name change.
Sign your petition and other documents with your current legal name in front of a Notary Public.
File your documents with the court clerk at the county courthouse.
Request a hearing date.
Publish notice of your intent to change your name.
Submit proof of publication to the court before your hearing.
Be on time and do not miss your court date.
Cooperate with courthouse security procedures.
Dress neatly and obey court rules.
Go to the front of the courtroom when your case is called and take a seat at the table.
Receive your name change order.
