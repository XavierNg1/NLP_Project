
Preheat the oven to 350ºF/180ºC.
Sift the flour into the mixing bowl.

,
Add the sugar and eggs.
Add water.
Divide then roll the dough into a sausage shape approximately 1.2cm/ 1/2" across.
Knot the biscuit dough.
Decide whether to use the boiling then baking method, or just the baking method alone.
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until they turn golden brown.

,
Remove from the oven.
Serve.
