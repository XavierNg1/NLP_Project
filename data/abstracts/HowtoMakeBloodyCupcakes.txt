
Prepare to make the cupcakes.
Cream the butter and sugar in a large mixing bowl.
Crack in the eggs, pour the vanilla, and add the red food coloring.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Pour in the buttermilk, baking soda, and vinegar.
Pour the cupcake batter into each case of the cupcake tray.

,
Bake the cupcakes for about 20 minutes, until the cupcakes rise and the edges are golden brown.

,
Have the cupcakes cool on a wire rack for about ten minutes, until cool enough to handle.

,
Make the frosting.
Frost all the cupcakes using the frosting pipe.

,
Make the candy glass mixture.
Pour the candy glass mixture on to a baking tray covered with parchment paper has been sprayed with non-stick greasing spray.
Crack the candy glass with a large, metal spoon by lightly banging on it.
Make the "blood".
Decorate the cupcakes.
Serve.
