
Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F (or 200 degrees C).
Make sure the pastry sheets are thawed; as this would make your job easier, and they won't break.
Place the baking sheets in the oven for about 10 to 15 minutes.
Get a large saucepan and mix the cornstarch, sugar, and custard powder.
Turn the heat off and add the vanilla and egg yolks.
