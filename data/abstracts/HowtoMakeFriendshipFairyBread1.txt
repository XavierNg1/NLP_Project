
Preheat the oven to 350 F or 180 C. Melt the butter in a saucepan.
Combine the white sugar, brown sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
Cut a shallow hole in the center of each biscuit with a small, sharp knife.
Dust or sprinkle a little of the sugar mixture onto the tops of every biscuit.
Drizzle a little of the cooled melted butter onto every biscuit in the individual wrappers, making sure "not to overcoat" each biscuit.
Bake the friendship fairy bread in the oven for about 5-8 minutes or until the sugar has melted onto the biscuits.
Combine powdered sugar, lemon juice and water in a bowl.
Drizzle the icing onto the fairy bread cups.
