
Build a water jacket.
Sterilize the equipment.
Put the smaller pot inside the pot of boiling water.
Use the thermometer to heat the milk to 185 degrees Fahrenheit (85 degrees Celsius).
Cool the milk's temperature down to 110 degrees Fahrenheit (43.33 degrees Celsius).
Pour the starter yogurt into the cooled milk.
Stir the milk and starter.
Remove the yogurt from the heating pad after 7 hours.
Pour the yogurt into containers.
Add lemon extract to the yogurt, and stir well to finish making lemon yogurt.
Finished.
