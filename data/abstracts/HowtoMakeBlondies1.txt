
Preheat the oven to to 350 degrees.
Butter and flour your baking dish.
Melt the butter.
Mix the butter into the sugar.
Whisk together the flour, salt, and baking powder in another bowl.
Whisk the egg and vanilla into the cooled sugar and butter mixture.
Fold the wet and dry ingredients together.
Pour the batter into the pan.
Cook the blondies for 20 minutes.
