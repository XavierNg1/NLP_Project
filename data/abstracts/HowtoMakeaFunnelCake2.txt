
Preheat your oven to 400ºF (204ºC).
Cook a 9 x 13" baking sheet with cooking spray.
Combine the water, butter, and salt in a medium saucepan.
Bring the ingredients to a boil.

,
Add the flour to the mixture.
Remove the mixture from the heat and let it cool for 10 minutes.

,
Add 4 eggs to the mixture, one at a time.
Spoon the dough into a large resealable plastic bag, Use scissors to snip a 1/4 to 1/2" hole in one corner of the bag.

,
Pipe the dough into twelve 3-4" circles on the baking sheet.
Bake the ingredients for about 20 minutes.
Sift 2 tbsp.
Serve.
