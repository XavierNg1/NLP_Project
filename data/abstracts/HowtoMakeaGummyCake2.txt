
Preheat the oven to 170ºC/325ºF.
Add the flour, cocoa powder and baking powder to the mixing bowl.
Spoon into the prepared cake pans.
Place in the oven.
Remove from the oven.
Warm the jam in a small saucepan.
Spread the warmed jam on one side of each cake.
Completely cover the cakes in frosting.
Decorate the cake with gummy candies.
Allow to set.
Serve.
