
Fill a large pot with 1 gallon (3.79 liters) of water and two tablespoons of salt and bring it to a boil.
Add the pasta and let it cook for the time specified on the package.
Drain the water from the pasta and rinse it under cold water.
Consider tossing the pasta with part of your dressing while it is still warm.
Leave the pasta in the refrigerator for one hour.
