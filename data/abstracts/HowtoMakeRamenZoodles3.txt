
Heat half of the oil in a skillet.
Rub the bok choy with the miso paste.
Saute the bok choy until it’s lightly charred.
Mix the remaining oil, green onion, ginger, onion, and garlic in the pan.
Stir in the stock and bring the mixture to a boil.
Lower the heat and simmer briefly.
Add the zucchini noodles and cook the mixture until they’re tender.
Divide the noodles between two bowls and pour the broth over them.
Top with the veggies and any other garnishes before serving.
