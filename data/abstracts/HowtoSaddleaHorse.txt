
Tie your horse.
Groom your horse.
Check for any sores or wounds that may cause discomfort under the saddle area.
Put on a saddle pad or blanket.
Proceed to saddle the horse.
Put the saddle in place.
Make sure the saddle is positioned properly.
Make sure the saddle fits correctly.
Secure the front (main) cinch.
Loosely secure the back (bucking) cinch.
Lead the horse forward five steps.
Attach the breast collar if you use one.
Mount up!,
Run up the stirrup irons on the stirrup leathers.
Detach the girth (or cinch) and set it aside.
Make sure the pad is lined up properly.
Place the saddle.
Replace the girth.
Drop your stirrups before mounting.
