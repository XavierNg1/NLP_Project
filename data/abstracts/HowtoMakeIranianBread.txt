
Mix together the yeast and the lukewarm water, then let stand for 5 minutes.
Combine the salt and flour, then add to the yeast and water mixture, mixing everything together thoroughly.

,
Knead the dough until it's very smooth.
Coat the inside of a bowl with about 1 tsp of oil.
Shape the dough.
Let the dough rest again.
Make the roomal.
Pre-heat the oven.
Create ridges in the dough.
Cover with roomal.
Place the dough on a baking sheet and bake for 15-18 minutes, or until golden brown.
