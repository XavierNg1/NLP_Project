
Get declined for a traditional business loan with a bank.
Schedule an appointment with your nearest Small Business Development Center (SBDC).
Complete all necessary paperwork.
Review your credit report by requesting a copy from all three credit agencies: Equifax, TransUnion and Experian.
Meet with your SBA banker to discuss SBA loans your business might qualify for.
Finalize your business plan and review your loan application package with your SBDC counselor to ensure viability of your application.
