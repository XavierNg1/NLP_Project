
Preheat the oven to 180ºC, 350ºF, Gas 4.
Beat the butter and sugar together.
Add the cherries, ginger, orange peel, flaked almonds, chopped walnuts and ginger to the bowl.
Sift in the flour.
Spoon the cookie dough in even blobs on the baking trays.
Place the cookies into the preheated oven.
Remove from the oven.
Place the flattened cookies back into the oven.
Remove from the oven and allow to cool in on the baking trays for 2 minutes.
Once completely cooled, spread half the cookies with the plain chocolate.
Serve.
