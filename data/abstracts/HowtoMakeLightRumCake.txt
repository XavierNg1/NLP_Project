
Preheat the oven and prepare the pan.
Coat the pan with finely ground pecans.
Beat most of the cake ingredients.
Stir in the light rum, vanilla, and the optional butter rum flavor.
Bake the light rum cake.
Combine most of the syrup ingredients.
Cook the syrup until it thickens.
Stir in the vanilla and poke holes in the cake.
Pour the light rum sauce over the cake.
Let the cake rest before you serve it.
