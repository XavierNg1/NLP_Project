
Gather the ingredients.
Prepare the baking pan or tin by either greasing it or lining it with parchment paper.
Add the butter to a mixing bowl.
Add the remaining ingredients to the beaten butter.
Transfer the dough to the pan or tin.
Remove the paper from the pressed dough.
Return the paper top and place in the refrigerator.
Place the shortbread into the oven.
Remove from the oven.
Allow to cool on a wire cooling rack.
