
Prepare the eggs.
Beat vigorously until the color and texture are uniform throughout.
Select the right sized pan for the number of eggs you will scramble.
Heat a pat of butter (2 teaspoons), bacon drippings or oil in a frying pan.
Flick some tap water onto the skillet (frying pan) from your fingertips to test the heat.
Watch that as the eggs start to cook, the mixture solidifies.
Look to see if your eggs are almost cooked.
