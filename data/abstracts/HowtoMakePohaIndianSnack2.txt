
Know that poha is a very customizable, versatile recipe.
Fry the potatoes ahead of time to make "batata poha.",
Add 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas, or chana for a heartier poha.
Try adding 1 cup of green peas for a more vegetable-based Poha.
Serve with a little bit of yogurt to counteract the spicy kick of hot poha.
