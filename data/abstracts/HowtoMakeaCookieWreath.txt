
Assemble the items needed.
Make the cookies, pierce two holes at the top of each cookie.Bake as recommended by the recipe.
Remove the baked cookies from the oven.
Arrange the cookie pattern you want to make into a wreath.
Thread the ribbon or other chosen thread through the holes of the cookies.Thread on as many as it takes to create a circle.Tie off the ribbon or other thread material with a knot.Hang on a tree, a door or other part of your house.
