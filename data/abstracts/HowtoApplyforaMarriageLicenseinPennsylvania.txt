
Make sure that you and your significant other meet the age requirements to get married in Pennsylvania.
Gather all required documents before you apply for your marriage license.
Be aware of special rules that may apply to your situation.
Plan to submit your marriage license application at least three days before your wedding.
Visit your county courthouse or district justice office to complete and submit the marriage license application.
Complete the application.
Pay the license application fee.
Pick up your marriage license when it is ready.
Get married within 60 days of receiving the marriage license.
