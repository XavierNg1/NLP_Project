
Preheat the oven and grease a cake pan.
Mix the cake mix according to the package instructions.
Bake the cake according to the package instructions.
Combine the sweetened condensed milk and caramel sauce.
Poke holes in the cake.
Add the caramel mixture to the cake.
Refrigerate the cake for several minutes.
Spread the frosting over the cake.
Sprinkle with pecans and chocolate chips.
Chill the cake for a couple of hours.
Drizzle the salted caramel sauce over the cake.
