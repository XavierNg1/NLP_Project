
Combine the oil, sugar, water, and vanilla essence in a big bowl.
Sift your dry ingredients into the mixture of wet ingredients.
Beat the mixture well.
Preheat the pressure cooker.
Grease the smaller tin.
Pour a little lemon juice on top of the cake batter.
Pour the batter into the greased tin.
Place the non-greased tin into the pressure cooker first.
Continue cooking the cake for 35 minutes.
Turn off the heat after 35 minutes.
Remove the cake from the pressure cooker.
Take out the cake.
