
Confirm your eligibility to marry.
Get parental consent.
Plan to apply for the license not less than three business days before the wedding.
Plan a wedding in Alaska that will take place not more than three months after you apply for your license.
Secure the necessary information for the application.
Download and fill out the marriage license application.
Bring the application to the Bureau of Vital Statistics office or Alaska court closest to the site of the ceremony.
Pick up the marriage license after three days.
Wed before an officiant and two witnesses.
