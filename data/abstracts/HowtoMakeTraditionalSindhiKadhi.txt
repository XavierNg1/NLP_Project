
Wash and soak kokum in warm water.
Heat oil in a pan.
Add gram flour.
Add half a litre of water and turmeric powder.

,
Allow it to boil for 2-3 mins.

,
Add salt.

,
Add the potatoes and lotus stalk (since these veggies take time to soften).

,
Add the french beans, green peas, cluster beans, and cauliflower florets.

,
Smash the kokum with soft hands.
Add the eggplant and okra; they may get smashed if added earlier.
Heat 2 tsp of oil and add red chili powder.
Serve.
