
Preheat your oven to 300 degrees F (148.8 C).
Separate the eggs.
Combine the egg yolks, cream cheese and honey.
Add cream of tartar to the egg whites.
Whip until stiff peaks form.
Combine the egg white mixture with the egg yolk mixture.
Fold the ingredients together.
Prepare two baking sheets.
Scoop the mixture onto the baking sheet.
Bake the bread in the oven for 30 minutes.
Place the bread on a cooling rack.
Serve and enjoy.
