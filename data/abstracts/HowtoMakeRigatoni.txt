
Brown the sausage in a saucepan.
Drain the grease from your meat.
Pour your sauce in with your sausage.
Add the cheese to your sauce and meat.
Boil the rigatoni noodles until slightly under cooked.
Sauce your pan.
Layer your noodles in a pan.
Cover your noodles with your meat/sauce mixture.
Bake at 350 °F (177 °C) until the cheese melts.
Garnish your rigatoni.
Allow to cool and serve.
