
Add the butter and soft sugar in a bowl.
Pour in the maple syrup.
Separate the egg white and yolk.
Add the flour.
Wrap the dough in plastic kitchen wrap or greaseproof paper.
While the dough is chilling, make the sugar coating for the cookies.
Remove from the refrigerator.
Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
Cut out the heart cookies.
Cut out the middle of the cookies.
Continue until all the dough is used up.
Beat the egg white briefly for a few seconds.
Bake the cookies.
Remove from the oven.
Serve when cooled.
