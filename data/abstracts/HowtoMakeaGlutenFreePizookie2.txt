Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C).
Grease an 8-inch (20.32-centimeter) cast iron skillet.
Mix together your dry ingredients.
Cream the butter and sugars.
Add the vanilla extract and unsweetened applesauce into the butter mixture.
Blend the dry ingredients and wet ingredients together.
Stir in the chocolate chips with a rubber spatula.
Transfer the batter into the prepared pan.
Bake the pizookie for 27 to 30 minutes.
Garnish the pizookie with dairy-free ice cream.
