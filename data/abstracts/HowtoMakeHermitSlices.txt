
Preheat the oven.
Line the baking sheets.
Combine the flour, spices, salt, and baking soda.
Beat the brown sugar and butter together.
Mix in the egg.
Blend in the vanilla and molasses.
Add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture.
Fold in the nuts and raisins.
Divide the dough and roll it into logs.
Place the logs on the cookie sheet and shape them.
Bake the logs until they are firm.
Melt the remaining butter and cook it until it browns.
Add the powdered sugar and some water.
Brush the glaze over the logs and allow them to cool completely.
Cut the logs into bars.
