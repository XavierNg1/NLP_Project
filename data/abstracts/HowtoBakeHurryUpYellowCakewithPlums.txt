
Stir the cake flour and sugar together in a large bowl.
Add in all the remaining ingredients except for the plums.
Halve and de-pit the plums.
Lay the plums face-down in the 9x13" cake pan and pour the batter over them.
Bake the cake at 350F for 30 minutes.
Ice the cake if you have an inclination for such, and serve with vanilla ice cream.
