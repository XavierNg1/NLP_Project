
Preheat the oven to 350ºF/180ºC.
Add the vanilla, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and butter.
Add the flour, salt, cocoa powder, and baking soda.

,
Mix until fully blended; add the milk to achieve a smoothly blended dough.
Apply the cooking spray to the pan so that the cookies won't stick to the baking pan.
Roll a small ball for each cookie.

,
Place the cookie balls on the baking pan.

,
Flatten each cookie with a fork.
Put cookies in the oven for about 8-10 minutes.
Take the cookies out of the oven and let them sit on a wire cooling rack for about 15 minutes to cool.
Store in an airtight container, or eat the cookies once they've cooled.

,
Finished.
