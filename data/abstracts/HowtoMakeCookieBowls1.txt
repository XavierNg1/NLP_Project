
Preheat the oven and grease the muffin tins.
Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
Mix the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar.
Stir in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla.
Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
Mix in the chocolate chips and chill the dough.
Press the dough onto the inverted muffin tin.
Bake the bowls until the edges are brown.
Cool the bowls and remove them from the tin to fill.
