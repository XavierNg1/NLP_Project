
Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
Put the margarine and sugar into the bowl of a standing mixer.
Combine the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
Add the dry ingredients mixture and almond milk/vinegar to the bowl.
Grate the butternut squash intro shreds.
Pour the batter into the paper cupcake cups.
Bake the cupcakes for 15 to 20 minutes.
Make the frosting while the cupcakes are baking.
Frost the cupcakes.
