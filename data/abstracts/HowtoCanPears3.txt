
Begin boiling water in a boiling-water canner.
Place a wide-mouth funnel into each jar as you fill it.
Scoop the pears into the jars.
Ladle syrup over the pears in the jars.
Remove any air bubbles.
Clean off the tops of the jars.
Place the lids on the jars.
Carefully lower the jars into the boiling water in the canner.
Process the canned pears until sealed.
Remove the jars from the water.
Allow the jars to cool before checking the seal.
Store the canned pears.
