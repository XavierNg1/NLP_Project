
Empty the crate of all items.
Use a clean cloth to wipe the crate clean, including the liner.
Close all the doors on the crate.
Consult the owner's manual for your particular crate model.
Fold down the shorter side panels of the crate.
Detach the sides of the crate.
Lift the top up slightly and push the side in.
Push one of the long ends towards the floor.
Latch the folded crate.
