
Melt the fat.
Whisk in the flour.
Stir the roux as it cooks.
Brown the chicken.
Saute onions, bell peppers, and celery.
Add and saute garlic, parsley, and sausage.
Put the chicken and the stock in the pot.
Add the roux and cayenne pepper.
Remove the chicken and cook the gumbo for 30 more minutes.
Return the chicken and cook the gumbo for 20 more minutes.
Season and serve the basic gumbo.
