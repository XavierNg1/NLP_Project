
Preheat the oven to 375ºF (190ºC).
Beat together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Add the eggs, one at a time.
Mix in the bananas and vanilla extract.
Sift together the flour, baking powder, and baking soda in a separate bowl.
Add the flour mixture and buttermilk to the butter mixture a little bit at a time.
Fill the cupcake liners two-thirds of the way with the batter.
Bake on the middle rack for 18 to 22 minutes.
Let the cupcakes cool.
Frost the cupcakes with your favorite frosting.
