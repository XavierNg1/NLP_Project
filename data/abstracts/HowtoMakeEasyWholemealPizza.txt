
Preheat the oven to 220ºC/425ºF/Gas Mark 7.;
,
Mix the flour and margarine.
Stir in the milk.
Make the dough into a ball.
Roll out the dough.
Spread the tomato purée over the base.
Sprinkle the grated cheese over the pizza.
Add any toppings you would like.
Check you are happy with what you've added, then put your pizza in the oven.

,
Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbling and just starting to brown.

,
Enjoy your healthy, homemade pizza!
