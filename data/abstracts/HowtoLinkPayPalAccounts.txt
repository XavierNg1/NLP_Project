
Navigate to https://www.paypal.com/signin.
Sign in using the account you want to close.
Click “Pay or Send Money”.
Select “Send money to friends or family”.
Enter the contact info of the PayPal account you wish to keep.
Click “Next”.
Enter the full balance of your PayPal account.
Click “Continue”.
Ensure PayPal balance is the selected payment method.
Click “Send Money Now”.
Wait for the transaction to clear.
Go to https://www.paypal.com/myaccount/settings/.
Click “Close your account”.
Select a reason for closing your account.
Click “Continue”.
