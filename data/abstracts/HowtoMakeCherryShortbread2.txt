
Preheat the oven.
Combine the flour and sugar.
Cut in the butter.
Mix in the cherries, almond extract, and some of the white chocolate.
Knead the dough and shape into balls.
Flatten the balls and bake until the cookies are set.
Melt the remaining white chocolate and shortening.
Cool the cookies.
Dip half of the cookies into the melted chocolate and allow to set.
