
Melt 2 cups(450 g) semisweet, milk or dark chocolate chips slowly in double boiler or in heat safe container in the microwave, stirring every 20 seconds.
Make sure butter is at room temperature so it will disperse properly, add 8 tbsp.
Stir well to make sure the butter and chocolate are combined, and pour into to a large mixing bowl.
Drop in 2 tsp.
Cover with plastic wrap and let the frosting cool in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before spreading.
Use this recipe to make 4 cups of frosting.
