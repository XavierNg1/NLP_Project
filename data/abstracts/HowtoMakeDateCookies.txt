
Cream the butter and sugar together.
Add the eggs to the mixture one at a time.
Stir in the vanilla.
Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
Mix the dry ingredients into the butter mixture.
Add the nuts and dates to the batter.
Chill the dough.
Preheat the oven.
Line two baking sheets for the cookies.
Scoop the cookie batter onto the baking sheets.
Bake the cookies until the edges are golden brown.
Let the cookies cool before serving.
