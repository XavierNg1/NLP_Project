
When the customer approaches your till point, greet them nicely.
Apologise for any wait, no matter how short.
Announce the deals or specials.
Ask the customer if they found everything they are looking for.
Offer a bag with their purchase.
Ask the customer how they would like to pay for their purchase.
Your last chance for sales; Ask the customer if they found everything they needed today.
Be thankful.
Say "Goodbye".
You're ready for your next customer.
