
Bake the cakes needed.Allow the cakes to cool fully before frosting.

Cut the 2 cupcakes into halves.
Frost the pudding bowl cake.
Frost the uncut sides of the cupcake feet.
While the frosting is still dry, push the feet firmly against the already frosted body of the turtle.
Attach the head at the front as well.
Push the tail into the back of the cake.
Make turtle shell marks across the green part of the cake.
