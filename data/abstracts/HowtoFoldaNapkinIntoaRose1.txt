
Unfold the napkin completely.
Hold the upper left corner between your fingers.
Wrap the napkin around your fingers.
Fold the flap in the upper right corner diagonally to the top edge.
Wrap the corner around your fingers, and hold under your thumb.
Twist the base of the rose tightly to form the stem.
Find a corner at the bottom of the napkin and pull it gently so it sticks out.
Display your rose, or give it to a special someone to make them smile.
