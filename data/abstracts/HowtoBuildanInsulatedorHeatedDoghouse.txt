
Determine the size of the doghouse: Measure your dogs length, then add 12” to determine the width of the doghouse.
Once you have determined the size the doghouse, begin by cutting the floor joists using the 2x4's.
Cut ¾ plywood to fit over floor frame.
Now to build the sidewalls.
Screw sidewall to the floor using 1 ¼ “ exterior screws.
Following the manufactures instructions for cutting hole for the dog door.
Cut 1” and ½ “ foam insulation board to fit inside walls, The 1” and ½ “ foam should be flush with the 2x2's on the interior.
Build the roof.
Shingle the roof.
Drill a hole in the back of the doghouse in the corner, down near the floor.
