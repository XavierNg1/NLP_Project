
Preheat the oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit (230 degrees Celsius).
Melt the butter in a saucepan.
Cook the shallot, garlic, and lemon zest.
Add the lemon juice.
Transfer the fish to your prepared baking sheet.
Drizzle with prepared butter mixture.
Sprinkle with minced dill.
Bake for 12 to 17 minutes.
Serve warm.
