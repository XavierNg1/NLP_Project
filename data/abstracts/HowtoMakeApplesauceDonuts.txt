
Set aside 2 cups of flour.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Add the eggs and applesauce.
Blend the ingredients with an electric mixer.
Add the remaining 2 cups of flour.
Refrigerate the dough.
Prepare a pastry board.
Heat the vegetable oil.
Divide the dough.
Roll out the dough.
Cut the donuts.
Fry the donuts.
Remove the donuts from the oil.
Sprinkle the donuts with sugar.
