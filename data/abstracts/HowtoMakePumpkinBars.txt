
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
Beat the eggs until they turn pale and foamy.
Mix in the sugar, oil, and pumpkin purée.
Add the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt.
Spread the batter in a lightly-greased baking pan.
Bake the pumpkin bars for 25 to 30 minutes.
Cool the pumpkin bars on a wire cooling rack for 2 hours.
Mix together the cream cheese, vanilla extract, and butter.
Beat in the confectioners' sugar 1 cup (125 grams) at a time.
Test the consistency and flavor of the icing.
Us a rubber spatula to spread a thick layer of icing over the pumpkin bars.
Add some extra flavor or texture.
Wait for the icing to set, then cut the sheet into bars.
Mix together the confectioners' sugar and spices in a large bowl.
Stir in the melted butter, vanilla extract, and 1½ (22.5 milliliters) of water.
Test the consistency.
Pour the glaze on top of the bars.
Wait 1 hour for the glaze to set, then cut the bars.Take them out of the pan, and serve them on a plate.
