
Set your oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.To ensure that your oven is hot and ready to bake the Walleye fillets, turn it on and set it to 425 degrees Fahrenheit before you start preparing the fillets.
Create your stuffing.
Cut your Walleye in half and then stuff it.
Bake your Walleye for about 30 minutes at 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
