
Powder your spices (orange peel, cinnamon, nutmeg).
Take 3 tablespoons of sugar.
Transfer all of the fruits and nuts into a big bowl.
Combine the baking soda, baking powder, and cocoa powder with the all-purpose flour.
Heat the remaining sugar in a pan.
Stir the water and sugar over low to medium heat.
Beat the eggs in a bowl.
Beat the butter in a bowl.
Add the flour mix to the butter, eggs, and powdered sugar.
Pour in the sugar syrup.
Add the nuts and fruits.
Place a standing set in your aluminum pressure cooker.
Grease the cake mold.
Pour the cake batter into the cake mold.
Place the cake mold into your pressure cooker.
Cook the cake for 50 minutes.
Turn off the heat.
