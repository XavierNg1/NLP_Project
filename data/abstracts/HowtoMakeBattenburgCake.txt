
Preheat the oven to 400ºF, 200ºC, or gas mark 6.

,
Make the batter.
Create the "pink" batter.
Use extra butter or grease to grease the cake tin.
Pour the colored batter into one side of the tin and pour the non-colored batter into the other side.

,
Bake the cake for 25 to 30 minutes or until it feels "springy".
Cut the cake in half to divide the colors from each other.
Measure lengthwise and cut the cakes in half.

,
Add apricot jam and water to a pot.
Create the checkerboard effect of the cake.
Brush the entire outside of the cake with more jam.

,
Measure and cut the marzipan so it's a little bigger or longer than the cake.
Use a knife or scissors to cut off the extra marzipan.
Place the cake in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes before serving.

,
Serve.
Get a bowl and combine the egg, almond extract, lemon juice, almonds, and confectioners' sugar.
Mix and knead the mixture until it's a smooth paste.
Use confectioners' sugar to dust the paste and "working space".
Finished.
