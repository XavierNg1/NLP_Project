
Combine the soy sauce and black vinegar.
Mix in the sugar.
Stir in the salt.
Cut the stalks from the cabbage root.
Remove the leafy tips of the stalks.
Rinse and drain the stalks.
Cut the stalks into strips and separate the stem and leafy sections.
Heat the oils.
Stir fry the crushed red pepper and ginger.
Mix in the red pepper and rice wine.
Add the cabbage stems and carrots.
Toss in the leafy cabbage sections and dressing.
Transfer the slaw to a dish and serve.
Finished.
