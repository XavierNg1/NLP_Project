
Preheat the oven and grease the pan.
Combine the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
Mix the peanut butter and sugars.
Stir in the egg and vanilla.
Fold in the bananas.
Add the flour mixture.
Pour the batter into the greased pan.
Bake the bread until it’s golden brown.
Allow the bread to cool completely.
Combine the peanut butter, butter, and confectioners’ sugar.
Heat the peanut butter mixture in the microwave.
Spread the glaze over the bread and top it with chopped nuts.
Finished.
