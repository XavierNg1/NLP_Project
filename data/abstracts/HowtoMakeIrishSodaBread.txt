
Preheat the oven to 190ºC (375ºF or gas mark 5).
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Gather your ingredients and cooking supplies.
Pour the dry ingredients into the mixer.
Whilst still mixing, add the egg and the buttermilk.
Shape the dough into a ball and place it onto the pre-lined baking tray.
Place the tray into the centre of the preheated oven and bake for 40 minutes.
After 40 minutes, remove the bread from the oven.
Cut it in slices or along the "X" into four equal parts to serve.

,
Finished.
