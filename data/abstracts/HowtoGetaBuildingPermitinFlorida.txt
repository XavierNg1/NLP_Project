
Begin by downloading a permit application.
Fill out the applicant information.
Select the permit type.
Select the type of permit request.
Provide the property type.
State the total value of the work and square footage of property.
Provide a description of the work.
Provide names of the responsible parties.
Sign the forms.
Visit the building department.
Submit the forms with plans.
Pay the upfront fee in exchange for processing number.
Wait for the City to conduct a plan review.
Fix problems with plans if necessary.
Know that your plan may have to go through a number of reviews.
Know the role of external agencies.
Know the consequences of plan rejection.
Visit the Building Department in the city in which the property is located.
Pay the remaining fee.
Collect the permit.
