
Pre-heat the oven to Gas Mark 4 (180°C/350°F).
Grease a rectangular bread tin.
Measure out your ingredients.
Mix until the sugar and butter is smooth and creamy.

,
Add some of the flour and one of the eggs.
Keep adding more flour and one egg at a time until the mixture is smooth and fairly stiff.
Spoon the cake mixture into the tin.
Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes.

,
Check the cake around 10 minutes before the estimated time by inserting a fork.
Begin to make the icing.
Finished.
