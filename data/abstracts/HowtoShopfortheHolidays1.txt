
Create a budget for your holiday shopping.
Keep a list of all the people you want to give presents to with you for 6 months before Christmas.
Use a reward points, credit card, or a mileage plan to buy presents.
Buy expensive gifts on layaway, starting a few months in advance.
Spread your remaining holiday shopping evenly among the holiday weeks.
Shop online as well as in stores.
Go to BlackFriday.com to research Black Friday before it happens.
Shop Cyber Monday for your electronics deals.
Make something, if you cannot find something.
