
Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
Prepare your baking dish.
Mix the eggs and milk together in a large bowl.
Chop the parsley.
Dice the cooked ham.
Add the cheese, ham, and parsley to the egg mixture.
Pour the mixture into the baking dish.
Put the dish into the oven.
Take the omelette out of the oven when it is finished.
Serve the omelette.
