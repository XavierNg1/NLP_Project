
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
Mix the dry ingredients.
Beat the egg.
Mix the wet ingredients.
Add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix.
Grease the muffin pan or insert muffin wrappers.
Pour the batter into the muffin pans.
Put extra chocolate chips and sparkling sugar on top.
Bake the muffins 18-22 minutes.
Wait for the muffins to cool.
Place the muffin on a cooling rack.
Serve the muffins.
Store uneaten muffins in a closed container.
