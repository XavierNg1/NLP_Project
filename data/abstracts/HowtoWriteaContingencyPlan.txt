
Prepare to write your contingency plan.
Make sure the plan answers the three key questions of contingency plans.
Figure out the risks that are most likely to occur for your specific organization.
Prioritize the risks.
Develop scenarios for the highest-ranked risks.
Create a timeline for how the scenarios might unfold.
Decide what will be most essential to get your business operational again.
Find ways to reduce risk.
Communicate about the plan to all employees.
Test your contingency plan.
Store the plan in a place where it can be easily accessed.
Revisit the plan on a regular schedule.
