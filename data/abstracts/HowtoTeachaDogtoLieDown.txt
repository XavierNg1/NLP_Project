
Have a yummy treat ready.
Get the dog to sit.
Say the cue “Down”.
Lure the dog into a lying down position.
Put your hand gently on his shoulder.
Try this trick if he’s reluctant.
”Catch” him in the behavior.
Praise the dog and let him eat the treat immediately when he lies down.
Give a release command.
Repeat.
Practice the steps above twice per day.
Start using an empty hand but still giving a treat.
Start shrinking the hand signal.
Continue shrinking the hand signal.
Take it on the road.
Start using fewer treats.
Give “life rewards”.
Keep training sessions short and sweet.
Use consistent, positive reinforcement.
Work on one part of a new skill at a time.
Practice everywhere and with everyone.
End on a good note.
