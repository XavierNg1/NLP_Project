
Slice the tops off of each garlic head and drizzle with olive oil.
Heat 2 tbsp.
Add onions to hot butter and oil and allow to cook for approximately 10 minutes on medium heat.
Add cooked garlic to pan.
Pour in chicken broth and add herbs and thyme.
Add diced bread to broth mixture.
Remove herbs from pan and allow broth to cool.
Blend soup on high until all ingredients are well combined.
Return soup to saucepan and cook on medium heat.

,
Add sour cream and salt and pepper to taste as soup warms.

,
Finished.
