
Prepare to make the cupcakes.
Cream the butter, sugar, and vanilla.
Mix the dry ingredients.
Pour the dry ingredients with the wet ingredients.
Scoop the batter into the cupcake tray.
Bake in the oven.
Have the cupcakes cool.
Separate the frosting.
Add pink food coloring to the first bowl.
Add light blue food coloring in the second bowl.
Place both frostings in the frosting pipe.
Top the cupcakes off with cotton candy.Enjoy!
