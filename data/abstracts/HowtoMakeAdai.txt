
Soak the rice, pigeon peas, chickpeas and lentils separately in water.
Grind the soaked ingredients together with the asafoetida and chilis.
Mix in the onions, grated coconut, curry leaves and salt.
Heat a teaspoon of cooking oil in a frying pan.
Pour a ladle of batter in the frying pan.
Flip the pancake.
Cook the adai until the other side turns golden brown.
Experiment with different varieties of adai.
