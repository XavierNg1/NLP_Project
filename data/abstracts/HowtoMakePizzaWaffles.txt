
Dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water.
Stir in flour, salt, and oil.
Mix the ingredients together.
Let the dough rest for five minutes.
Buy store-bought pizza dough.
Roll out your pizza dough.
Spread your marinara sauce on the dough.
Place your cheese on the pizza waffle.
Add your favorite pizza toppings.
Create another layer of pizza and sauce.
Add the dough “cover.” After you’ve added all of your cheese, sauce, and toppings, you’ll need to roll out another flat piece of dough.
Close the waffle iron.
Remove your waffle and serve.
