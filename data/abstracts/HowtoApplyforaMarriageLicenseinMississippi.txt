
Confirm that you and your fiancé are legally entitled to marry one another.
Collect the necessary documentation.
Decide whether or not you want to change your last name.
Find a place to apply for your marriage license.
Go to the appropriate circuit court.
Apply for a marriage license by completing the required information.You’ll need to include your full names, names and addresses of your parents (including the maiden name of your mother), age, date of birth, place of birth, highest level of education completed, number of previous marriages, and a description of why previous marriages ended.
Pay the appropriate fee of approximately $21 (prices may vary).
Wait 3 days for your license.
