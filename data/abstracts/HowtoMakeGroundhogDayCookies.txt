
Sift the following ingredients into a bowl: the flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, and three spices.
Cream the butter and sugar.
Stir the sifted mixture into the butter mixture.
Form into a dough ball.
Preheat the oven to 350ºF/180ºC.
Pull off a small piece of dough and place it on plastic food wrap.
Roll over the plastic wrap to flatten to the dough to about 1⁄8 inch (0.3 cm) thickness.

,
Cut out the cookies.
Arrange the cookies on the cookie sheet.
Place in the oven.
Leave the cookie sheet to cool slightly before removing the cookies.

,
Finished.
