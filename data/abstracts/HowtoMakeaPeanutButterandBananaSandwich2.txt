
Pre-heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
Spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter onto each slice of bread.
Peel and slice a banana.
Place the banana slices onto one of the peanut buttered bread slices.
Place the other piece of bread, peanut buttered side down, on top.
Fry the sandwich, turning once, until it is golden brown on both sides.
Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
