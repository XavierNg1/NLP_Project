
Make as many layers as you like.Pour the cold water into a saucepan.Add the jelly or jello crystals to the cold water.Add the gelatine powder.
Set aside for 10 minutes.
Turn the heat to medium.
Pour the liquid jelly or jello into the baking tin or pan.
Place it in the freezer to set.
Prepare the next jelly mix as for layer 1.Repeat until you've added all the desired layers.
Carefully turn the baking tin or pan upside down over a large cake plate.Slice in the same way as for a flour baked cake.
