
Use filling amounts appropriate for the number of tacos you'll be making.
Add Nutella to a double boiler.
Dip the edges of the taco shells in the melted Nutella.
Place the Nutella-dipped tacos into the freezer.
Cut the fruit while the tacos are setting.
Remove the tacos from the freezer.
Add fruit pieces to each taco.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar for garnish.
Serve.
