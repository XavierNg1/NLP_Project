
Prepare the oven and tray.
Combine the flour, baking soda, and cornstarch with a whisk.
Cream the butter, sugar, vanilla, and pudding mix.
Add the flour mixture.
Add the chocolate chips and blend again until combined well.
Separate the cookie dough.
Place the cookie dough on the baking tray using a mini ice cream scoop.
Bake the cookies.
Let the cookies cool.
Serve and enjoy!
