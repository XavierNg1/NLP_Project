
Gather your ingredients.
Sift the powdered sugar into a bowl.
Separate the egg whites into a separate bowl.
Combine lemon juice and egg whites and mix briefly with a wire whisk.
In a stand mixer, combine the lemon juice and egg white mixture with the sugar, adding the sugar a little at a time.
For icing to be used as piping, mix the royal icing until soft peaks begin forming.
Continue mixing and adding sugar, 2 teaspoons at a time, for firmer peaks.
Adjust as necessary with sugar, water, or egg whites.
Use royal icing immediately or transfer to an airtight container.
