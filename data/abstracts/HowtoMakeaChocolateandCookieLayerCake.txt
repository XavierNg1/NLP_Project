
Preheat the oven to 350 °F (177 °C).
Sift the dry ingredients.
Mix in the wet ingredients.
Stir in the boiling water with a spoon.
Pour the cake batter into the two pans.
Allow the cake to bake for 30 to 35 minutes.Allow the cakes to cool for 10 minutes.
Remove the cakes from their pans.
Preheat the oven to 350 °F (177 °C).
Cream the butter.Blend in the brown sugar on medium speed until smooth.
Combine in the wet ingredients.
Sift together the dry ingredients.
Slowly combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients.Fold in the chocolate chips.
Press the cookie dough into the cake pan.
Bake the cake for 20 to 25 minutes.
Place the cake pan on a wire rack and allow it to cool.
Make the chocolate frosting.
Prepare the cookie dough frosting.
Remove the cookie cake from the pan.Frost the top of the cookie cake with the chocolate frosting.
Stack one of the chocolate cakes on top of the frosted cookie cake.Place the second chocolate cake on top of the frosted chocolate cake.
Frost the rest of the cake with the frosting of your choice.
Garnish the cake.
Serve.
