
Bring a large pot of water to a simmer on the stove.
Add the rennet to the water.
Add the citric acid powder to the water.
Pour the milk into a pot.
Pour the water in with the dissolved citric acid into the milk.
Heat the mixture until it reaches 88ºF (31ºC).
Add the water with the dissolved rennet to the milk mixture.
Take the pot off the stove and let it stand undisturbed for 15 minutes.
Cut the curd.
Place a colander or a piece of cheesecloth over a bowl.
Prepare the curd.
Pour some water from the simmering pot into a large bowl.
Transfer the curd to the hot water.
Remove the curd from the water.
Shape the mozzarella cheese.
Make the brine.
Store the cheese.
