
Choose a valid reason for changing your name.
Review the information on how to petition to change your name in Arkansas.
Write your petition for change of name.
Format your petition.
Take your completed petition to a notary public.
Complete the Cover Sheet for Civil Court.
Print an “Order of Name Change.” This is a document a judge will sign to approve your name change.
Take the petition and 3 copies to your local county court.
Pay the filing fee.
Go to your local Social Security office.
Apply for a name change on your driver's license.
Contact Arkansas Vital Records to request information about changing your name on your birth certificate.
Change your name on all other documents and accounts.
