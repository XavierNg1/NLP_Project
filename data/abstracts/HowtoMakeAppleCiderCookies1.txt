
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line your baking sheets with parchment paper.
Mix together the flour, baking soda, salt, ground pecans, and spices.
Cream together the sugar and butter in a separate bowl.
Add the egg into the butter mixture and beat again.
Add the flour mixture in two parts, beating well after each addition.
Beat in the apples and apple cider using a low speed setting.
Drop the batter onto the prepared baking sheets using a tablespoon.
Bake the cookies for 20 to 25 minutes.Check the cookies at 20 minutes first, then decide whether you need to bake them longer.
Transfer the cookies to a wire cooling rack.
Allow the cookies to cool for a few minutes before serving them.
