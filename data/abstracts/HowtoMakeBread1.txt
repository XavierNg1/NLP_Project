
Assemble your ingredients.
Activate the yeast.
Mix the flour and salt together thoroughly in a large mixing bowl.
Add the water, mixing thoroughly.
Turn the dough out on a well-floured kneading surface.
Knead the dough.
Let the dough rise for about 3 hours.
Punch the dough down, turn it out, and reform it.
Let the dough rise again for about 90 minutes.
Form the dough into whatever shape you choose.
Let the dough proof for at least 45 minutes.
Bake at 400 degrees F for about 30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
Crust the bread up with steam.
