
Open the Apple Watch app on your iPhone.
Tap the "My Watch" tab and select "Passbook & Apple Pay.",
Tap "Add Credit or Debit Card" and follow the prompts.
Double-tap the side button on your watch to launch Apple Pay when you want to pay with it.
Swipe left and right to select the card you want to use.
Hold you watch face close to the reader until it vibrates.
