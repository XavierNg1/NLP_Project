
Open your device's Settings.

Tap Photos.
Tap iCloud.
Scroll down and tap Keychain.
Slide the "iCloud Keychain" button to the "On" (right) position.
Tap iCloud.

Tap iCloud.

Slide the button next to "iCloud Backup" to the "On" (right) position.
