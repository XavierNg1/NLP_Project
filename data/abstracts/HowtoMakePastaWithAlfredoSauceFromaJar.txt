
Decide how much pasta you need.
Fill a tall pot with water and bring it to a boil over high heat.
Add the pasta to the boiling water.
Cook the pasta for the amount of time indicated on the package.
Remove the pasta from heat.
Figure out how much Alfredo sauce to use.
Pour the sauce into a medium pan or pot.
Heat the sauce over medium heat.
Add the pasta to the sauce.
Stir it together and cook for three more minutes.
Scoop the pasta and sauce onto a plate.
Add toppings if desired.
Enjoy with bread and salad.
