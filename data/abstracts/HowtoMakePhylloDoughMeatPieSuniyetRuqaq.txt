
Grease a large ovenproof dish with butter.
Divide the phyllo dough into two equal parts.
Cover the bottom of the dish with one sheet of phyllo dough.
Cook the onions, ground beef and spices in a frying pan.
Cover with the second half of the dough.
After 10 minutes, spoon the chicken stock and egg glaze over the ruqaq.
Remove from the oven when baked.
