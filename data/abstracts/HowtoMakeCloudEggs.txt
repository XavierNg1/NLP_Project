
Preheat the oven and prepare a baking sheet.
Separate 4 egg whites and yolks.
Season and beat the egg whites.
Fold in the cheese, chives, and ham (optional).
Make 4 egg white mounds.
Indent the egg whites and bake them for 3 minutes.
Place an egg yolk on each egg white mound.
Season and bake the cloud eggs.
