
In a mixing bowl, or the bowl of an electric mixer, combine all of the ingredients to make a dough; everything but the egg wash and the optional ingredients -- you don't want to knead your mix-ins to a pulp!,


Proof for at least 1 hour covered.
Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface when the dough has doubled in size.
Chill the dough, allowing it to rise again.
Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
Place your dough into a greased baking tin.
Glaze with egg wash using a pastry brush.
Bake until golden and cooked through for 30-40 minutes.
Serve.
