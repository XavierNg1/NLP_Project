
Preheat oven to 400ºF/200ºC.
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and sugar and whisk to combine.
Form dough into a disc 1-inch (2.54 cm) thick and place on your baking sheet.
Bake at 400ºF/200ºC for 15-18 minutes, until lightly browned.

,
Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
