
Remove the bottom from an aluminum can.
Empty and clean the can.
Coat the can with cooking spray.
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (177 degrees Celsius).
Combine the egg white and sugar.
Mix in the vanilla and butter.
Add the remaining dry ingredients.
Stir in the milk.
Pour the batter into the can.
Bake for 20 minutes.
Cool completely.
Slide the cake out of the can.
Slice the cake into layers.
Clean the can again.
Place sprinkles in the bottom of the can.
Squeeze frosting into the can.
Slide the first layer back into the can.
Alternate the remaining layers with additional frosting.
Tape the bottom back on.
Decorate the outside of the can as desired.
Enjoy.
Finished.
