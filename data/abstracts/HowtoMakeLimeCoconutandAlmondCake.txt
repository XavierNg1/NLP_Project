
Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
Separate the eggs.
Grate three teaspoons of lime from the limes and put aside.
Mix the almonds, coconut, flour, 1 tsp of the grated lime, baking powder and salt in the medium bowl.
Beat the granulated sugar and then the vanilla essences into the egg yolks.
Fold the almond mixture in.

,
Wash egg beaters or whisk well and dry thoroughly.
Pour the mixture into the cake pan and smooth.

,
Place in the center of the oven and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a skewer comes out clean.

,
Add icing.
Remove the cake from the oven and leave to sit on a wire cooling rack for 10 minutes to cool.
Put the cake on its serving plate and add the icing.
