
Mix yeast and water.
Add flour.
Make dough ball.
Mix sauce ingredients.
Season the sauce.
Heat mixture until it boils.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Fill the deep baking dish halfway with water.
Add a layer of dough to the mason jars.
Place the jars in the deep baking dish.
Remove baking dish from oven.
Add another layer of dough.
Place deep baking dish and jars into the oven.
Remove from oven.
