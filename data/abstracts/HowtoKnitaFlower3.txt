
Knit an i-cord.
Decrease the cord size.
Work five more rows.
Bind off.
Cast 28 stitches.
Knit across the first round.
Slip, knit, and decrease across the second round.
Repeat these rounds.
Decrease across round 11.
Decrease across round 12.
Cut the yarn.
Pull the blossom and stem together.
Secure the tail.
