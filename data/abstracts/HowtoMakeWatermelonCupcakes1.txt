
Preheat the oven and line muffin pans.
Whisk together the dry cupcake ingredients.
Cream the butter and sugar.
Beat in 3 eggs and watermelon flavoring oil.
Add some of the flour and half of the sour cream.
Add the rest of the flour and sour cream.
Fill the muffin pans and bake the cupcakes.
Cream the butter and some of the powdered sugar.
Add the milk and more powdered sugar.
Stir in the lemon juice, watermelon flavoring oil, and powdered sugar.
Frost the watermelon cupcakes.
