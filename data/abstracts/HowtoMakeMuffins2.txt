
Preheat your oven to 350ºF (176ºC).

Crack six eggs into a bowl.
Pour 3/4 cups of low fat milk into the bowl.
Heat 2 tbsp.
Carefully pour the melted butter into the bowl.
Make the flour mix.
Pour the egg mix into the flour mix.
Add 1/4 cup of sunflower seeds to the mixture.
Place 10-12 muffin cups into a muffin tray.
Pour the batter into each cup until they're 2/3 full.
Place the tray in the oven and bake for 25 minutes.
Remove the muffins from the oven.
Serve.
