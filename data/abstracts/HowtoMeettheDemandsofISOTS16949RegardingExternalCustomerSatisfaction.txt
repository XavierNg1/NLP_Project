
Be aware that ISO/TS 16949 is a technical specification specific to the automotive industry.
Note that two elements of ISO/TS 16949 specifically address customer satisfaction.
Be aware that some of the “highlights” regarding problematic issues will be explained below :


ISO/TS 16949 is not limited to the requirements listed in the elements of the specification.
Understand that the very first step in meeting the customer demands will be “obtaining, evaluating and internalizing” the CSRs.
Be willing to think this through a lot.
Note that the second critical issue is customer scorecards.
Understand that the third critical issue is the communication with the customer.
Consequently, the organization is expected to go beyond complaint resolution.
The requirements of ISO/TS 16949 may look short in text, however they imply a lot more.
