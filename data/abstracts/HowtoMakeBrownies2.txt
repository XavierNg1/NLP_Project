
Preheat your oven to 325ºF (162ºC).
Prepare an 8 x 8 in.
Pour 1-2 in.
Combine the cocoa powder, sugar, butter, and salt in a bowl.
Let the bowl cool for 3-5 minutes.
Stir in the vanilla.
Add the eggs.
Add the flour.
Stir in the nuts.
Pour the mixture into a pan.
Bake for 20-25 minutes.
Remove from the oven and let cool.
Cut the brownies.
Serve.
