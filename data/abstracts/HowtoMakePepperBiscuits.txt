
Preheat the oven.
Blend the dry ingredients in a food processor.
Cut the butter into small cubes.
Add the butter gradually to the food processor and pulse.
Add the buttermilk.
Knead the dough.
Flour a counter or a large cutting board.
Make the dough into a rough rectangle on the floured surface.
Cut out the biscuits.
Form the leftover dough into biscuits.
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
Brush the biscuits with cream and top with pepper.
Bake the biscuits for 15-18 minutes.
Cool the biscuits for 15 minutes.
Serve the biscuits while still warm or at room temperature.
