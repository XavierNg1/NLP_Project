
Chill the cake.
Decide whether or not to divide the layers in half.
Slice away any domes.
Prepare the frosting.
Place a dollop of icing in the center of the plate.
Lay out strips of wax paper or parchment paper.
Center the first layer on the plate.
Spread frosting over the first layer.
Add any filling, if desired.
Place the second layer on top of the first.
Repeat as needed.
Apply a crumb coating.
Chill the cake again.
Spread the final coating of frosting onto the cake.
Smooth the frosting.
Add other decorations as desired.
Remove the wax paper strips.
Finished.
