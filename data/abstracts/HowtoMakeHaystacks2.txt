
Cover a large baking sheet with parchment paper or wax paper.
Melt the sugar and corn syrup in a large saucepan over low to medium heat.
Stir in the peanut butter and vanilla extract.
Take the saucepan off the stove, and set it down onto a heat-resistant surface.
Stir in the cornflakes cereal.
Scoop the mixture out onto the baking sheets using a spoon.
Put the tray into the fridge so that the haystacks can cool and harden.
Transfer the haystacks to a plate to serve them, and store any leftovers in an airtight container.
