
Preheat the oven to 375°F/190°C.

,
Get the muffin pan ready.
Combine the dry ingredients.
Whisk together the sugar, butter, milk and eggs.
Combine the wet and dry mixtures.
Fold the blueberries into the batter.
Fill the muffin cups and sprinkle each with turbinado sugar.
Put the pan in the center rack of the oven and bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
Check them at 20 minutes.
Take the pan out of the oven and put it on a cooling rack.
Polish them off now while they're freshly baked or freeze them individually in plastic wrap for breakfast on-the-go!
