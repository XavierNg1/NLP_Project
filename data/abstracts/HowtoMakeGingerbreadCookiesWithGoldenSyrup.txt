
Add the golden syrup and sugars to a heavy-based pan.
Sprinkle in the spices and baking soda.
Remove from the heat once the mixture begins bubbling.
In a large mixing bowl, add the butter.
Add the egg and mix through.
Take small amounts of the dough and roll each into a ball shape, about the size of a walnut.
Place the baking tray into the oven.
Ice when cooled.
Finished.
