
Preheat an oven to 350°F (180°C).
Put 1/2 cup flour, 1/4 cup sugar, the 1 tablespoon (14.8 ml) cocoa powder, 3/4 teaspoon baking powder, and 1/4 teaspoon salt in the medium mixing bowl.
Add 1/4 cup milk, 2 tablespoons (29.6 ml) oil, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
Put the 1/2 cup sugar and the 2 tablespoons (29.6 ml) cocoa powder in the small mixing bowl.
Pour evenly over batter in casserole.

Put the casserole in the oven.
