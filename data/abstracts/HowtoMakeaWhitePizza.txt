
Heat the olive oil.
Saute the onion until translucent.
Mix in the garlic and cook until fragrant.
Stir in the cream and cook the mixture until it thickens.
Remove the sauce from the heat and add the thyme.
Season the sauce with salt and pepper and allow it to cool.
Preheat a pizza stone in the oven.
Shape the dough into a circle.
Dust a peel with flour and place the dough on it.
Combine the ricotta, salt, thyme, and pepper.
Spread the white sauce over the dough.
Sprinkle the mozzarella over the sauce.
Add dollops of the ricotta mixture over the mozzarella.
Slide the pizza onto the heated stone.
Bake the pizza until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted.
Remove the pizza from the oven and garnish with parsley.
Allow the pizza to cool briefly before slicing.
