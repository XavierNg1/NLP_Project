
Research the need to incorporate.
Investigate legal structures available for businesses.
Meet with an attorney and/or a Certified Public Accountant.
Choose a business name.
Complete Form BCA-4.10, Application for Reservation of Name.
List up to 3 possible business names on the form.
Mail the form and payment.
Register with the IRS and get a tax ID number.
Appoint a registered agent of the business.
Complete Form BCA 2.10, Articles of Incorporation online and print it.
Compute the franchise tax.
Mail the form and/or filing fee.
