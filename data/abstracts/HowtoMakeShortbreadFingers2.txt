
Gather the ingredients.
Prepare the baking sheet or pan.Sift the flour, cocoa, cinnamon, salt and baking powder into a mixing bowl.
In another bowl, add the butter and sugar.
Add the dry ingredients to the butter and sugar mix.
Transfer the dough to the prepared baking sheet or pan.
Place the dough, covered, into the refrigerator to chill.
Remove the chilled dough from the fridge.
Place into the oven and bake.
Remove from the oven.Set aside on a wire cooling rack to finish cooling.
Serve.
