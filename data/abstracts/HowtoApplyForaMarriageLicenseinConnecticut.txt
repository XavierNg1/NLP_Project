
Confirm your eligibility to marry in Connecticut.
Obtain written consent from a conservator.
Obtain consent from a parent.
Finalize any prior divorces.
Ensure timely application of your marriage license.
Collect appropriate identification.
Gather information required for the application.
Bring identification and money to the Office of Vital Records in the town where the wedding will be performed.
Pick up your validated marriage license and perform ceremony within two months.
