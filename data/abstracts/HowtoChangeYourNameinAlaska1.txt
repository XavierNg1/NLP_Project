
Consider why you wish to change your name.
Fill out a Petition for Change of Name.
Fill out a Request to Waive Posting in Adult Change of Name Case if applicable.
Sign the forms in the presence of a notary public.
Fill out an Application for Legal Name Change.
Make a copy of the forms.
File the original forms in court.
Receive from the court clerk an Order for Hearing, Posting and Additional Service.
Attend your court hearing.
Read the court judgment carefully.
Begin using your new name.
Change your name with government agencies and private institutions.
