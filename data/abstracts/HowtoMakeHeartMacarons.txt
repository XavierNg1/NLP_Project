
Whisk the egg whites until frothy.
Mix in the sugar.
Stir in the food coloring.
Sift the almond flour and powdered sugar.
Fold the dry ingredients into the egg white mixture.
Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
Transfer the macaron batter to a piping bag.
Pipe the macarons into heart shapes on the cookie sheet.
Allow the macaron to dry an hour.
Preheat the oven.
Bake the macarons until they are firm.
Allow the macarons to cool.
Whip the butter until light and fluffy.
Add the powdered sugar gradually.
Beat the butter and sugar mixture for a couple of minutes.
Mix in the vanilla and cream.
Spread the buttercream on the bottoms of cooled macarons.
Press another macron onto the buttercream to form a sandwich.
