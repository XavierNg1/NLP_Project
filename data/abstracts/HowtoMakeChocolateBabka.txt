
Combine the milk, yeast, and pinch of sugar, and let the mixture sit.
Mix the egg, egg yolk, and sugar.
Stir in the yeast mixture.
Combine the salt and flour in a stand mixer.
Add the egg mixture.
Switch the stand mixer to a dough hook and add the butter.
Knead the dough on a floured surface.
Place the dough in a bowl and allow it to rise.
Chill the dough.
Preheat the oven and grease a pan.
Combine the chocolate, cocoa, butter, sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a heatproof bowl.
Heat the mixture in a double boiler.
Form a rectangle out of the chilled dough.
Spread the filling mixture over the dough.
Roll the dough into a log.
Cut the log in half and set the pieces side by side.
Place one piece of the dough over the other to form an “X.” With the log halves side by side, lift one piece and place it over the other.
Twist the two ends on each side twice.
Transfer the dough to the pan and allow it to rise.
Combine the brown sugar, remaining granulated sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
Stir in the remaining butter.
Mix in the flour and toss.
Combine the egg and cream in a separate bowl.
Spread the egg wash over the dough and sprinkle it with the streusel.
Bake the babka until it is golden brown.
Allow the babka to cool in the pan.
Turn the babka out onto a rack and let it cool completely.
