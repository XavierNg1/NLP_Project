
Team up when you show a home, especially if you are dealing with an agent or a client you do not know or have not met.
Do not show the home outside of a scheduled appointment.
Identify all visitors.
Protect your home security codes and keys.
Keep some pepper spray within reach at all times.
Trust your instincts.
Keep all valuables out of sight and stored securely.
Put away and conceal prescription medications, mail and anything else with identifying information.
