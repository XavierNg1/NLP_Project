

Recognize the hazards of farming in general.Understand the nature of specific hazards you may experience when farming, and use precautions to decrease the risk of injury when working around these hazards.
Prepare an emergency plan for potential hazards, ranging from evacuation and escape routes, to response to chemical spills, injuries, and other events.
Have an emergency communication plan in place.
Have a well-stocked emergency response kit, including first aid supplies, food, water, flashlights, a weather radio, and other items that may help you survive an emergency situation.
Remember Murphy's Law: Anything bad that can happen, will happen.
