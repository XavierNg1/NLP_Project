
Zip up your zipper.
Find a large container.
Add mild detergent to your water.
Submerge your hoodie.
Soak your hoodie.
Use your hands to agitate it.
Remove your hoodie from the soapy water.
Put your hoodie into a colander.
Rinse your hoodie.
Squeeze out the water.
Lay your hoodie out to dry.Keep in mind that handwashed items usually take longer to dry because they hold onto more water.
