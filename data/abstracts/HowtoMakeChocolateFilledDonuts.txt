
Cut the butter into pieces.
Let the milk and butter come to room temperature.
Attach a dough hook to your mixer.
Combine the milk and yeast.
Add the eggs, sugar, salt, and flour.
Add the butter.
Refrigerate the dough.
Add the milk, egg yolks, vanilla extract, and sugar to a small saucepan.
Add the cornstarch.
Stir in the chocolate chips.
Add the chocolate filling to a piping or pastry bag.
Prepare a baking sheet.
Flour a work surface.
Roll the dough.
Cut the dough into donuts.
Let the donuts rise.
Prepare the sugar coating.
Line a large baking sheet with paper towels.
Prepare your frying equipment.
Fry the donuts.
Toss the donuts in the sugar.
Allow the donuts to cool completely.
Remove the chocolate filling from the refrigerator.
Poke a hole in the side of the donut.
Fill each donut with chocolate.
