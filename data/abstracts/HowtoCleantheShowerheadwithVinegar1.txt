
Gather your supplies.
Remove the showerhead by twisting it counterclockwise.
Put the showerhead into a pot.
Fill the pot with enough white vinegar to cover the showerhead.
Let the showerhead soak in the vinegar for 30 minutes to overnight.
Take the showerhead out of the pot and rinse it.
Scrub away any residue using an old toothbrush.
Use a soft cloth to polish the showerhead.You can use a microfiber cloth or even a piece of flannel.
Put the showerhead back onto the wall pipe.
Turn the water on in your shower for a few moments.
