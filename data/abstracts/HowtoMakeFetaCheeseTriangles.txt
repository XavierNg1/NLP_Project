
Crumble or chop the feta cheese into smaller chunks.
Shred the mint leaves and add them to the bowl.

,
Use an immersion blender to blend the feta cheese and mint until there are no big chunks of feta cheese and no big mint leaves left.
Slice the filo pastry sheets in half.
Use a tablespoon to scoop up some of the feta-mint mixture and drop the mixture onto one of the filo pastry halves.

,
Fold the filo pastry.
Continue folding the pastries until you're done.

,
Cook the triangles.
Let the cooked feta cheese triangles cool on a plate.
Finished.
