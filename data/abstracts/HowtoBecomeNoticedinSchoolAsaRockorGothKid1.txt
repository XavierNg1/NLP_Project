
Dress in a lot of black.“Goth” and “rock” fashions have many similarities and a lot of overlap, but one of the main differences between them is the preference by goths to have an almost entirely black wardrobe.
Wear goth/rock band t-shirts.
Wear dark eyeliner and dark nail polish.Rock kids and goth kids both like to experiment with black eyeliner and dark nail polish, although they are much more common in goth fashion.
Go for a dramatic hairstyle and consider dyeing your hair.
Wear leather, studs, spikes, fishnets and other alternative accessories.
Consider getting tattoos, piercings and other body modifications.Goth and rock kids both favor tattoos and piercings.
Get a pair of black boots.
