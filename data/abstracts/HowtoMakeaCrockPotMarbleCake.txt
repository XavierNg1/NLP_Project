
Place the pre-made chocolate cake mix into one large mixing bowl.
Place the pre–made vanilla cake mix into one large mixing bowl.
Apply non–stick cooking spray in the crock pot, and pour the chocolate cake mix inside.

,
Add the vanilla cake mix into the crock pot.
Turn the crock pot on high for 4 hours, or on low for 6-8 hours.
Turn the crock pot off and let the cake sit for about 45 to 60 minutes, to allow the cake adequate time to cool and settle.

,
Take out the removable bowl containing the cake.
Frost and decorate your crock pot cake and enjoy.

,
Finished.
