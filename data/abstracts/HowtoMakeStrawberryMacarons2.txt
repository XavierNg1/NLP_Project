
Prepare the baking sheets (trays).
Place the icing (powdered) sugar into the flour sifter.
In a separate bowl, prepare the egg whites.
Add the almond flour to the meringue mixture.
Transfer the meringue and almond mixture to the baking sheet.
Preheat the oven 10 minutes before baking.
Place the rested macarons into the preheated oven.
Purée the strawberries.
Add the white chocolate to a small saucepan with a heavy base.
Pour the strawberry purée into the melted white chocolate mixture.
Spread the ganache on one half of a macaron.
Serve.
