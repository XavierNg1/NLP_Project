
Preheat the oven and prepare a pan.
Combine the melted butter and sugar.
Mix in the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Press the crust into the bottom of the pan.
Whisk the cream cheese and sugar together.
Add the flour, egg, and vanilla.
Fold in the coconut.
Spread the batter over the crust and bake the bars until the top is golden.
Cool the pan before slicing into bars.
