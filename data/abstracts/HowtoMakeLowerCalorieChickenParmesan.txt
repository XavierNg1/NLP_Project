
Preheat the oven.
Process the bread in a food processor.
Place the crumbs on a cookie sheet and bake until they are golden.
Raise the oven temperature and grease a baking sheet.
Slice the chicken breasts in half.
Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
Combine the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning.
Mix the butter and the olive oil and brush the chicken.
Dredge the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture.
Place the chicken on the baking sheet and mist with oil.
Bake the chicken until golden brown.
Heat the marinara sauce in a pan.
Place the cooked chicken in the sauce and top with mozzarella.
Cover the pan and heat until the cheese melts.
