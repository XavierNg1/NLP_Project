
Wash the rice and put it into a large microwave-safe bowl.
Add 2 cups of water to your rice.
Soak the rice for 20-30 minutes.
Add your oil, seasonings, and any other flavors.
Place the bowl in the microwave uncovered and begin cooking.
After that time has lapsed, cover the bowl.
Let the bowl stand for 10 minutes to steam.
Fluff and serve.
