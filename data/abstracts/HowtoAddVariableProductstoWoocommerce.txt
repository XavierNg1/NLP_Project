
Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
Fill in the title for your product as you would do in a normal blog post.
Scroll down the page, then by Product Data you have to select Variable Product.

,
Add product details.
Fill in product custom attributes.
Fill in your variables, and you separate them using the vertical bar.
Fill in product variations.
Click on the add variable button and it will add the same box again.
Add product image and publishing.
Select the relevant product category for your variable product you just created.

,
Hit the publish button and then have a look at your product on your site.
