
Preheat the oven to 350ºF/180ºC.
Peel the apples.
Put the brown sugar and cinnamon into a small bowl.
Place the white sugar and butter into another larger mixing bowl.
Add the eggs one by one.
Add the flour and baking powder.
Pour one half of the batter into the baking pan.
Pour the second half of the batter over the top of the apples and first batter layer.
Spread the second half of the cut apples across the top of the cake batter.
Finish with the rest of the brown sugar and cinnamon mixture, sprinkling it evenly across the top of the cake mix.
Place into the oven to bake.
Remove from the oven using oven mitts.
Serve.
