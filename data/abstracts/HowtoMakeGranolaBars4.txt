
Preheat your oven to 325°F (162°C).
Spread the almonds and cashews on a cookie sheet.
Toast them in the oven for 6-8 minutes.
Remove them from the oven.

,
Grease a 9 x 13-inch baking dish with butter.
Mix the oats, rice cereal, banana chips, and chocolate chips in a big bowl.
Add the cooled nuts to the mixture.
Heat the remaining ingredients in a small saucepan over medium-high heat.
Cook for an additional 3-5 minutes after the syrups come to a light boil.
Pour the syrup over the oats and nuts.
Pour the contents of the bowl into the baking dish.
Let the ingredients stand for 2-3 hours to thicken.

,
Cut them into squares or rectangles.

,
Serve.
