
Preheat the oven.
Combine the flour, chocolate chips, almonds, and baking powder.
Mix the coffee and cocoa powder.
Stir in the spices and salt.
Whisk in the egg, egg yolk, sugar, oil, and fruit zest.
Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
Break off a piece of the dough and roll into a rope.
Flatten the rope slightly.
Slice the rope into cookies.
Repeat the process with all of the dough.
Transfer the cookies to a lined baking sheet.
Bake the cookies until they are firm around the edges.
Allow the cookies to cool.
Melt the chocolate and butter in a double boiler.
Add the powdered sugar and boiling water.
Dip the tops of the cookies in the frosting and garnish.
Allow the frosting to set completely before serving.
