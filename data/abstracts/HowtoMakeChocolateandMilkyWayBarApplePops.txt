
Prepare a place to stick the apple chocolate pops while drying.
Cut the apples into slices.
Stick the slices onto lollipop sticks.
Melt the dark chocolate and Milky Way bars together.
Dip the apple slices in the melted chocolate and Milky Bar mixture.
Dip the coated apple slices gently in the sprinkles.
Push the stick ends of the apple pops into a foam or egg carton base.
Done.
